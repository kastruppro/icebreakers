# Daggerford

## Quick Reference
**Region:** Sword Coast
**Type:** Small Town
**Status:** Active

## Description
A small but vibrant town located approximately one hour from Waterdeep. Daggerford serves as a waypoint between larger settlements and maintains its own distinct character. The town is home to the Temple of Desna and is the origin point for the Daggers of the North adventuring guild. Currently, the town is celebrating the "Day of the Beast" festival, filling the streets with festivities and celebration.

## Notable Areas

### Temple of Desna
- Beautiful temple dedicated to the dream goddess
- Features constellation ceiling and calming atmosphere
- Where the party received the Dream Staff

### Southern Marsh
- Marshland south of town
- Contains ruins connected to Tharizdun cult
- One of four known Tharizdun ruins in the region

### Festival Grounds
- Currently hosting "Day of the Beast" celebration
- Streets filled with revelers and activities
- Festive atmosphere throughout town
