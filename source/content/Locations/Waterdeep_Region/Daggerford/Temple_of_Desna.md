# Temple of Desna

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Daggerford]]
**Type:** Temple (Desna)
**Status:** Active

## Description
A beautiful and serene temple dedicated to Desna, goddess of dreams, stars, travelers, and luck. The temple features a stunning constellation ceiling that creates the illusion of an endless night sky. Dim, atmospheric lighting fills the space, while a white cloudy substance drifts through the air, creating an immediate sense of calm and tranquility for all who enter. The priests wear distinctive purple robes and bear glowing moon symbols on their hands, marking their devotion to the dream goddess.
