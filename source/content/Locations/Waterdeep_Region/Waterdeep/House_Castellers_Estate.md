# House Castellers Estate

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]]
**Type:** Noble Mansion
**Status:** Active

## Description
An opulent noble mansion in Waterdeep's northern district, House Castellers serves as the residence of the Castellers noble family. The mansion is known for hosting elaborate social events, including the masquerade ball the party infiltrated. The house conceals dark secrets behind its refined exterior, serving as a location where Tharizdun cultists have been conducting their schemes.

## Notable Areas

### Ballroom
- Grand hall used for social events
- Features self-playing magical instruments
- Site of the masquerade ball infiltration

### Children's Room
- Location where Thistle was found floating in a magical trance
- Other children discovered in beds, potentially under similar enchantments
- Connected to Tharizdun's dream manipulation

### Second Floor Study
- Contains the Book of Vile Darkness
- Repository of dark knowledge and cultist materials
- Key discovery location during infiltration

### Second Floor Left Room
- Protected by animated armor trap
- Dangerous area for intruders
- Part of the mansion's defenses

### Hidden Room (Study)
- Concealed behind a painting in the study
- Access via pressure plate at floor level
- Small chamber with table/shrine and shelf
- **Items Found:**
  - Mysterious Shrine Chest (taken by Milo, Session 21)
  - Three glass containers ("music-playing glasses" per Ammalia, magical, not taken)
- Discovered Session 21 after Victor was claimed by Tharizdun
