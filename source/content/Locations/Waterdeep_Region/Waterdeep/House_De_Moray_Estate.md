# House De Moray Estate

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]] (North Ward)
**Type:** Noble Estate
**Status:** Active (Neglected)

## Description
A tall, imposing building in Waterdeep's prestigious North Ward noble district. The estate is decorated with blue banners bearing the family heraldry: two crossed swords, one aflame. Once an inviting and loving home, it has fallen into darkness under Godwin's decline.

## Notable Areas

### Exterior
- Tall building, more imposing than neighboring estates
- Blue banners with crossed swords heraldry (one sword aflame)
- Guard post at entrance

### Throne Room
- Currently pitch black, windows covered
- Only torch light
- Two thrones (one empty - mother's seat)
- Empty bottles scattered around Godwin's throne
- Foul smell of alcohol and unwashed body

### Barracks
- Guard quarters
- Party decided against staying here (too dangerous)

- Modred grew up here before leaving

- Declined after Arthur's possession and disappearance
- Morgane left for House of Justice
- Now dark and abandoned feeling
