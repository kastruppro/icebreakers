# Waterdeep Cemetery

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]]
**Type:** Cemetery
**Status:** Active

## Description
A guarded cemetery in Waterdeep where notable citizens are buried. The grounds are patrolled by guards on a fixed rotation, with a guard station/outhouse nearby. The cemetery contains the grave of [[Edward_Hamel|Edward Hamel]], a figure connected to the party's investigation.

## Notable Areas

### Edward Hamel's Grave
- Located in a corner of the cemetery
- Guard patrols pass this area on a 10-minute rotation
- The party plans to return and dig up the head for Speak with Dead

## NPCs Found Here
- Guards on patrol rotation

## Events at This Location
- **Session 27:** The party scouted the cemetery before leaving Waterdeep. [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]] used his Rogue expertise to map guard patrol patterns, identifying a corner with a 10-minute window between passes. [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] cast Detect Magic on [[Edward_Hamel|Edward Hamel's]] gravestone to check for hidden magic. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] purchased a spade (2 silver) for future grave-digging. The party decided the guard timing made extraction impractical and postponed the plan.

## Secrets/Hidden Areas
- Edward Hamel's body may hold answers accessible via Speak with Dead (only the head is needed)
- Unknown if magical protections exist on the grave itself
