# House Castellers Basement

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[House_Castellers|House Castellers]], [[Waterdeep]]
**Type:** Hidden Dungeon/Cult Headquarters
**Status:** Active - Ritual in progress

## Description
A hidden underground complex beneath House Castellers, accessed through a basement door in the garden. The basement contains three branching passages leading to distinct areas: a tomb room, a bedroom, and a massive ritual chamber. This location serves as a secret headquarters for Tharizdun cultists and is the site of an active ritual involving [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]].

## Notable Areas

### Tomb Room (Left Passage)
- Dead-end chamber containing stone coffins
- Coffins belong to previous generations of the Castellers noble family
- One figure depicted holding a mace
- No active threat discovered

### Bedroom (Middle Passage)
- Living quarters with beds, closets, and furniture
- Evidence that someone currently resides here
- Occupant unknown - possibly cultists or prisoners

### Ritual Chamber (Right Passage)
- Massive square room serving as the cult's primary ritual space
- Giant statue of [[Tharizdun]] depicted in chains dominates the room
- Pool/basin of water positioned before the statue
- Central summoning circle where [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] is currently chained
- 8-10 cloaked cultists observed standing in a circle, chanting "Tharizdun"
- Two individuals in earthy clothes with flowers in their hair - possibly druids - actively casting spells
- Active ritual in progress

## NPCs Found Here
- [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] - Chained prisoner in the central summoning circle
- Unknown Cultists (8-10) - Cloaked figures performing the ritual
- Two Unknown Druids - Nature-dressed individuals with flowers in hair, casting spells
