# Sevarna's Magic Shop

## Quick Reference
**Type:** Magic Shop
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]] (near Raenar's house)
**Status:** Active

## Description
A magic shop in Waterdeep owned by [[Savan]], located near [[Raenar_Neverember|Raenar Neverember]]'s residence.

## Proprietor
[[Savan]] - A human mage who dismisses non-magical technology in favor of pure magic. Respects competent magic users.

## Services
- Magical items
- Spell components
- Knowledge about rare materials (diamond dust sources)

## History with Party
- **Session 12**: Party met Savan at Statue Protectors Square where he debated with [[Gigz]] about magic vs technology
- **Session 14**: Party visited the shop, sold items to [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]]

## Connections
- [[Savan]] - Owner
- [[Raenar_Neverember|Raenar]] - Shop is near his house
- [[Gigz]] - Philosophical rival
