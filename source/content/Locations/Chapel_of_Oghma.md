# Chapel of Oghma

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Neverwinter]]
**Parent Location:** [[House of Knowledge]]
**Type:** Sacred Chapel
**Status:** Active

## Description
A chapel within the House of Knowledge dedicated to Oghma, god of knowledge. Features a prominent statue of Oghma. The space is sacred enough that Oghma can directly communicate with mortals from here.

## Notable Areas

### Statue of Oghma
- Large statue of the god of knowledge
- Shadow entity and Arthur hovered atop it during the battle
- When Milo confronted the statue in anger, Oghma actually answered
- Functions as a focal point for divine communication

## NPCs Found Here
- [[Oghma]] - Can be contacted through the statue (if approached with courage)
- [[Arthur De Moray]] - Shadow battle took place here
- [[Clover]] - First encountered the party here

## Items Found Here
- None recovered

## Events at This Location
- Session 10: Major shadow battle. Shadow entity controlling Arthur fled here. Party fought to contain the shadow with light magic. Clover joined the party here. Milo confronted Oghma's statue and was transported to the god's divine throne room.

## Secrets/Hidden Areas
- The statue may serve as a gateway to Oghma's divine realm for those bold enough to demand answers
- Light magic is particularly effective here against dark entities
