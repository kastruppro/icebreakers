# Phandalin

## Quick Reference
**Region:** Sword Coast
**Type:** Small Town
**Status:** Active (recovering from dragon attack)

## Description
A small frontier town south of Neverwinter, Phandalin serves as the starting location for the Adventures of Icespire Peak campaign. The modest settlement features simple wooden buildings, a central square, and the typical amenities of a frontier community. The town suffered a devastating dragon attack in Session 1, which left many residents frozen in ice and set the party on their initial adventures.

## Notable Areas

### Toblen's Tavern
- Main tavern and inn in town
- Owned by Toblen, the mayor

### Shrine of Luck
- Small shrine dedicated to Tymora
- Place of worship for travelers and locals
- Maintained by Sister Gale
