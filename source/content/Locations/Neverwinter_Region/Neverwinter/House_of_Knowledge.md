# House of Knowledge

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Neverwinter]]
**Type:** Temple/Library
**Status:** Active
**Deity:** [[Oghma]] (God of Knowledge)

## Description
The House of Knowledge is the temple of Oghma in Neverwinter, serving as both a place of worship and a repository of arcane and scholarly knowledge. It houses researchers, wizards, and archivists who serve Oghma's mission to preserve and spread knowledge.

## Notable Areas

### Main Library
- Extensive collection of books and scrolls
- Multiple researchers work here
- Elira Moonshadow serves as Archivist/Ink-Scribe

### Gearwhistle's Private Room
- Can be magically secured for private conversations
- Used for sensitive magical examinations
- Site of Arthur's curse removal attempt (Session 9)

### Chapel of Oghma
- Sacred chapel within the House of Knowledge
- Contains large statue of Oghma
- Arthur and the shadow entity fled here during combat (Session 10)
- Site where Milo met Oghma directly and received divine favor

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Professor_Thaddeus_Blackthorn|Professor Thaddeus Blackthorn]] - Dunamancy Wizard (suspicious behavior)
