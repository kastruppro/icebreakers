# Neverwinter Castle

## Quick Reference
**Type:** Castle/Seat of Power
**Region:** [[Neverwinter]]
**Status:** Active (in crisis)

## Description
The seat of power for [[House_Neverember]] and the ruling castle of Neverwinter. Contains the tombs of the Guardians of Nine and the [[Crown_of_the_Guardians]].

## Notable Features
- Tombs of the Guardians of Nine
- Location of the [[Crown_of_the_Guardians]]
- Unstable floors in some areas (one collapsed into a portal)

## History with Party
- **Session 3-4**: Party searched the castle for Lord Neverember. Battled ice skeletons. Lord Neverember fell through a collapsing floor into a mysterious portal. Discovered the tombs of the Guardians and a screaming ghost trapped in ice.
