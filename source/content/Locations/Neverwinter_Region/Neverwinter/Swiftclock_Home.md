# Swiftclock Home

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Neverwinter]]
**Type:** Family Residence / Clock Workshop
**Status:** Active

## Description
The family home of the Swiftclock family in Neverwinter. Contains both living quarters and Ren Swiftclock's prestigious clock workshop. Also contains a secret room with books about the cult of Tharizdun and void portal research.

## Notable Areas

### Main Living Area
- Where Serina and Ren were found (unfrozen)
- Family reunion took place here
- Party slept here overnight

### Secret Room
- Contains books about Tharizdun's cult
- Holds information about void portal mechanics
- Contains the Luxon fragment (or information about it)
- Generational research accumulated by the Swiftclock family

### Clock Workshop
- Ren's workplace
- Prestigious establishment in Neverwinter

### Nearby Park
- Clover slept here as a dog to avoid guards and be closer to nature
