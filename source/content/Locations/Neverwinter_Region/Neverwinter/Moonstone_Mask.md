# Moonstone Mask

## Quick Reference
**Type:** Floating Inn
**Region:** [[Neverwinter]]
**Status:** Active

## Description
A fantastical floating building that hovers above Neverwinter, illuminated by moonlight from behind. One of the most upscale establishments in the city. Masked waiters with necklaces serve guests, and the air is filled with overwhelming floral scents.

## Features
- Magically floats above the city
- Moonlight illumination creates ethereal atmosphere
- Quest board with city tasks posted outside
- Rooms available with optional "companionship" for extra gold
- Masked waiters serve guests

## Proprietor
[[Liset]] runs the Moonstone Mask. She greets guests with enthusiasm and an overpowering floral perfume. She speaks Celestial.

## History with Party
- **Session 3**: Party arrived here after entering frozen Neverwinter. Met [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]] here.
- **Session 5**: Modred and Auren infiltrated to gather intelligence on the election. Auren sang in Celestial, captivating the crowd. Liset supporters gathered here.

## Political Significance
During Lord Neverember's disappearance, the Moonstone Mask became a gathering place for Liset's supporters in the election against Sebastian.
