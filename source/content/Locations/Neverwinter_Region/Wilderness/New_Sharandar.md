# New Sharandar

## Quick Reference
**Region:** Northern [[Neverwinter Forest]]
**Type:** Eladrin Settlement
**Status:** Effectively destroyed (Mythal breached and failed, Temple of Leaves burned, many eladrin dead -- Session 29)

## Description
A hidden eladrin settlement woven into the canopy of Neverwinter Forest, reached by a 10-15 minute walk from the edge of the Mythal ward. Treehouses are built into the living canopy; huts grow around the trunks themselves rather than standing apart from them. The camp feels grown rather than constructed -- a living place shaped by the forest rather than imposed on it.

The settlement is protected by a **Mythal** -- a slow-moving wave of shimmering light that passes through the forest like a breathing ward. The Mythal alters how magic functions inside it and reacts violently to Tharizdun-corrupted substances. Crossing while possessed or carrying corrupted material causes dangerous reactions; [[Modred De Moray|Modred]] felt his certainty about magic and his father's lessons unravel on contact, and [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]]'s containment battery nearly burst when pushed through the veil. [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]], having been marked by the Mythal during his year at Old Sharandar, passed through without ill effect.

At the center of the camp stands the [[Temple of Leaves]] -- a massive golden-leafed tree forming a natural dome, the heart of the settlement and site of the monthly Lateu'quor ceremony. Outside the Mythal perimeter, a quarantine tent holds [[Arthur De Moray]], whose shadow possession went feral when he first crossed the ward.

## Notable Areas

### Inside the Mythal
- Treehouses and grown huts throughout the canopy
- The [[Temple of Leaves]] at the center -- open-roofed tree-temple around the great golden tree
- War maps and a command area where [[Merrisara Winterwhite]] works
- Gathering and drinking areas used by the eladrin community

### Outside the Mythal
- Quarantine tent holding [[Arthur De Moray]], isolated to prevent his possessed shadow from reacting with the ward
- The forest road approaching from Neverwinter (site of the cultist ambush, Session 28)

### Nearby
- The [[Feywild Portal Tree]], a short distance further into the forest -- the oak fey crossing [[Corellon Larethian]] pointed them toward; confirmed as the cult's route into the Material Plane; the party entered it at the end of Session 29

## Events at This Location
- Session 26 (mentioned): [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] sent telepathic messages to [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] revealing the Swiftclock family had fled their home because [[Cora]] was hunting them. They took shelter with the eladrin here. Liora's final message was prophetic: *"The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun. The chains will wither all you hold."*
- Session 28: The party arrived after ambushing eleven cultists at the Mythal perimeter with eladrin assistance. [[Pelendethas Everstride]] escorted them inside. They met [[Merrisara Winterwhite]], [[Emrae Firesky]], and [[Thradisar Starwise]]. [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] reunited with [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]]. The party attended the Lateu'quor ceremony at the [[Temple of Leaves]] and received [[Corellon Larethian]]'s warning about cult activity originating from the Feywild. The session ended with a wave of cultists storming the camp, the [[Temple of Leaves]] burning, and [[Cora]] fleeing with [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]].
- Session 29: New Sharandar fell. The [[Mythal]] visibly failed during the attack -- [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] watched the shimmering protective dome thin and fade out of the sky in real time as the defenders died. The camp became an inferno: burning trees fell across the chase route, cultists cut down screaming eladrin, and the eladrin defensive lines were collapsing. Many eladrin were killed. [[Emrae Firesky]] was last seen with a dagger to her throat inside the burning [[Temple of Leaves]], fate unknown. One eladrin shielded [[Modred De Moray|Modred]] from a black spell-bolt with his own body so Modred could keep running. [[Merrisara Winterwhite]] personally arrived at the [[Feywild Portal Tree]] with a soldier and a pair of bows to save the party as they fought the cultists guarding the portal. As the party fled into the Feywild through the portal tree, Merrisara shouted something unheard after them. The settlement was effectively destroyed; [[Arthur De Moray]]'s status in his quarantine tent outside the Mythal is unknown.

## Secrets/Hidden Areas
- Cult forces probing the perimeter are entering Neverwinter Forest from the Feywild itself, per [[Corellon Larethian]] -- the eladrin do not understand how
- The oak tree fey crossing (not yet visited) is the party's likely route into the Feywild to investigate
- Eladrin metalsmiths here may be able to help [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]] construct a stronger containment battery using Feywild crystals and materials
