# New Sharandar

## Quick Reference
**Region:** Northern [[Neverwinter Forest]]
**Type:** Eladrin Settlement
**Status:** Active (not yet visited by the party)

## Description
An eladrin settlement located in the northern part of Neverwinter Forest. New Sharandar is connected to the historical restoration of Old Sharandar, which was guided by Merrisara Winterwhite (from [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]]'s backstory).

The Swiftclock family has taken refuge here after fleeing from [[Cora]], who has been hunting them.

## NPCs Found Here
- [[Liora Swiftclock]] - Milo's sister, sheltering here with the family
- The Swiftclock family - Hiding from [[Cora]]
- Eladrin residents (unnamed)

## Events at This Location
- Session 26 (mentioned): [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] sent telepathic messages to [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] revealing the family had fled their home because [[Cora]] was trying to find them. They found shelter with the eladrin here. Liora's final message was prophetic: *"The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun. The chains will wither all you hold."*
