# Temple of Leaves

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[New Sharandar]], [[Neverwinter Forest]]
**Type:** Open-air tree-temple
**Status:** Destroyed (burned in cultist attack, Sessions 28-29)

## Description
The Temple of Leaves is the spiritual heart of [[New Sharandar]], built around -- and inseparable from -- a massive golden-leafed tree at the center of the eladrin camp. The tree itself forms a natural dome of shimmering gold-white canopy. The temple structure is not bricks or stone but living trees grown together into an open-roofed enclosure around the central tree, their trunks serving as walls and their branches meeting overhead to frame the sky.

At the base of the great tree stands a stone statue of [[Corellon Larethian]], bow in hand, with the tree's lower branches curling around and over it. The ground around the statue is bare and sacred. During the monthly Lateu'quor ceremony, offerings are laid in a ring around the statue by the assembled eladrin.

The temple is used for community gathering, worship, and commerce as well as ceremony -- [[Thradisar Starwise]] was found drinking here, and eladrin with goods to trade use the space.

Under moonlight during the Lateu'quor, the golden leaves glow white-gold and the tree acts as a conduit for [[Corellon Larethian]]'s presence. During Session 28, the party were lifted through the canopy and into a visionary sky-space where Corellon spoke to them directly.

## Notable Areas

### The Central Tree
The great golden-leafed tree itself. Its leaves glow during the Lateu'quor under moonlight. During Session 28, its leaves physically wrapped around the party and carried them upward into Corellon's vision-space. Site where the [[Moonlight Longbow (Corellon's Gift)]] was bestowed.

### The Statue of Corellon
Stone figure of [[Corellon Larethian]] with a bow, set at the base of the central tree with branches draped around it. The offerings ring is laid around the statue's base during ceremonies.

### The Offering Ring
During Lateu'quor, dozens of leaf-wrapped packages from eladrin and guests form a ring around the statue. The party placed offerings here in Session 28 -- the party's included forest berries, useful plants, and natural fibers wrapped in leaves, with calligraphy by [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] marking words like "family" and "friendship."

## Events at This Location
- Session 28: The party attended the Lateu'quor -- the monthly Forest Communion of the Crescent Moon. They laid offerings at the base of the Corellon statue, drank red elven wine, and were drawn up into a vision by the golden tree. [[Corellon Larethian]] spoke to them directly: he congratulated them on killing one of Tharizdun's Princes, warned that the cult besieging the forest appeared to be entering from the Feywild, and directed them toward the oak tree fey crossing further in the woods. The moon melted into the [[Moonlight Longbow (Corellon's Gift)]] and floated down to the party. Corellon's warning was cut short by *"Oh no, they're here."* The party was returned to the temple to find it burning, eladrin dead, and a wave of cultists closing in. [[Cora]] was seen fleeing the camp with [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]].
- Session 29: The party snapped back from Corellon's vision into the full fury of the attack. The central golden tree was visibly burning; offerings were scattered; eladrin lay dead around the statue of [[Corellon Larethian]]. [[Emrae Firesky]] was on the ground further down the temple with a dagger held to her throat -- her final fate unresolved. Four or five cultists were closing on the party as they began the chase after [[Cora]]. The party fought their way out of the burning temple, leaving it effectively destroyed behind them. During the flight, [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] watched the [[Mythal]] dome visibly fade and fail in the sky overhead, confirming the ward had been broken.
