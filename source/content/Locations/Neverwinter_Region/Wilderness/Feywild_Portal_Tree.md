# Feywild Portal Tree

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Neverwinter Forest]], a short distance deeper into the woods from [[New Sharandar]]
**Type:** Fey crossing (oak portal tree)
**Status:** Active -- currently being used by the Tharizdun cult as a route between planes

## Description
A massive oak standing in [[Neverwinter Forest]] with a gaping hole cut clean through the back of its trunk -- large enough to ride a horse through. The hole functions as a permanent fey crossing into the [[Feywild]].

Unlike most portals the party has encountered, this one does not visibly flicker, shimmer, or otherwise advertise itself as active. From the forest side it reads as a large hollow tree: cultists walk out of the hole as if stepping out of a doorway, and travellers can walk straight in without any visual cue that they are about to change planes. This makes it nearly impossible to identify as a portal on sight from the outside.

[[Clover]], as a druid, recognized it instantly as a Feywild crossing the moment it resolved out of the forest.

This is the oak [[Corellon Larethian]] pointed the party toward during the Lateu'quor vision in Session 28 -- the route he identified as the source of the cult's incursion into [[Neverwinter Forest]]. Session 29 confirmed his warning: cultists emerged from the hole mid-fight and formed up in front of the party, proving the tree is the Tharizdun cult's operational route from the [[Feywild]] into the Material Plane.

## Notable Features
- Horse-sized hole cut through the back of the trunk; functions as the portal aperture
- No visible activation effect -- the portal is simply passable
- Used continuously by cultists as a door rather than as a rare crossing
- Recognizable on sight to a druid but not to ordinary observers

## NPCs Found Here
- [[Cora]] - Fled through the portal with [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] at the start of Session 29; vanished into the [[Feywild]] beyond
- [[Merrisara Winterwhite]] - Arrived with a soldier carrying two bows to save the party during the portal fight; last seen fighting at the portal as the party crossed; shouted something unheard after them
- Tharizdun cultists - Stepped out of the portal during the Session 29 fight to form up against the party; marked with glowing forehead-eyes that fire [[Eldritch Blast]] as pure glares

## Events at This Location
- Session 29: The party chased [[Cora]] from the burning [[New Sharandar]] to this tree. [[Cora]] ran straight into the hole and vanished with [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]]. A fresh wave of cultists stepped out of the same hole to block the party. The party fought them at the portal's mouth -- [[Clover]] in Dire Wolf form, [[Modred De Moray|Modred]] splitting the "blue" cultist in two with an Action Surge, [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] being knocked to 0 HP twice before throwing a spiteful Fireball at the final two cultists. [[Merrisara Winterwhite]] personally held the line with them. At session end, the party sprinted into the hole and crossed into the [[Feywild]], ignoring whatever Merrisara shouted after them.

## Secrets/Hidden Areas
- Whether the portal can be closed, collapsed, or warded from the Material side is unknown
- Whether travel through the tree is one-way or two-way has not been tested -- the party has only seen it used from the Feywild side out (cultists) and from the Material side in (Cora, then the party)
- What, if anything, is on the immediate Feywild side of the tree is unknown to the party
