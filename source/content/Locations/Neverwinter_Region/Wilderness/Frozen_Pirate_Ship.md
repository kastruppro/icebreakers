# Frozen Pirate Ship

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Neverwinter]] Harbor
**Type:** Ship (frozen)
**Status:** Impaled by ice spike, suspended 70 feet from harbor

## Description
A pirate vessel caught mid-voyage by the supernatural freeze affecting the region. A massive ice spike pierces the ship from bottom to top, suspending it 70 feet above the harbor. Pirates remain frozen on deck and within the ice spike itself. The anchor hangs in the air, providing the only feasible access point.
