# Dream Realm

## Quick Reference
**Type:** Plane of Dreams
**Region:** Other Planes
**Status:** Active

## Description
A realm of dreams where consciousness can travel while the body sleeps. Connected to the goddess [[Desna]], who has power over dreams. The Dream Realm can be shaped by dreamers but is also vulnerable to dark influences like [[Tharizdun]].

## Campaign Significance
[[Thistle]] was trapped in a nightmare version of the Dream Realm by Tharizdun's influence. The party traveled into the Dream Realm to rescue her.

## Known Locations Within
- [[Dream_Realm_Castellers]] - Nightmare version of House Castellers where Thistle was held

## History with Party
- **Session 19**: Party sought [[Desna]]'s help to enter the Dream Realm
- **Session 20-21**: Party entered the Dream Realm to rescue Thistle from Tharizdun's nightmare
