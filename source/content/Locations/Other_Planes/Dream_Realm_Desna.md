# Dream Realm - Desna

## Quick Reference
**Region:** Other Planes
**Type:** Divine Dream Realm
**Status:** Active
**Accessed via:** [[Temple_of_Desna|Temple of Desna]] in [[Daggerford]]

## Description
A divine dream realm where the goddess [[Desna]] resides and receives visitors who seek her aid. The party traveled here through a ritual circle of sleep at the Temple of Desna in Daggerford.

Within this realm, Desna appeared as a constellation face with starry hair, able to read the dreams of mortals and grant divine gifts.

## Key Events

### Session 19 - The Dream Staff
- The party sought [[Desna]]'s help to save [[Thistle]] from [[Tharizdun]]'s spell
- Desna revealed that no mortal magic could break Tharizdun's direct curse
- She bestowed the [[Dream_Staff|Dream Staff]] upon [[Clover]] as the only means to enter Thistle's nightmare
- Desna read the dreams of several individuals:
  - [[Cora]] - Dreams full of destruction and hate
  - [[Pogo]] - Dreams of treats
  - [[Rafael]] - Nightmares about chains and a demon (Tharizdun)
