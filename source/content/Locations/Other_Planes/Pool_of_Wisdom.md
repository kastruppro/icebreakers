# Pool of Wisdom

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Feywild]]
**Type:** Fey site / emotional-regulation springs
**Status:** Active -- referenced but not yet visited by the party

## Also Known As
- **Hot Springs of Wisdom** (Doggo's phrasing)

## Description
A [[Feywild]] site, referenced in Session 30 by both [[Meadow Tree|Doggo]] and [[The Door]] as the **canonical fix for emotional overload**. In the Feywild, emotion is currency and fuel -- it powers travel, it reads on sight, and high/unchecked anxiety makes a traveler untravelable (you cannot move through the Feywild while your head is full of thundering feelings). The Pool of Wisdom is the Feywild's correction for that state.

The pool is **not at a fixed location**. It *"finds you when you're ready."* Both Doggo and The Door phrased this as a soft rule rather than a riddle. The Feywild opens the path when the traveler's emotional state is receptive -- not the other way around.

The party is aware of it. The party has not visited it. Their Session 30 workaround (bulk wine) suppressed emotion hard enough to force transit without the Pool's help, but the long-term fix is still open.
