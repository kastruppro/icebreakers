# Mr. Plant's Spiral Garden

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Feywild]]
**Type:** Spiral flower bed with central cottage
**Status:** Active -- [[Mr Plant|Mr. Plant]] in residence

## Description
A large **spiral flower bed** with a cottage at its center. The flowers are thin, pale, mounted on thin stalks -- each has a face. They cry when tickled, snore when sleeping, and **decompose corpses on command** by rolling over them. The big flower-entity **Mr. Plant** tends the bed; one of his head-flowers can whistle, gape open, and vomit up cadavers as a demonstration of displeasure with cultist visitors.

The red-robed cultists came through here. Mr. Plant reported they "stepped on his flowers." That made him angry. As proof, one of his head-flowers regurgitated a decomposing cultist onto the ground, where the spiral's little flowers rolled over it and buried it. *"It's the circle of life,"* [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] said.

The cottage at the center -- see [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden#The Cottage|below]] -- contains a gateway downward to [[Mr Plants Basement|the basement]] and [[The Door]].

## Notable Areas

### The Spiral Bed
- Thin pale flowers on thin stalks, each with a face
- Small flowers in groups that snore in unison, cry when tickled
- Decomposition service: the flowers roll over any corpse dropped here and bury/dissolve it into new blooms
- Do NOT step on the flowers. Modred's tickle of a flower drew an immediate scolding from Mr. Plant.

### The Cottage
- One large warm interior, dim-lit with hanging vines
- Several beds; three set aside for party guests
- Mr. Plant said additional beds belong to "my parents" -- identity and presence of these parents unresolved
- A cultist corpse hangs upside down in the corner, vine-wrapped, dripping decomposition-feed for the little flowers; after the party's long rest, flowers had bloomed out of the body
- **Windows run on inverted time**: from inside, the view is pitch night while outside is day. Modred verified this by walking in and out. The cottage runs on the opposite day/night cycle from the garden.
- Blankets and soft fabrics contain microscopic civilizations of tiny people walking around; touching the fabric reveals them
- Detect Magic reads enchantment on *everything* here (Milo got a headache)

### The Basement Stairs
- Lead down into the [[Mr Plants Basement|blue-lit bridge chamber]] and [[The Door]]
- Mr. Plant declared visiting the basement non-optional: *"not going into the basement is mean"*
- Climbing back up does not necessarily return you to the cottage -- see [[Mr Plants Basement|basement notes]]; the party emerged instead into the [[Queens Diamond Chamber|Queen of Darkness's Diamond Chamber]]

## NPCs Found Here
- [[Mr Plant|Mr. Plant]] - Bellsprout-bodied, humanoid face, petal-ears, impossibly thin limbs. Keeper of the garden. Gave a fake name. Killed cultists, fed them to his flowers, sheltered the party. Opened a giant sky-eye on [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] that follows him permanently.
- The Little Flowers - Small sub-entities with faces. Cry, snore, decompose, bury.
- Dead Cultist - Upside-down in the cottage corner, vine-wrapped; composted into fresh flowers by the end of the party's rest.

## Items Found Here
- **Mr. Plant's Eye (tracking)** - [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] - A giant disembodied eye opens in whatever sky is over Modred. It follows him, winks when waved at. Sight-confirmed; whether it also hears is unresolved. Imprinted after Modred revealed the Unseelie mark and tried to explain his choice.

## Events at This Location
- Session 30: Party followed the cultists' path from the [[Meadow Tree]] and emerged at the spiral. [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] tickled a flower (made it cry, got scolded). Mr. Plant demonstrated anger by vomiting a cultist corpse. [[Cora]] was revealed to be the *daughter of the Queen of the Unseelie* -- Mr. Plant's load-bearing revelation. A giant eye opened above Modred as Mr. Plant declared he was "having his eye on you." The party accepted a long rest in the cottage. Verified the windows run inverted to outside time. Modred saw civilizations in the bedding. Party slept (Modred's snoring synced with all the flowers). Woke to find Mr. Plant gone and the eye still overhead. Proceeded down to the [[Mr Plants Basement|basement]].

## Secrets/Hidden Areas
- **Mr. Plant's real name** - Unknown; "Mr. Plant" is a fake (confirmed by Modred's nat 20 Insight)
- **Mr. Plant's "parents"** - He mentioned beds were reserved for them. They were not seen. Older flower entities may be sleeping somewhere in the cottage.
- **The inverted-time mechanic** - Why the cottage runs on opposite time from the garden, and what this implies about its placement in the Feywild, is unexplored.
