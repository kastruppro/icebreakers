# Sphinx Guardian's Corridor

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Feywild]]
**Type:** Stone corridor / threshold
**Status:** Active -- gate of the Feywild Guardian on the Feywild side of the [[Feywild Portal Tree]]

## Description
A narrow stone corridor with torches and pillars on the **Feywild side** of the oak portal. Noticeably colder than the Material Plane. Unlike every other portal the party has used, the oak does not feel like a portal at all -- you simply step through the hole and are suddenly on the other side of the tree, already in the corridor.

Travelers are forced into a line that moves forward as if on a conveyor belt. The corridor opens into a chamber with a very high ceiling, where the **Sphinx Guardian** processes each entrant one at a time. [[Clover]] recognized it instantly from eladrin childhood scare-tales: this is a gate of a Feywild Guardian.

## Notable Areas

### The Corridor Proper
- Narrow, torch-lit, pillared stone
- Markedly colder than the Material Plane
- Travelers cannot meaningfully leave the line -- movement is forward-only

### The Guardian's Chamber
- Very high ceiling
- The Sphinx waits at the head of the line, neck able to stretch serpent-long
- Attackers are flicked out of existence by a flick of the paw mid-strike

## The Sphinx's Process
The Sphinx asks each traveler how they feel (reading emotions directly -- they are the Feywild's currency) and then asks: **"Do you like summer or winter the most?"**

- **Summer** = [[Seelie Court]] mark: a golden flower blooms on the body or armor
- **Winter** = [[Unseelie Court]] mark: a black rose burns itself into the body or armor

The mark cannot be hidden. Modred tried a cape toga; the rose simply bled through, outline and all. Every fae in the Feywild reads the mark on sight.

Attack attempts are terminal. The Sphinx swats attackers out of existence. A line of red-robed [[Tharizdun Cult|Tharizdun cultists]] entering ahead of the party all drew blades and were all flicked out in turn.

## NPCs Found Here
- The Sphinx Guardian - Female voice. Humanoid face, griffin wings, scorpion-like tail, feline claws, stretchable serpent-neck. Processes traffic, marks travelers, flick-kills attackers. Told [[Clover]] privately that his friends' emotions were *"delicious."*

## Events at This Location
- Session 30: Party entered the Feywild through the [[Feywild Portal Tree]] and arrived in this corridor, queued behind cultists. Cultists ahead attacked the Sphinx and were annihilated. Milo chose summer (Seelie mark). Modred chose winter ("Waterdeep summer stinks like piss") and accidentally received the Unseelie mark -- later revealed to specifically signify the Queen of the Winter Court's line. Clover, Feywild-born, chatted comfortably, chose summer, and was greeted as one of the Sphinx's own. The party passed through.
- Session 30 (briefly): Mid-drunken-travel, the party flickered back here for a moment. The Sphinx looked at them confused: *"How are you even here?"*

## Secrets/Hidden Areas
- The corridor's architecture (stone, torches, pillars) is anomalous in a plane otherwise dominated by wild natural settings -- whether this is a constructed threshold or an illusion is unknown.
- What lies to the Sphinx's side of the line (past travelers, failed ones) is not described.
