# The Depths

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Other Planes]] (accessed via [[House Castellers]])
**Type:** Extradimensional Dungeon/Plane
**Status:** Active - Party currently trapped inside

## Description
A pocket dimension or underworld accessed through the whirlpool pool beneath House Castellers. The space manifests as a massive crater-like void with an ocean far below and a violent whirlpool current that drags victims downward. The entire realm appears designed as a death trap, filled with the corpses of adventurers who came before. A malevolent sentient water entity controls this space, treating intruders as entertainment in a deadly game.

The atmosphere is oppressive and ominous. The Water Sphere Entity's mocking laughter echoes through the chambers as it manipulates water, blood, and the environment itself.

## Notable Areas

### Whirlpool Passage
- Entry point from House Castellers' ritual chamber
- Violent water current creates near-impossible resistance
- The force is strong enough to bend and snap stone (broke the Tharizdun statue's leg)
- Leads down through a dimensional boundary into the crater void

### Shore of the Dead
- Sandy beach at the base of the descent
- Littered with corpses of dead adventurers: humanoids, dwarves, gnomes
- Bodies in various states of decay
- Strong smell of rot confirms long-term accumulation
- A massive staircase leads up from the shore to the main chambers

### Water Sphere Chamber
- Central cave-like chamber at the top of the stairs
- Features a huge floating water sphere within a ring of three stone pillars
- Purple-black corruption liquid floats around the sphere
- More dead adventurers scattered throughout
- Three stairways lead to different rooms/challenges (right: Fountain Room, center: Illusion Room, left: Brazier Room)
- The Water Sphere Entity splits into three smaller spheres, each taking a different path
- **THREE-PILLAR MECHANISM:** Each stone pillar lights up when its corresponding room's challenge is completed and its water sphere returns
- **FINAL TRIGGER:** When all three pillars are lit, the three water spheres create a massive vortex of water spinning between the pillars, summoning a Water Elemental called [[Old Hydra]] ("Bullhydra"). [[Lord Neverember]] (possessed, with red/purple corruption goo) emerged from within the Water Elemental. Necromancy magic detected on him.
- **BOSS ARENA (Session 25):** The chamber served as the arena for the fight against Old Hydra and possessed Lord Neverember. The three stone pillars generated environmental hazards during combat -- first a thunderstorm (lightning damage in a circle, DC 17 Con save) and then blizzard zones (flat cold damage, no save). The blizzard nearly caused a TPK, knocking out Milo and Modred simultaneously while forcing Clover out of his Wild Shape.
- **POST-COMBAT:** After Modred destroyed Old Hydra with the [[Sunfire Mace]] and Jaspar landed the final blow on Neverember, the Tharizdun corruption (purple/red goo) left Neverember's body and swirled into the air, forming a triangular portal to [[The Void]]. Through the portal, a massive chain hangs from the air ending at the floor, with screams echoing from within.

### Fountain Room (Right Path - Completed Session 24)
- Oval-shaped room accessed via the right stairway
- Contains a massive fountain over 100 feet tall
- One water sphere floats atop the fountain, releasing water
- Three floor drains in the floor
- A door is visible 120 feet up in the ceiling/wall
- **TRAP:** Clogging the drains causes:
  - Entrance door to slam shut (click sound)
  - Water sphere turns red
  - Blood begins pouring instead of water
  - Tentacles emerge from the floor drains, grappling victims and pulling them underwater
- **RESOLUTION:** The blood/water rises to the 120-foot ceiling door. The door leads to a tube-like water slide passage that loops and spirals before ejecting occupants back into the lower area of the Water Sphere Chamber
- **Challenge:** Survive the rising blood and tentacles long enough to reach the exit door

### Illusion Room (Center/Straight Path - Completed Session 24)
- Accessed via the center stairway from the Water Sphere Chamber
- Initially appears as an empty room with an illusion of [[Lord Neverember]] floating in the air, head tilted up, seemingly suspended by magic
- Detect Magic reveals the Neverember figure as illusion magic
- Physical objects pass through the illusion (confirmed with a ball bearing)
- **PUZZLE:** After the illusion is exposed, the Water Sphere Entity explodes into many small water spheres (one real, the rest illusions). Door slams shut.
  - Hitting an illusion sphere deals 4 acid damage on contact
  - Detect Magic identifies the real sphere (the only one not radiating illusion magic)
  - The puzzle escalates each round: first one real sphere among fakes, then two real spheres among increasing numbers of copies
  - Destroying all real spheres ends the puzzle; the water sphere reforms and flies back to the central chamber
- **Challenge:** Identify and destroy the real sphere(s) while avoiding acid damage from illusions

### Brazier Room (Left/Last Path - Completed Session 24)
- Accessed via the left stairway from the Water Sphere Chamber
- Contains five braziers in the center of the room
- Rain falls constantly from the ceiling, dousing the brazier flames in a cycle
- When the braziers go out, the entrance door slams shut and the room plunges into darkness
- **PUZZLE:** Keep all five braziers lit simultaneously
  - Hidden brick buttons in the walls (three total) control two wide wooden planks on rail tracks in the ceiling - a sliding roof mechanism
  - Pressing the buttons slides the planks across the ceiling, shielding four of the five braziers from the rain
  - The fifth brazier must be manually covered (Modred used his cloak)
  - Once all five braziers stay lit, the rain stops, the water sphere forms from the remaining raindrops and flies away, and the door opens
- **Challenge:** Discover the hidden ceiling mechanism and activate all buttons to block the rain

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Water Sphere Entity]] - Sentient water being that controls this realm, splits into three forms, fills rooms with blood, and treats adventurers as prey in a deadly game. *"More adventurers has come to die. Well, let the games begin."* / *"I guess I underestimated you all."*
- [[Lord Neverember]] - Found possessed in The Depths after all three challenges were completed. Red/purple corruption goo emanated from him. An entity connected to [[Tharizdun]] spoke through him with a layered voice. Freed from possession in Session 25 after the party defeated him in combat. The corruption left his body and he regained consciousness disoriented, with fragmented memories.
- [[Old Hydra]] (Bullhydra) - Water Elemental summoned by possessed Neverember. Had an acid body cycling mechanic (alternating blue/green forms; green form dealt acid damage to melee attackers). Could generate environmental weather hazards through the stone pillars. Destroyed by Modred's [[Sunfire Mace]] in Session 25.
- Tentacle Creatures - Appendages emerging from the Fountain Room's floor drains, grappling and pulling victims underwater

## Items Found Here
- Dead adventurers' equipment (cloaks, clothes, supplies)
- Barrels and materials used by party to build a raft

## Events at This Location

### Session 23
- Party descended through whirlpool after Clover heard her parents' voices calling from below
- Tharizdun statue leg snapped from the force, dragging entire party down
- Clover (as giant octopus) caught Milo and Jaspar but ultimately everyone was pulled to the Shore
- Explored Water Sphere Chamber and received the Entity's challenge
- Entered Fountain Room and attempted to clog drains to float up to ceiling door
- Trap triggered: room filling with blood, entrance sealed
- Milo and Jaspar pulled underwater by unknown creatures
- Session ended with party in combat for survival

### Session 24
- **Fountain Room resolved:** Fought tentacles from the floor drains. Milo used Chronoshift to land a Booming Blade dagger strike; Modred used bonded weapon recall after rolling a 1. All tentacles retreated. Blood/water rose to the ceiling door; party was sucked through a water slide tube back to the Water Sphere Chamber. First pillar lit.
- **Illusion Room completed:** Lord Neverember illusion exposed via Detect Magic and ball bearing. Water sphere puzzle with illusion copies - Milo identified real spheres with Detect Magic while Jaspar and Modred destroyed them. Illusion spheres dealt acid damage on contact. Second pillar lit.
- **Brazier Room completed:** Five braziers doused by ceiling rain. Modred climbed walls naked with daggers, found hidden brick button. Jaspar found second button. Milo used Mage Hand for the third. Sliding wooden planks covered four braziers; Modred's cloak covered the fifth. Third pillar lit.
- **Final confrontation triggered:** All three pillars lit, water vortex formed, Water Elemental summoned. Possessed Lord Neverember emerged with red/purple corruption goo. Entity addressed Clover about his parents and the "prince of Tharizdun." Tempted Modred with Tharizdun's power. Modred showed the Crown of the Guardians - Neverember briefly broke free and said "Run!" Milo used Prestidigitation on Neverember. Entity screamed "ENOUGH!" - session ended before combat began (waiting for Clover's player).

### Session 25
- **Boss fight:** Possessed [[Lord Neverember]] commanded [[Old Hydra]] ("Bullhydra, I guess we will teach them a lesson") and combat began in the Water Sphere Chamber
- **Old Hydra mechanics:** Alternated between blue (normal) and acid green (melee attackers take acid damage) body forms. Stone pillars generated thunderstorm and blizzard environmental hazards
- **Near-TPK:** The blizzard dealt 24 flat cold damage to everyone in range -- Milo went down (despite Ring of Cold Resistance), Modred went down (exactly 24 HP remaining), and Clover lost his snake Wild Shape form. Only Jaspar remained standing
- **Modred's heroic kill:** Destroyed Old Hydra with the [[Sunfire Mace]], its blinding light evaporating the elemental, but the acid retort from the killing blow knocked him unconscious at the same moment
- **Neverember freed:** Jaspar landed the final blow on Neverember. The Tharizdun corruption (purple/red goo) crunched out of his chest, swirled into the air, and formed a triangular portal to [[The Void]]. Tharizdun's voice said *"Come to the Void."* Neverember collapsed, alive but unconscious
- **Parents revelation:** Before going down, the entity through Neverember told Clover *"Parents are still alive"* -- Clover called for a ceasefire but Jaspar struck the final blow anyway
- **Neverember woke:** Clover healed and then kicked him awake. He was disoriented, thought weeks had passed. Revealed hearing a vile voice talking about *"resurrecting the Four Princes"* / *"Champions of the Four Princes."* Recognized the chain visible through the Void portal: *"Is that a chain? It looks like this weird place that I went to."*
- **Session ended** with the party preparing to rest on the underground coast and interrogate Neverember further
