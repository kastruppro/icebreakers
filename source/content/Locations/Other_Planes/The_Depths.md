# The Depths

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Other Planes]] (accessed via [[House Castellers]])
**Type:** Extradimensional Dungeon/Plane
**Status:** Active - Party currently trapped inside

## Description
A pocket dimension or underworld accessed through the whirlpool pool beneath House Castellers. The space manifests as a massive crater-like void with an ocean far below and a violent whirlpool current that drags victims downward. The entire realm appears designed as a death trap, filled with the corpses of adventurers who came before. A malevolent sentient water entity controls this space, treating intruders as entertainment in a deadly game.

The atmosphere is oppressive and ominous. The Water Sphere Entity's mocking laughter echoes through the chambers as it manipulates water, blood, and the environment itself.

## Notable Areas

### Whirlpool Passage
- Entry point from House Castellers' ritual chamber
- Violent water current creates near-impossible resistance
- The force is strong enough to bend and snap stone (broke the Tharizdun statue's leg)
- Leads down through a dimensional boundary into the crater void

### Shore of the Dead
- Sandy beach at the base of the descent
- Littered with corpses of dead adventurers: humanoids, dwarves, gnomes
- Bodies in various states of decay
- Strong smell of rot confirms long-term accumulation
- A massive staircase leads up from the shore to the main chambers

### Water Sphere Chamber
- Central cave-like chamber at the top of the stairs
- Features a huge floating water sphere within a ring of three stone pillars
- Purple-black corruption liquid floats around the sphere
- More dead adventurers scattered throughout
- Three stairways lead to different rooms/challenges (right: Fountain Room, center: Illusion Room, left: Brazier Room)
- The Water Sphere Entity splits into three smaller spheres, each taking a different path
- **THREE-PILLAR MECHANISM:** Each stone pillar lights up when its corresponding room's challenge is completed and its water sphere returns
- **FINAL TRIGGER:** When all three pillars are lit, the three water spheres create a massive vortex of water spinning between the pillars, summoning a Water Elemental. [[Lord Neverember]] (possessed, with red/purple corruption goo) emerged from within the Water Elemental. Necromancy magic detected on him.
- This is where the boss fight will begin in Session 25

### Fountain Room (Right Path - Completed Session 24)
- Oval-shaped room accessed via the right stairway
- Contains a massive fountain over 100 feet tall
- One water sphere floats atop the fountain, releasing water
- Three floor drains in the floor
- A door is visible 120 feet up in the ceiling/wall
- **TRAP:** Clogging the drains causes:
  - Entrance door to slam shut (click sound)
  - Water sphere turns red
  - Blood begins pouring instead of water
  - Tentacles emerge from the floor drains, grappling victims and pulling them underwater
- **RESOLUTION:** The blood/water rises to the 120-foot ceiling door. The door leads to a tube-like water slide passage that loops and spirals before ejecting occupants back into the lower area of the Water Sphere Chamber
- **Challenge:** Survive the rising blood and tentacles long enough to reach the exit door

### Illusion Room (Center/Straight Path - Completed Session 24)
- Accessed via the center stairway from the Water Sphere Chamber
- Initially appears as an empty room with an illusion of [[Lord Neverember]] floating in the air, head tilted up, seemingly suspended by magic
- Detect Magic reveals the Neverember figure as illusion magic
- Physical objects pass through the illusion (confirmed with a ball bearing)
- **PUZZLE:** After the illusion is exposed, the Water Sphere Entity explodes into many small water spheres (one real, the rest illusions). Door slams shut.
  - Hitting an illusion sphere deals 4 acid damage on contact
  - Detect Magic identifies the real sphere (the only one not radiating illusion magic)
  - The puzzle escalates each round: first one real sphere among fakes, then two real spheres among increasing numbers of copies
  - Destroying all real spheres ends the puzzle; the water sphere reforms and flies back to the central chamber
- **Challenge:** Identify and destroy the real sphere(s) while avoiding acid damage from illusions

### Brazier Room (Left/Last Path - Completed Session 24)
- Accessed via the left stairway from the Water Sphere Chamber
- Contains five braziers in the center of the room
- Rain falls constantly from the ceiling, dousing the brazier flames in a cycle
- When the braziers go out, the entrance door slams shut and the room plunges into darkness
- **PUZZLE:** Keep all five braziers lit simultaneously
  - Hidden brick buttons in the walls (three total) control two wide wooden planks on rail tracks in the ceiling - a sliding roof mechanism
  - Pressing the buttons slides the planks across the ceiling, shielding four of the five braziers from the rain
  - The fifth brazier must be manually covered (Modred used his cloak)
  - Once all five braziers stay lit, the rain stops, the water sphere forms from the remaining raindrops and flies away, and the door opens
- **Challenge:** Discover the hidden ceiling mechanism and activate all buttons to block the rain

## NPCs Found Here

- [[Water Sphere Entity]] - Sentient water being that controls this realm, splits into three forms, fills rooms with blood, and treats adventurers as prey in a deadly game. *"More adventurers has come to die. Well, let the games begin."* / *"I guess I underestimated you all."*
- [[Lord Neverember]] - Found possessed in The Depths after all three challenges were completed. Red/purple corruption goo emanates from him. An entity connected to [[Tharizdun]] speaks through him with a layered voice. Briefly broke free when shown the [[Crown of the Guardians]] and said *"Run!"*
- Tentacle Creatures - Appendages emerging from the Fountain Room's floor drains, grappling and pulling victims underwater
