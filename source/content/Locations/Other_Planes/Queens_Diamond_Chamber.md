# Queen's Diamond Chamber

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Feywild]]
**Type:** Domain of the Queen of the Unseelie Court
**Status:** Active -- Queen in residence; the party has been here and refused her

## Description
The domain of the **Queen of Darkness** -- the ruler of the [[Unseelie Court|Unseelie Court / Winter Court]]. A small, dark chamber centered on a **massive floating black diamond**, suspended in midair, **bleeding black fog**. The Queen manifests as the diamond itself -- a woman's voice speaks from it -- and [[Clover]] recognized her from eladrin childhood scare-tales, which describe her exactly as this.

The black fog is her active medium. She condenses it into **shadow-figures of loved ones**, who speak with those people's voices and mimic their emotional hooks. She cannot fake specific private information (favorite dishes, childhood-spell tests), but she can produce convincing emotional presences on demand. She is also the mother of [[Cora]].

The chamber is **reached through anxiety**. Travelers whose emotions are ungoverned -- anchored by high anxiety -- arrive here instead of wherever they thought they were going. The party climbed the stairs out of [[Mr Plants Basement]] expecting [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden|the spiral garden]] and emerged here instead. The escape route is **emotional suppression**. Meditation nearly works but gets overwhelmed by the Queen's amplification of anxiety. The party's actual exit was **wine**: drunkenness flattens the emotional noise long enough for the Feywild's emotion-travel to move you. Feywild intoxication runs triple speed.

## Notable Areas

### The Central Diamond
- Massive, black, floating in midair
- Bleeds black fog continuously
- The Queen's voice issues from it
- Source of the shadow-figures

### The Fog
- Black, ambient, fills the chamber
- Condenses on command into humanoid shadow-doubles
- The doubles speak with the voices of the travelers' loved ones

## NPCs Found Here
- **The Queen of Darkness** / Queen of the Unseelie Court / Queen of the Winter Court - Female voice, manifesting as the floating black diamond. Ruler of the Unseelie Court. Mother of [[Cora]]. Offered to take the party to their "loved ones" with *"no cost"* -- refused by Clover on a nat-20 Insight reading malevolence. Can pull travelers here whenever their emotions are ungoverned; now knows the party exists and refused her.

### Shadow-Figures Manifested This Session
- **Shadow-[[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]]** - Baited [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]]: *"Brother, come to me. The place is burning."* Failed Modred's favorite-dish test (roasted pork with mushrooms). Did not dissipate; Queen laughed: *"Very clever of you."*
- **Shadow-[[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]]** - Baited [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]]: *"They have captured me. Please, you need to find me."* Glitched in the fog when Milo asked who she talked to outside their house. Queen: *"You are very smart for such a tiny creature."*
- **Shadow-Parents of [[Clover]]** - The hardest test of the session. **Passed** Clover's age-4 spell test word-for-word. They were **real** -- not illusions. They said, *"We are real, but these are not. We are alive. Leave this place and just -- try to find us."* Later confirmed alive at [[The Moonlit Pool]].

## Items Found Here
- None physical. The party lost no material resources here.
- **Emotional Exhaustion (condition)** - All three PCs. Normal Feywild travel remains unavailable until the [[Pool of Wisdom]] is visited.

## Events at This Location
- Session 30: Party climbed out of [[Mr Plants Basement]] and emerged here instead of [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden|the spiral garden]]. Met the Queen. Faced shadow-figures of their loved ones -- Modred's brother, Milo's sister, Clover's parents. The favorite-dish and house-question tests exposed Arthur-shadow and Liora-shadow as fakes. The age-4 spell test confirmed Clover's parents as REAL. Queen offered transport to "loved ones" at "no cost"; Clover refused (nat 20 Insight, read malevolence). Queen: *"Well, then you will never find peace."* Party tried to meditate out (4-7-8 breathing, two rounds of Wisdom saves); Queen amplified their anxiety and pulled them back each time. Modred remembered his wine. Party drank. Feywild intoxication kicked in triple-speed. The chamber crashed open and the party flickered through their whole prior Feywild route at speed (back to the [[Mr Plants Basement|basement]], briefly to the [[Sphinx Guardians Corridor|Sphinx]]), before landing outside the tree line of [[The Moonlit Pool]].

## Secrets/Hidden Areas
- **What the Queen wants from the party** - She has offered to transport them to their loved ones. The party refused. Her retaliation is unresolved.
- **Whether the chamber has walls at all** - The room is described as small and dark; whether it has physical edges or is pure domain-magic is unclear.
- **Other shadow-figures she could manifest** - Only three were summoned this session, one per PC. The capability is clearly broader.
- **How to refuse her without being trapped long-term** - The wine solution worked once. It is not documented as a repeatable exit.
