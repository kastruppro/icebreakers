# Feywild

## Quick Reference
**Type:** Parallel Plane
**Region:** Other Planes
**Status:** Active

## Description
The Feywild is a parallel plane to the Material Plane, a land of wild magic and untamed beauty. It is the home of fey creatures and exists in a state of perpetual twilight and wonder.

## Connection to Campaign
[[Thistle]] has a connection to the Feywild, with a version of herself existing there as [[Thistle_Feywild|Thistle (Feywild)]].

## Known Features
- Home of fey creatures
- Wild, untamed magic
- Time flows differently than the Material Plane
