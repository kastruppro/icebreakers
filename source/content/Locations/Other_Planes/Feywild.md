# Feywild

## Quick Reference
**Type:** Parallel Plane
**Region:** Other Planes
**Status:** Active -- party first entered in Session 29

## Description
The Feywild is a parallel plane to the Material Plane, a land of wild magic and untamed beauty. It is the home of fey creatures and exists in a state of perpetual twilight and wonder.

## Connection to Campaign
[[Thistle]] has a connection to the Feywild, with a version of herself existing there as [[Thistle_Feywild|Thistle (Feywild)]].

The Tharizdun cult has been using the Feywild as its staging ground to pour forces into the Material Plane -- [[Corellon Larethian]] confirmed this in Session 28, and cultists were seen stepping out of the [[Feywild Portal Tree]] during the Session 29 fight, confirming the route.

## Known Features
- Home of fey creatures
- Wild, untamed magic
- Time flows differently than the Material Plane
- Currently being used by the Tharizdun cult as a staging area to enter the Material Plane

## Guidance for the Party
- From [[Corellon Larethian]] (Session 28): The Feywild "might change you, it might scare you, it might make you different." Do not give your real name to a fey.
- Corellon's warning was cut off mid-sentence by the cult attack; the full advice was never delivered.
- [[Merrisara Winterwhite]] shouted something unheard at the party as they crossed in Session 29. The content of her warning is unknown.

## Events at This Location
- Session 29: The party -- Milo, Modred, and Clover -- entered the Feywild at session end via the [[Feywild Portal Tree]] oak, in pursuit of [[Cora]] and [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]]. First time the party has set foot on the plane. They crossed on adrenaline with incomplete warnings from both Corellon and Merrisara.
