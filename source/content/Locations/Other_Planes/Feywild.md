# Feywild

## Quick Reference
**Type:** Parallel Plane
**Region:** Other Planes
**Status:** Active -- party first entered in Session 29

## Description
The Feywild is a parallel plane to the Material Plane, a land of wild magic and untamed beauty. It is the home of fey creatures and exists in a state of perpetual twilight and wonder.

## Connection to Campaign
[[Thistle]] has a connection to the Feywild, with a version of herself existing there as [[Thistle_Feywild|Thistle (Feywild)]].

The Tharizdun cult has been using the Feywild as its staging ground to pour forces into the Material Plane -- [[Corellon Larethian]] confirmed this in Session 28, and cultists were seen stepping out of the [[Feywild Portal Tree]] during the Session 29 fight, confirming the route.

## Rulership (Session 31 update)
The Feywild is ruled by **two Queens** -- [[Queen Meb]] and [[Queen Tiandra]]. Per rumor they were formerly at war; they are now in an **alliance**, driven by the [[Tharizdun Cult]]'s sustained incursions and the impending threat of [[Tharizdun]]'s release. The Queens hold court together at the [[Queens Court Feywild|Queens' Court]].

The Queens' strategic plan, delivered to the party in Session 31: **seal the Feywild closed** against external access, so the realm cannot be reached by Tharizdun or his cult after release. They asked for [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] specifically to make this possible (reason not stated; chronurgy is the working hypothesis). They also raised the option of leaving the carrier of [[Arthurs Shadow|Arthur's Shadow]] (currently [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]]) inside the sealed realm permanently as a separate containment strategy.

**Strong inference, not yet confirmed in-fiction:**
- **Meb** is likely the [[Queen of Darkness]] the party met in S30 -- the Unseelie / Winter Queen, [[Cora]]'s mother.
- **Tiandra** is likely the Seelie / Summer Queen -- the highest authority of the court whose golden-flower mark Milo and Clover carry from the [[Sphinx Guardians Corridor|Sphinx Guardian]].
- If both hold, the party stands in front of the same Unseelie ruler whose offer they refused in S30, now co-signed by the Seelie Queen. To be confirmed next session.

## Known Feywild Rules (learned Session 30)
- **Fae literalism**: Every word said to a fae is taken at its strictest meaning. *"Lend me a hand"* means your hand. Asking for someone's name takes the actual name. Giving your name voluntarily is giving something of power away.
- **Emotion is fuel and currency**: Fae see emotion directly, can taste it, and travel runs on emotional state. High anxiety makes you untravelable; the [[Pool of Wisdom]] is the canonical fix.
- **Wine workaround**: Feywild intoxication runs triple speed and can flatten emotion enough to force transit. Unofficial, dumps you wherever your suppressed longing wants to go.
- **Court marks at the Sphinx**: Summer = [[Seelie Court]] (golden flower). Winter = [[Unseelie Court]] (black rose). Cannot be hidden; read on sight by all fae.
- **Detect Magic is useless here** -- reads enchantment on literally everything.
