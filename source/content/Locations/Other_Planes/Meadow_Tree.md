# Meadow Tree

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Feywild]]
**Type:** Sunlit meadow with central tree
**Status:** Active -- keeper in residence

## Description
A sunlit meadow where the party emerged after passing the [[Sphinx Guardians Corridor|Sphinx Guardian]]. Time runs visibly fast here: with every step the party took, the sun jumped across the sky, day to night to day again -- though they felt no passage of time themselves. [[Clover]] confirmed this is normal and that, contrary to [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]]'s worry, Feywild time does not actually run much faster than Material Plane time; the visible sun-moon cycling is local to the meadow.

At the meadow's center stands a large tree with a high hollow. A golden retriever -- **Doggo** -- is usually found at its base, barking up at a rabbit who stole his bone.

A road leads out of the meadow through a gap in the bushes toward [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden|Mr. Plant's Spiral Garden]]. A separate (never-fixed) path leads toward the [[Pool of Wisdom]], which Doggo recommends to any traveler arriving with their emotions in disorder.

## Notable Areas

### The Tree
- Large, central, has a high hollow
- A rabbit lives in the hollow (stole Doggo's bone; vanished saying *"What the fuck is happening?"* in a deep voice when [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] shooed it with Mage Hand)
- The bone can be retrieved from inside via reach or Mage Hand

### The Meadow Floor
- Sunlit, grassy, pleasant
- Time visibly cycles overhead with each step

### Exit Paths
- **To [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden|Mr. Plant's Spiral Garden]]** - Through a gap in the bushes; the red-cloaked cultists took this path
- **To the [[Pool of Wisdom]]** - Not a fixed location; the pool "finds you when you're ready"

## NPCs Found Here
- **Doggo** - Male long-haired golden retriever, speaks. Meadow-keeper. Friendly, direct, and literal. Teaches Feywild 101, warns travelers about emotional travel, points them to the [[Pool of Wisdom]]. Took [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]]'s hand as a Feywild joke about *"lend me a hand"* and spat it back covered in flowers on the wound side.
- The Rabbit - Spooked off by Milo's Feywild Mage Hand; current whereabouts unknown

## Items Found Here
- **Mage Hand (Feywild)** - [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] - Gift from Doggo after Modred lost and regained his hand. Once-per-short-rest while in the Feywild. Modred can physically detach his hand as the somatic component.

## Events at This Location
- Session 30: Party emerged from the [[Sphinx Guardians Corridor|Sphinx Guardian's Corridor]] into the meadow. Witnessed time cycling overhead. Met Doggo and the tree-rabbit. Modred offered to "lend a hand" and literally lost it (returned, flowered, reattached). [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] cast Mage Hand for the first time in 29 sessions of play (table cheer) to retrieve Doggo's bone from the high hollow. Doggo warned the party their emotions were "everywhere" and would be used against them, pointing them toward the [[Pool of Wisdom]]. Modred received Mage Hand as a Feywild cantrip gift. Party departed along the bush-gap path the cultists had taken.

## Secrets/Hidden Areas

- Whether the tree hollow has further contents beyond the bone is unknown.
- Doggo's actual nature (fey? archfey? guide-spirit?) is not disclosed.
