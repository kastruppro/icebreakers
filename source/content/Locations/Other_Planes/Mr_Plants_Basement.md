# Mr. Plant's Basement

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Feywild]]
**Parent:** [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden|Mr. Plant's Spiral Garden]]
**Type:** Bridge chamber / Feywild transit hub
**Status:** Active -- [[The Door]] in residence, currently locked

## Description
Accessed via stairs from [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden#The Cottage|Mr. Plant's cottage]]. A cool, **blue-lit chamber** opening onto a crashing peaceful ocean 40-50 feet below, with clear blue water and fish jumping. A bridge spans the chamber. Further bridges lead off to the left and right -- **broken**, as The Door later described them.

At the center, in a frame set against apparent open ocean, stands a single closed wooden door: [[The Door]]. Mr. Plant's sky-eye is visible overhead through the cloud cover; [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] waved at it and it winked back.

The basement is functionally a **Feywild transit hub**. The Door can transport travelers to any named person or place in the Feywild -- when it is not locked. It is currently locked; a fairy stole its key.

- Sentient wooden door in a frame
- Set against what appears to be open ocean
- Handle turns into a human hand when grasped presumptuously

## NPCs Found Here
- [[The Door]] - Sentient fae door. Masculine voice, highly offended by discourtesy, fancies **whale oil or (preferably) duck oil** as tribute. Can teleport the party to any named person or place in the Feywild. Currently locked -- **a fairy stole the key**. Picked up the false name "Lyra Swiftclaw" from Modred without complaint. Cheerfully told the party, *"Oh, I'm looking forward to you coming back and opening me."*

## Items Found Here
- None acquired. The Door itself is the loot -- a traversal asset, blocked by a missing key.

## Events at This Location
- Session 30: Party descended from the [[Mr Plants Spiral Garden|spiral garden cottage]] after their long rest. Clover reached for The Door's handle and was slapped by it. The Door negotiated like a prissy nobleman, explained emotion-guided Feywild travel, disclosed the locked-key problem, and named duck oil as a preferred tribute. Modred tried visualizing the [[Pool of Wisdom]] to emotion-travel and was bounced back by his own anxiety-cloud (which formed into a screaming thundercloud). The party turned back upstairs -- and emerged NOT in the spiral garden but in the [[Queens Diamond Chamber|Queen of Darkness's Diamond Chamber]]. Feywild transit is emotion-guided: unchecked anxiety anchored them to the Queen's domain instead of home. During the wine-fueled drunken exit, the party flickered briefly back through this basement before flickering out again.
