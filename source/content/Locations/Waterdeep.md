# Waterdeep

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Sword Coast]]
**Type:** Major City
**Status:** Active

## Description
The Crown of the North, Waterdeep is a massive metropolis approximately ten times larger than Neverwinter. The city is known for its enormous crowds, diverse population, and the constant bustle of commerce and intrigue. The sheer scale of the city can be overwhelming to newcomers, with its towering buildings, winding streets, and countless districts.

## Notable Areas

### Field of Triumph
- Large arena used for gladiatorial combat and public spectacles
- Hosts various tournaments and events
- Popular entertainment destination

### Statue Square
- Central plaza featuring two large protective statues
- Significant landmark and meeting point
- The statues are said to protect the city

### Southern District
- Commercial hub filled with shops and merchants
- Bustling marketplaces
- Accessible to common folk

### Northern District
- Noble quarter where the wealthy reside
- Home to major noble houses
- More refined and less crowded than southern areas
