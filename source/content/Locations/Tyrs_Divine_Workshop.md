# Tyr's Divine Workshop

## Quick Reference
**Region:** Divine Realm (Tyr's Domain)
**Type:** Divine Plane/Realm
**Status:** Active

## Description
A divine workshop existing within Tyr's realm, accessible only through divine summons. The space is filled with tools, craftsmanship equipment, and weapons befitting the god of justice. The atmosphere radiates purpose and righteous intent. A smaller, human-sized statue of Tyr stands within the workshop, through which the god communicates.

## Notable Areas

### The Workshop
- Filled with divine tools and craftsmanship equipment
- Various weapons on display
- Human-sized statue of Tyr serves as the god's avatar for communication

### Notable Features
- A black dog with golden eyes (Tyr's herald) leads visitors here
- The space feels timeless and sacred
- Brief visions of other locations (such as cornfields) may appear during transitions

## NPCs Found Here
- [[Tyr]] - God of Justice, speaks through the statue
- Black Dog - Divine herald with golden eyes, guides Modred to and from the workshop

## Events at This Location
- **Session 13:** Modred first visited this divine realm during a vision from Tyr
- **Session 22:** Modred was summoned here via the black dog apparition in the Temple of Tyr. Tyr warned him that Tharizdun is "moving fast in the shadows" and urged swift action. Tyr confirmed the mission to the temple ruins south of Daggerford remains urgent - the Gauntlets of Tyr must be used to break Tharizdun's chains. Tyr also sensed "great power coming from somewhere" within Waterdeep's walls.

## Important Revelations (Session 22)
- **"Things are moving fast for the Betrayer God. Things are happening. Things are being put in process. You need to act swiftly soon. Otherwise Tharizdun will destroy the whole world."** - Tyr
- **"I also feel something is happening within the walls of Waterdeep. Great power is coming from somewhere."** - Tyr
- Tyr gave Modred freedom to choose priorities between investigating Waterdeep and clearing the temple ruins

## Access
- Only accessible through divine summons from Tyr
- Modred has been summoned here multiple times
- Entry typically occurs when approaching Tyr's statue in the Temple of Tyr
- The black dog with golden eyes serves as the herald/guide
