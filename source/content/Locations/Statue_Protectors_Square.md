# Statue Protectors Square

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]] (City Center)
**Type:** Public Square/Landmark
**Status:** Active

## Description
A major hub of activity in central Waterdeep, dominated by two massive protective statues. These ancient guardians would animate to defend the city in times of war. The square serves as the traditional starting point for the Pilgrim's Road.

## Notable Features

### The Guardian Statues

**Forest-Pointing Statue (Elven Woman)**
- Drawn bow, flowers in hair
- Points toward the forest
- Speaks a riddle: "Through the forest you must go, eat the berries will make you grow, see yourself as you are, only then have you come so far. Make your wish upon the altar, only then the fish might enter."

**Sea-Pointing Statue (Human Warrior)**
- Armed with sword
- Points toward the sea
- Speaks a riddle: "Seek my brother true, leap of faith will take you through, water will become ground, only then will you know what you have found, treasure you shall find, be careful not to go blind."

### The Pilgrim's Road
- Traditional journey beginning at this square
- Multiple purposes:
  - Treasure hunting expeditions
  - Mystical/religious experiences
  - Coming-of-age trials for youth
  - Adult rite of passage
- Many pilgrims return claiming they found nothing
- Modred dismissive: "Still pilgrims leaving from Waterdeep for nothing at all"
