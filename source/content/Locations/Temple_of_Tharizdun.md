# Temple of Tharizdun

## Quick Reference
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]] - Forest outside city
**Type:** Dungeon / Underground Temple
**Status:** Cleared - Temple completed in Session 16

## Description
An ancient underground temple dedicated to Tharizdun, the Chained One, an imprisoned evil god. The temple is accessed via a blood-activated elevator platform hidden among cult ruins in the forest outside Waterdeep. The complex features a massive statue of the chained god with chains extending throughout the structure, connecting to various puzzle mechanisms.

## Notable Areas

### Surface Level - Blood Altar Platform
- Elevated platform among scattered cultist corpses
- Ancient inscription requiring blood sacrifice to activate
- Circular pattern on floor is actually an elevator platform
- Descends when activated with blood

### Main Chamber
- Massive underground room
- **Giant statue of Tharizdun** dominates the space
- Chains extend from the statue in multiple directions:
  - Two chains to left and right walls
  - One chain behind the statue
  - Chains connect to ceiling and disappear into walls
- No obvious mechanisms visible - chains provide the puzzles

### Well Chamber (Right Side) - Session 15
- Accessed by following chain from main chamber
- Chain runs along ceiling then descends into deep well (several hundred feet)
- Three pullable chain levers on walls
- Three hooks positioned opposite each chain
- Chain at bottom disappears into hole in wall
- **Puzzle Mechanic:** Must secure all three chains while fighting regenerating oozes
- Guarded by regenerating ooze creatures and an Oblex boss

### Lever Room (Level 2) - Session 16
- Chain enters through hole in wall
- Two levers on party's side, one lever on elevated platform across gap
- One hook visible in middle of room
- **Puzzle Mechanic:** Sequential operation (not simultaneous) - raise, move horizontally, release
- Clover used spider form to reach isolated platform with third lever

### Horned Statue Chamber - Session 16
- Chain on ground, horned statue blocking passage
- Hands emerged from statue's mouth, speaking riddles
- **Three Riddles:** Shiny things (light), Red (blood), Warmth (fire)
- Jaspar provided light ball and blood; Modred provided torch
- Statue turned to dust after accepting all three offerings

### Gauntlets Chamber (Final Room) - Session 16
- Tyr's Gauntlets floating mid-air
- The Substance (corruption/ooze matter) swirling around gauntlets
- Altar with hand symbol
- Inscription: "By loss, Tharizdun shall grant what you wish for."
- Clover (as tiny spider, thrown by Jaspar) grabbed gauntlets through barrier
- Gauntlets shrank to fit Clover; Substance dissolved
