## Resolution
The party escaped The Depths through two methods in Session 26:
- **Jaspar, Clover, and Lord Neverember** were transported out by the divine entity (Jaspar's patron) to the Castellers mansion basement
- **Milo and Modred** escaped the Void by drawing a triangular portal with a dagger, specifying their destination

The full party reunited at the Castellers basement with [[Ammalia_Castellers|Ammalia]] and [[Raenar_Neverember|Raenar]].
