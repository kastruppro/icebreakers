# Escape The Depths

## Quick Reference
**Status:** ACTIVE (Boss Fight Imminent)

## Objective
Escape the pocket dimension known as The Depths and return to the surface.

## Background
While investigating House Castellers' basement, the party discovered a whirlpool connected to the Tharizdun ritual pool. Clover heard his parents' voices calling from within, prompting the party to descend. When the rope anchor (tied to the Tharizdun statue) broke, all four party members were pulled into The Depths.

## Current Situation

### Location: Water Sphere Chamber (Final Area)
All three challenge rooms are complete. The three water spheres formed a massive vortex between the stone pillars, summoning a **Water Elemental**. From within the elemental, **Lord Neverember** emerged -- but he is possessed by a Tharizdun entity. Red/purple corruption goo emanates from him. Necromancy magic detected by Milo's Detect Magic.

### Immediate Threat
- **Possessed Lord Neverember** + **Water Elemental** in the Water Sphere Chamber
- The entity controlling Neverember has declared "ENOUGH!" and called for initiative
- Boss fight begins next session (paused so Clover's player can be present)
- The entity offered the party a choice: walk away, join Tharizdun, or fight

## The Depths - Known Areas

### 1. Shore of the Dead
- Sandy beach where the party landed after falling
- Littered with dead bodies - humanoids, dwarves, gnomes, adventurers in various states of decay
- Massive staircase leads up from here

### 2. Water Sphere Chamber
- Cave-like space above the shore
- Floating sentient water sphere surrounded by corruption liquid
- Three stone pillars in a ring
- The sphere spoke: **"More adventurers has come to die. Well, let the games begin."**
- The sphere divided into three smaller spheres, each going down different staircases
- Dead adventurers scattered here too
- Three paths leading to different rooms

### 3. Fountain Room (COMPLETED - Session 23-24)
- Path to the right from the Water Sphere Chamber
- Tall fountain with water sphere on top, three floor drains
- **Challenge:** Survive rising blood and tentacles grappling from the drains
- **Resolution:** Party fought off tentacles (Booming Blade, Magic Missile, greatsword, Firebolts). Blood rose to the ceiling door at 120 feet. Party escaped through a water-slide tube that ejected them back to the Water Sphere Chamber
- First stone pillar lit upon completion

### 4. Illusion Room (COMPLETED - Session 24, Center/Straight Path)
- Empty room with an illusion of Lord Neverember floating in the air
- Milo's Detect Magic immediately identified it as illusion magic; Modred confirmed with a ball bearing throw
- **Challenge:** Water Sphere Entity divided into many copies (one real, rest illusions). Hitting illusions dealt 4 acid damage. Puzzle escalated from one real sphere to two real spheres among increasing fakes
- **Resolution:** Milo used active Detect Magic to identify real spheres; Jaspar and Modred destroyed them with ranged attacks. Magic Missile broke the illusion's concentration
- Second stone pillar lit upon completion

### 5. Brazier Room (COMPLETED - Session 24, Last/Left Path)
- Five braziers that kept relighting but were constantly doused by rain from the ceiling
- Door sealed and room went dark when braziers went out
- **Challenge:** Keep all five braziers lit simultaneously despite the rain
- **Resolution:** Party discovered hidden brick buttons in the walls that controlled two sliding wooden planks on ceiling rail tracks. Three buttons activated the planks, covering four of the five braziers from rain. Modred covered the fifth with his cloak. Modred stripped naked to climb the slippery walls using daggers as handholds
- Third and final stone pillar lit upon completion

## Session 24 - Three Rooms Completed

### Fountain Room Resolution
The tentacles were from the floor drains. Party fought them off across four rounds of combat. Highlights: Milo used Chronoshift to re-roll a failed acrobatic jump and landed Booming Blade on a tentacle for 14 total damage. Modred's bonded weapon feature saved his greatsword after rolling a 1. Jaspar's firebolts were halved underwater. The blood rose to the ceiling door; the party was sucked through a water-slide tube back to the Water Sphere Chamber.

### Illusion Room
An illusion of Lord Neverember floating in the air was the fake-out. Milo's Detect Magic identified it instantly. The Water Sphere Entity then divided into real and illusory copies, escalating from one real sphere to two. Key: only the real spheres lacked illusion auras under Detect Magic. Hitting illusions dealt 4 acid damage.

### Brazier Room
Five braziers needed to stay lit despite constant rain from the ceiling. The real solution was hidden brick buttons in the walls controlling two sliding wooden planks on ceiling rail tracks. Modred climbed the walls completely naked using daggers as handholds (his splint armor was too heavy). Three buttons covered four braziers; Modred's cloak covered the fifth.

### Final Confrontation Triggered
All three stone pillars lit. Water spheres formed a massive vortex. A Water Elemental was summoned. Lord Neverember emerged from within it -- possessed, with red/purple corruption goo. The entity spoke through him, taunted the party, and offered them a chance to join Tharizdun ("the Maw"). Modred refused. Neverember briefly broke free when shown the Crown of the Guardians and said "Run!" before the entity regained control. Session ended just before initiative.
