## Session 24 - LORD NEVEREMBER FOUND

### Discovery
After completing all three challenge rooms in The Depths (Fountain Room, Illusion Room, Brazier Room), the three water spheres formed a massive vortex in the Water Sphere Chamber and summoned a **Water Elemental**. Lord Neverember walked out from within the Water Elemental -- but he was wrong. Red/purple corruption goo emanated from him. His eyes were unnatural. Something was controlling him.

### The Possession
- Milo's active **Detect Magic** detected **necromancy** magic on Lord Neverember
- Modred identified a **second layer in his voice** -- something else speaking through him
- The entity controlling Neverember spoke to the party, addressing Clover specifically about his parents
- The entity referred to Tharizdun as **"the Maw"** and tried to recruit the party to join him
- It specifically targeted Modred's corruption: *"I think you like it. I think you want the powers that he can give. This massive power."*

### The Crown of the Guardians
Modred showed Lord Neverember the Crown of the Guardians (the heirloom given to him by the Seven Ghosts):

> "Lord Neverember, if you can hear me, try to fight this demon that is possessing you. This is the crown of your ancestors."

For a brief moment, **Neverember broke free**. His eyes became normal. He reached toward the crown and said in his own voice: **"Run!"**

Then the entity regained control, laughing: **"He's mine. It's now with Tharizdun."**

### Key Revelations
- Lord Neverember is ALIVE and conscious inside the possessed body
- The Crown of the Guardians can briefly break through the possession
- Necromancy magic is the mechanism of possession
- The entity is connected to Tharizdun and calls him "the Maw"
- This is no longer a simple rescue -- it requires combat and potentially an exorcism
- The Sunfire Mace (extra damage vs fiends/undead) may be effective

### Current Status
Boss fight against possessed Neverember + Water Elemental begins next session. Fight was paused because Clover's player was absent and the entity directly addressed Clover about his parents.
