# Investigate Edward Hamel

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (NEW LEAD)
**Priority:** MEDIUM (Not Time-Sensitive, but Valuable)
**Quest Giver:** [[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina Nightgale]]
**Started:** Session 26
**Last Updated:** Session 26

## Objective
Investigate [[Edward_Hamel|Edward Hamel's]] grave and/or house in Waterdeep to recover information and items that could help the party fight Tharizdun's Princes.

## Background
[[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina Nightgale]] revealed in Session 26 that her adventuring party killed **3 of the 4 Princes of Tharizdun** before failing on the fourth. **Edward Hamel** was the last surviving member of her party. He is now dead and buried in a Waterdeep cemetery.

Sabrina suggested that Edward:
- May have been buried with useful items
- May have left belongings at his house in Waterdeep
- Could be questioned using **Speak with Dead** if the party finds a caster

## What Edward Hamel Could Provide
- Knowledge about the **Princes of Tharizdun** -- which three were killed and how
- Information about the **fourth Prince** they failed to defeat
- Locations, weaknesses, or tactics for fighting the Princes
- Potentially useful magic items or research notes from his adventuring career
- Intelligence about Sabrina's party's methods for finding and entering Prince dungeons

## How to Investigate

### Option 1: Speak with Dead
- The **necromancer at the Waterdeep cemetery** was mentioned as a potential caster
- Speak with Dead allows 5 questions to the corpse
- The party needs to locate Edward's specific grave
- Questions should focus on: the fourth Prince, Prince dungeon locations, items left behind, Tharizdun's weaknesses

### Option 2: Edward's House in Waterdeep
- Edward may have a house in Waterdeep containing research, journals, or equipment
- Location currently unknown -- would need to be found through city records or cemetery records

### Option 3: Grave Contents
- Edward may have been buried with items from his adventuring days
- Grave robbing would have social/legal consequences in Waterdeep
