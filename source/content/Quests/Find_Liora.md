# Find Liora

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (RE-OPENED -- Liora re-captured, now in the Feywild)

**Quest Giver:** [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo Swiftclock]] (Personal Quest)
**Started:** Session 01
**Last Updated:** Session 29

## Objective
Recover [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] from [[Cora]]. She is currently being dragged through the [[Feywild]] after being re-captured during the assault on [[New_Sharandar|New Sharandar]].

## Background
Liora Swiftclock, Milo's sister, possessed prophetic visions that troubled her deeply. She disappeared mysteriously early in the campaign, leaving behind only a cryptic note. Milo joined The Icebreakers in part to find her. The search led the party to discover she had been involved with [[Cora]] and the cult, and was frozen on the mountain near [[Axeholm]]. She was freed and returned safely to Neverwinter.

In Session 28, Cora seized Liora again during the cult assault on New Sharandar. In Session 29, the party pursued Cora through the burning camp -- Clover's [[Plant Growth]] failed to slow her when she flash-teleported clear of it -- and Cora vanished through the oak portal tree into the Feywild with Liora under her arm. The party followed through the portal, ending the session in pursuit somewhere in the Feywild.

## Key Information
- Liora has prophetic visions and has been in telepathic contact with Milo
- She delivered a prophecy early in the campaign: *"A time will come when you have to choose between family and power. If you're not ready, it will be the end of the world."*
- Her Session 26 prophecy -- *"The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun. The chains will wither all you hold"* -- came true: New Sharandar and the Temple of Leaves burned.
- Cora has a personal short-range flash-teleport that can clear druidic difficult terrain (Session 29 -- cleared a 100-foot Plant Growth in a single move).
- The oak portal tree in Neverwinter Wood is a confirmed Fey Crossing used by the cult; it does not visibly flicker when active.
- Cultists preferred to capture rather than kill Milo during the chase (flat-swording him unconscious), consistent with the Cora/Liora kidnapping pattern -- the cult wants Swiftclocks alive.

## Session 29 - Into the Feywild
The party chased Cora through the burning ruins of New Sharandar. Milo was downed twice and had to burn a Fireball on near-dead cultists. Clover went Dire Wolf and took heavy damage. Modred killed the "blue" cultist with an Action Surge. Merrisara Winterwhite arrived personally to hold the portal position, was shouting something at the party as they crossed -- but they did not stop to hear it and went through the oak portal tree after Cora and Liora. The session ended with the party in the Feywild, with no idea where Cora has taken Liora.
