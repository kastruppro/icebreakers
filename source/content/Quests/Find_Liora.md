# Find Liora

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Completed (Found)
**Priority:** Low (Resolved)
**Quest Giver:** [[Milo Swiftclock]] (Personal Quest)
**Started:** Session 01

## Objective
Find Milo's missing sister Liora.

## Background
Liora Swiftclock, Milo's sister, possessed prophetic visions that troubled her deeply. She disappeared mysteriously, leaving behind only a cryptic note. Milo joined the The Icebreakers in part to find her. The search led the party to discover she had been involved with Cora and the cult, and was frozen on the mountain near Axeholm.

## Progress
- [x] Followed clues to Axeholm
- [x] Found Liora frozen on mountain with Cora
- [x] Freed Liora from the ice
- [x] Liora returned safely to the Swiftclock home in Neverwinter

## Key Information
- Liora has prophetic visions
- She was involved in stealing the hammer with Cora
- She was frozen on the mountain, preserved in ice
- Upon being freed, she delivered a crucial prophecy to Milo

### Liora's Prophecy
> "A time will come when you have to choose between family and power. If you're not ready, it will be the end of the world."
