# Find Liora

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active ([[Cora]] claims to know Liora's location -- offered as chip in a deal at the Queens' court, not yet delivered)
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Quest Giver:** [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo Swiftclock]] (Personal Quest)

**Last Updated:** Session 31

## Objective
Recover [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] from [[Cora]]. She is currently being dragged through the [[Feywild]] after being re-captured during the assault on [[New_Sharandar|New Sharandar]].

## Background
Liora Swiftclock, Milo's sister, possessed prophetic visions that troubled her deeply. She disappeared mysteriously early in the campaign, leaving behind only a cryptic note. Milo joined The Icebreakers in part to find her. The search led the party to discover she had been involved with [[Cora]] and the cult, and was frozen on the mountain near [[Axelholm]]. She was freed and returned safely to Neverwinter.

In Session 28, Cora seized Liora again during the cult assault on New Sharandar. In Session 29, the party pursued Cora through the burning camp -- Clover's [[Plant Growth]] failed to slow her when she flash-teleported clear of it -- and Cora vanished through the oak portal tree into the Feywild with Liora under her arm. The party followed through the portal, ending the session in pursuit somewhere in the Feywild.

## Key Information
- Liora has prophetic visions and has been in telepathic contact with Milo
- She delivered a prophecy early in the campaign: *"A time will come when you have to choose between family and power. If you're not ready, it will be the end of the world."*
- Her Session 26 prophecy -- *"The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun. The chains will wither all you hold"* -- came true: New Sharandar and the Temple of Leaves burned.
- Cora has a personal short-range flash-teleport that can clear druidic difficult terrain (Session 29 -- cleared a 100-foot Plant Growth in a single move).
- The oak portal tree in Neverwinter Wood is a confirmed Fey Crossing used by the cult; it does not visibly flicker when active.
- Cultists preferred to capture rather than kill Milo during the chase (flat-swording him unconscious), consistent with the Cora/Liora kidnapping pattern -- the cult wants Swiftclocks alive.

## Session 29 - Into the Feywild
The party chased Cora through the burning ruins of New Sharandar. Milo was downed twice and had to burn a Fireball on near-dead cultists. Clover went Dire Wolf and took heavy damage. Modred killed the "blue" cultist with an Action Surge. Merrisara Winterwhite arrived personally to hold the portal position, was shouting something at the party as they crossed -- but they did not stop to hear it and went through the oak portal tree after Cora and Liora. The session ended with the party in the Feywild, with no idea where Cora has taken Liora.

## Session 30 - No Visual Contact
The party traveled deep into the [[Feywild]] this session -- through the Sphinx Guardian's corridor, the Meadow Tree, [[Mr. Plant]]'s Spiral Garden, the Door's basement, the [[Queen of Darkness|Queen of Darkness's]] diamond chamber, and finally stumbled out (via wine-fueled emotion travel) at [[The Moonlit Pool]] in the Feywild. Liora was **not** in the camp when the party arrived. [[Cora]] was there at the dry pool with [[House Castellers]] cultists and with Clover's parents, but Liora was not visible in the scene.

The Queen of Darkness manifested a shadow-puppet of Liora inside her diamond chamber, reaching for Milo and pleading *"They have captured me. Please, you need to find me."* Milo tested the shadow by asking *"Who did you talk with outside our house? Back in the place where we came from?"* The Liora-shadow glitched mid-question and could not answer. The Queen laughed: *"You are very smart for such a tiny creature."* The shadow was an illusion -- not real contact with Liora.

Liora's most likely current location is **below the dry Moonlit Pool** -- Cora directed Clover's parents to jump into the pool to "protect Okramok," and they vanished without hitting bottom. Liora may have been moved down there ahead of them, either by Cora when she first arrived or by other cultists off-screen. She could also be at another Feywild location the party has not yet seen.

### New Pressure
- Cora knows she was followed through the portal (her own words, read off her lips by Clover) and has already moved Okramok and Clover's parents "below." Any Liora-directed asset would have been moved along with them.
- The [[Queen of Darkness]] now knows the party refused her deal and explicitly threatened them: *"You will never find your family, even though they are alive and in my care."* Liora may be considered one of those held "in her care."
- Cora is confirmed as the **daughter of the Queen of the Unseelie** -- the crown princess of the Winter Court. This recontextualizes every Liora kidnapping: Cora was never a lone cultist; she is the Queen's agent and has the full weight of the Winter Court backing her.

## Session 31 - Cora Names Liora's Location as the Deal Chip
The Session 30 ambush at the [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]] failed at the threshold. [[Cora]] sensed the party's volatile emotions across the tree line before they could engage and pivoted to negotiation rather than combat. Her pitch, per the DM summary: she had valuable information to give them, and **she knew where [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] was**. Liora's location was named explicitly as her chip in the deal in exchange for the party going with her. The party went with Cora to the Queens' court rather than press the kill.

**The information has not yet been delivered.** This is a new lead, contingent on the party continuing to negotiate with Cora. The terms of the exchange, what Cora is charging for the location, and whether she is telling the truth are all open. No description of Liora's whereabouts has been given beyond Cora's claim that she knows.
