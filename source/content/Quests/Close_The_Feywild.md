# Close the Feywild

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (formal offer from the Queens of the Feywild; decision pending; [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] specifically requested)
**Priority:** HIGH
**Quest Giver:** [[Queen Meb]] and [[Queen Tiandra]] (the Queens of the Feywild)
**First Mentioned:** Session 31
**Last Updated:** Session 31

**Note:** This is a new tracking file as of Session 31.

## Objective
Decide whether to help the Queens of the Feywild seal their realm against external access as a containment strategy against post-release [[Tharizdun]]. If accepted, [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] is the operative piece; the mechanism, cost, and reversibility are unknown.

## Background
After the failed ambush on [[Cora]] at the [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]] in Session 31, [[Cora]] transported the party (Milo, Modred, Clover -- Jaspar not present) to the audience chamber of the Queens of the Feywild: **[[Queen Meb|Meb]]** and **[[Queen Tiandra|Tiandra]]**. The two Queens are introduced as having been at war in the past and now allied; the cause of the alliance, per their own framing, is the [[Tharizdun Cult|Cult]].

The Queens laid out a situation and an ask.

## The Queens' Brief

1. **The Cult is operating inside the Feywild at sustained scale.** Per the Queens' framing, cultist incursion is no longer occasional -- it is sustained, and it is causing real damage to the realm. The party has already seen the surface of this in S30 (cultist line at the Sphinx Guardian, cultist compost at Mr. Plant's, cultist tents and cages of captured fae at the Moonlit Pool under a [[House Castellers]] banner).
2. **The Queens want to close the Feywild off** -- to seal the realm from outside access so the Feywild can be safe.
3. **A sealed Feywild would be unreachable by [[Tharizdun]] and his cult after release.** The Queens are treating Tharizdun's release as a foreseeable event and framing closure as a containment strategy.

## Milo Specifically Required
The Queens specified that they need **[[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]]** to close the Feywild. The DM summary does not record the reason or the mechanism. They did not ask the party as a whole; they asked Milo by name.

(Working inference, **not** in the DM summary: Milo is a Chronurgy wizard -- the school whose entire premise is timeline and dimensional manipulation. A working hypothesis for why the Queens want Milo specifically is that closing the Feywild is a chronurgy / dimensional-anchor operation. This is not confirmed and should not be treated as such.)

## What is Not Known
- **Mechanism.** What does "closing the Feywild" actually require, mechanically? Unknown.
- **Cost.** What does Milo specifically have to do, give up, or carry? Unknown.
- **Timeline.** When does this need to happen, and how long does the sealing operation take? Unknown.
- **What gets sealed in vs. out.** Are the party's allies in the material plane cut off? Are the existing cultist incursions in the Feywild trapped inside with the Queens? Unknown.
- **Reversibility.** Can the Feywild be reopened later? Unknown.
- **Who decides.** Is the seal a one-time choice, or a process the party can revisit? Unknown.
- **Coupling to the Shadow Option.** The Queens raised a separate option of sealing the Shadow's carrier inside the closed Feywild (see [[Contain_Arthurs_Shadow]]). Whether the two are required together, alternatives, or independent is unclear.

- Does sealing the Feywild also seal the path back home for the party?
