# Tyr's Gauntlets Quest

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (FIRST CHAIN DESTROYED - Three Remain)
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Quest Giver:** [[Tyr]] (God of Justice)
**Started:** Session 13
**Last Updated:** Session 26

## Objective
Use Tyr's Gauntlets to destroy Tharizdun's four chains, pushing him further into imprisonment.

## Background
Tyr, the god of justice, appeared to the party with a divine mission. His sacred gauntlets had been stolen by the cult of Tharizdun and placed within one of their temple ruins. These gauntlets are the only weapons capable of destroying the chains that bind Tharizdun in his prison. Four ruins exist around Waterdeep, each containing a chain. Destroying all four will push Tharizdun further into imprisonment and weaken the cult's efforts to free him.

## Session 22 - Tyr's Urgent Warning

Tyr appeared to Modred via a black dog at the Temple of Tyr. The dog led Modred to Tyr's divine workshop where the god delivered a critical warning:

> "Things are moving fast for the Betrayer God. Things are happening. Things are being put in process. You need to act swiftly soon. Otherwise Tharizdun will destroy the whole world."

Tyr specifically reminded Modred about the temple ruins south of Daggerford:

> "As I've requested before, I want you and your friends to go to the ruins of Tharizdun's temple. Find the chains, use the weapon I gave you to throw Tharizdun back into the light."

### Additional Divine Intel
- Tyr confirmed "great power" is emanating from somewhere within Waterdeep
- The situation is escalating - Tharizdun is "moving fast in the shadows"
- When Modred asked what to prioritize (ruins vs. Waterdeep), Tyr gave freedom: "Go wherever you feel is best to go. Something might happen here in Waterdeep as well."

### Current Party Decision
The party chose to deal with the Lord Neverember rescue first (Session 22-23), then travel to the temple ruins south of Daggerford. This mission cannot be delayed much longer.

## Session 25 - Chain Location Discovered

### The Void Portal
When [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] was freed from Tharizdun's possession, the corruption substance formed a **triangular portal to the Void**. Through the portal, the party can see:
- A **massive chain** hanging from the air, ending at the floor
- Screams echoing from within

**DM post-session clarification:** This space is one of the locations containing a chain of Tharizdun that must be struck with the Gauntlets of Tyr. The DM will no longer refer to these locations as "Ruins" going forward.

### Neverember Recognizes the Void
[[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]], after being freed from possession, stared at the portal and said: **"Is that a chain? It looks like this weird place that I went to."** He was about to reveal more when the session ended. He may have crucial information about the chain and the Void from his time possessed.

### Dual Objective Opportunity
Entering the Void could serve two purposes simultaneously:
1. **Strike Tharizdun's chain** with the Gauntlets of Tyr
2. **Rescue Clover's parents**, who the entity confirmed are alive in the Void

### Current Obstacle
The party is exhausted (2 points of exhaustion, nearly all spell slots expended, multiple party members went down during the boss fight). They must rest before attempting to enter the Void. The portal's permanence is unknown.

## Session 26 - FIRST CHAIN DESTROYED

### Striking the Chain
[[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] and [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] entered the Void through the triangular portal in the Water Sphere Chamber. They walked approximately 30 feet to a heavy chain anchored in the ground. Modred said **"Tyr, give me the power"** and grabbed the chain with the Gauntlets of Tyr.

**The Effect:**
- Both gauntlets vibrated and emanated blue light
- Blue fire hooked onto the chain and engulfed it
- The fire traveled up the chain into the sky, revealing **Tharizdun's massive bound form** above
- Tharizdun is bound by **four chains** going to different locations in the Void
- The blue fire spread around Tharizdun's lower body, engulfing his feet and lower half in blue flame
- Tharizdun screamed in pain in an alien language

### The Elemental Eye
As a consequence of destroying the first chain, a **red eye called the Elemental Eye** opened above Tharizdun's head. It scanned downward and fixated on the party's position. [[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina]] called this "the first phase of getting rid of Tharizdun" -- each chain destroyed has escalating consequences.

### Consequences
- **Modred's corruption spread** -- his entire hand is now black (previously just fingers)
- **Milo gained corruption** -- blackened fur on both cheeks, +5 permanent HP, cannot receive temp HP for 12 hours
- **Modred gained Corrupted Second Wind** -- rolls normal Second Wind HP recovery plus gains that amount as temp HP (effectively double healing), but requires a d100 roll each use
- The portal closed after the chain was struck, temporarily stranding Milo and Modred in the Void

### Intelligence from Sabrina
- **Sabrina's party killed 3 of the 4 Princes** but failed on the fourth
- Prince dungeons **change locations over time** -- cult activity nearby signals their current position
- Sabrina will provide the **names of the remaining Princes** (DM to share outside session)
