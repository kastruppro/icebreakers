# Rescue Lord Neverember

## Quick Reference

**Completed:** Session 25

## Objective
Rescue Lord Neverember from possession by a Tharizdun entity in The Depths.

## Background
Lord Neverember, the Open Lord of Waterdeep, went missing under mysterious circumstances. The Daggers of the North tasked the party with finding him. Investigation led to House Castellers, where Lord Neverember was ultimately discovered in The Depths beneath the estate, possessed by a Tharizdun entity.

## Resolution - Session 25

The party fought [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] (possessed) and the Water Elemental known as **Old Hydra** in the Water Sphere Chamber of [[The_Depths|The Depths]].

### The Boss Fight
- Old Hydra alternated between normal blue and acid green forms; melee attacks during the green phase dealt acid damage back to attackers
- Stone pillars generated environmental hazards: thunderstorm (lightning) and blizzard (cold) circles
- The blizzard nearly caused a TPK -- [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] and [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] both went down, [[Clover]] lost his snake Wild Shape form
- [[Modred|Modred]] went down **twice** (2 death save failures) and was revived by [[Clover]] and [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]]
- Modred destroyed Old Hydra with the [[Sunfire_Mace|Sunfire Mace]] but was knocked unconscious by the acid retort at the same moment
- Milo used Temporal Stasis to incapacitate Neverember at a critical moment
- Clover called for a ceasefire when Neverember said "Parents are still alive," but Jaspar landed the final blow while Neverember still had his sword raised

### Neverember Freed
When Neverember was knocked unconscious, the purple/red corruption goo flowed out of his body. Tharizdun's voice spoke: **"Come to the Void."** The dark substance formed a **triangular portal to the Void**. Neverember fell unconscious but alive -- the possession was broken.

### Post-Combat
- Clover stabilized Neverember with Cure Wounds
- Neverember woke disoriented, confused about the timeline (thought weeks had passed; it was roughly one day)
- He had fragmented memories of falling, and a **"very vile voice"** speaking about **"resurrecting the Four Princes"** / **"Champions of the Four Princes"**
- He recognized the chain visible through the Void portal: **"Is that a chain? It looks like this weird place that I went to."**
- Neverember is alive, freed from possession, injured but stabilized, and with the party in The Depths
