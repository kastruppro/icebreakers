# Escape The Depths

## Objective
Escape the pocket dimension known as The Depths and return to the surface.

## Background
While investigating House Castellers' basement, the party discovered a whirlpool connected to the Tharizdun ritual pool. Clover heard his parents' voices calling from within, prompting the party to descend. When the rope anchor (tied to the Tharizdun statue) broke, all four party members were pulled into The Depths.

## Resolution - Session 26

The party escaped The Depths through two separate methods:

### Jaspar, Clover, and Lord Neverember
While Milo and Modred were in the Void striking the first chain, the **divine entity** (Jaspar's patron) appeared to Jaspar. After delivering warnings about the Shadow and reiterating the deal regarding Arthur De Moray, the entity transported Jaspar, [[Clover]], and [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] directly to the **Castellers mansion basement** -- the room with the Tharizdun statue and whirlpool -- where [[Ammalia_Castellers|Ammalia]] and [[Raenar_Neverember|Raenar]] were waiting.

### Milo and Modred
After destroying the first chain with the [[Tyrs_Gauntlets_Quest|Gauntlets of Tyr]], [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] and [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] were stranded in the Void when the portal closed behind them. They fell through the Void and landed on [[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina's]] boat. After receiving critical lore and anti-corruption bread from Sabrina, Milo drew a **triangle in the air with his dagger** and specified their destination, opening a portal back to the Water Sphere Chamber. From there, they arrived at the Castellers basement where the full party reunited.

## The Depths - Areas Explored

### 1. Shore of the Dead
Sandy beach littered with dead adventurers in various states of decay. Massive staircase leading up.

### 2. Water Sphere Chamber
Cave-like space with floating sentient water sphere surrounded by corruption liquid. Three stone pillars in a ring. Three paths leading to challenge rooms.

### 3. Fountain Room (COMPLETED - Session 23-24)
Survived rising blood and tentacle attacks. First stone pillar lit.

### 4. Illusion Room (COMPLETED - Session 24)
Detected real vs illusory water spheres using Detect Magic. Second stone pillar lit.

### 5. Brazier Room (COMPLETED - Session 24)
Kept five braziers lit using hidden ceiling plank mechanism. Third stone pillar lit.

### 6. Boss Arena (COMPLETED - Session 25)
Old Hydra + possessed Neverember defeated. Void portal opened.
