# Retrieve the Door's Key

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (side quest; the key is somewhere in the Feywild with an unknown fairy)
**Priority:** LOW (powerful if completed, but not urgent)
**Quest Giver:** [[The Door]] (sentient fae door in [[Mr. Plant]]'s basement)
**Started:** Session 30
**Last Updated:** Session 30

## Objective
Recover the Door's key from the fairy that stole it, or bring sufficient **duck oil** as an alternative tribute, so that The Door can be used to travel to any named person or place in the Feywild.

## Background
In [[Mr. Plant]]'s basement, the party found a blue-lit chamber with a bridge over crashing ocean water. At the end of the central bridge stood a single wooden door in a frame set against apparent open ocean. Two additional bridges led off broken to the left and right. Mr. Plant's sky-eye was still visible through the cloud cover.

When [[Clover]] reached for the handle, the handle turned into a human hand and slapped his away: *"How dare you touch me?"* The Door was sentient. It is a fae. It insisted on courtesy.

## What The Door Does
The Door is a **Feywild transit hub**. It can transport travelers to **any named person or place** in the Feywild. The traveler states a name or destination; The Door delivers them. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] tested it by feeding it the false name "Lyra Swiftclaw" (the false name the party had been using for [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]]) and The Door accepted the name without complaint -- it would take them to a Lyra Swiftclaw if one existed.

This is a much more powerful form of travel than the emotion-travel the Feywild normally uses. It does not require emotional regulation. It does not care about anxiety. It just needs a name.

## Why It Does Not Work Right Now
The Door is **locked**. A fairy flew through the basement at some point and stole its key. The Door does not know where. The Door was emphatic that lockpicking is not acceptable (*"how dare you even ask?"*). The key must be retrieved properly.

Alternatively, The Door will accept **whale oil or (preferably) duck oil** as a tribute. This was offered explicitly -- it is a tribute The Door can take in lieu of the key, not a replacement for the key's mechanical function. The exact quantity and whether the tribute fully unlocks the Door (or just buys a one-time use) is unclear.

## The Key Fairy
- Gender, species (within fae), and motive: unknown
- Current location: unknown -- somewhere in the Feywild
- Took the key from Mr. Plant's basement on some unspecified occasion
- The Door did not describe the fairy further than *"a fairy"*

## Duck Oil (alternative)
The Door mentioned: *"If you squeeze them, they will --"* before Clover interrupted to ask if the ducks liked that. *"I don't care."* Duck oil appears to be a standard Feywild commodity. Ducks themselves are unspecified -- unclear whether Feywild ducks are regular animals, fae creatures, or something weirder. The party has not yet encountered any ducks in the Feywild. Whale oil also works but is implied to be a worse tribute.

## Why This Matters
If the party reaches a point where they have a name for **anything** -- a missing PC, a hidden Prince of Tharizdun, a chain location, an NPC they need to reach -- The Door is the fastest way there. It is strictly more useful than the emotion-travel system and strictly more useful than a second wine bottle. It also bypasses needing to regulate emotion, which makes it a backup if the [[Reach_the_Pool_of_Wisdom|Pool of Wisdom]] remains elusive.

## Key Questions
- Which fairy? There are presumably many fairies in the Feywild.
- Does the key travel with the fairy, or was it dropped somewhere?
- Will duck oil fully replace the key or only buy a single use?
- Does a name need to be a real name, or can the fake-name trick continue? (The Door accepted "Lyra Swiftclaw" without question -- but this may be a courtesy, and it may refuse to deliver to a non-existent name.)
- Does The Door know about Okramok if the party tries that name?
