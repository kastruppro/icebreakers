# Protect the Swiftclock Family

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (URGENT - Liora in the Feywild, Arthur's status unknown)
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Quest Giver:** [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora Swiftclock]] (Telepathic Message)
**Started:** Session 26
**Last Updated:** Session 30

## Objective
Recover Liora from [[Cora]] and keep the Swiftclock family safe from the cult.

## Background
Upon returning to the material plane from the Void in Session 26, [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] received four telepathic messages from his sister [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] in rapid succession:

1. *"Milo, can you hear me? We had to leave the house because Cora has been trying to find us."*
2. *"Milo, please answer. We're hiding in the Neverwinter Forest."*
3. *"We found shelter with the eladrin in New Sharandar in the northern part of Neverwinter Forest. Can you meet us there?"*
4. (Prophetic voice -- multiple voices around Liora's): *"The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun. The chains will wither all you hold."*

## Current Situation

### Family Location
The Swiftclock family has fled their home in Neverwinter and taken shelter with the **eladrin in [[New_Sharandar|New Sharandar]]**, in the northern part of Neverwinter Forest. New Sharandar is a known eladrin settlement previously connected to Milo's backstory (Merrisara Winterwhite guided restoration of Old Sharandar).

### The Threat
- [[Cora]] is actively hunting the Swiftclock family
- Cora is a dangerous antagonist who uses triangular portals and has repeatedly escaped the party
- The party has nicknamed her the "backflipping bitch"
- Cora's motivations for targeting the Swiftclocks are not fully clear but likely connected to Tharizdun's cult

### Liora's Prophecy
The fourth message contained a **prophetic warning** delivered in multiple voices:

> "The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun. The chains will wither all you hold."

This prophecy connects to:
- [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora's]] original prophecy about choosing between family and power
- The ongoing chain destruction quest ([[Tyrs_Gauntlets_Quest]])
- Potential destruction of Neverwinter Forest as a consequence of fighting Tharizdun

### Converging Threats in Neverwinter Forest
Multiple dangers converge in the same area:
- **Cora** hunting the Swiftclock family
- **The Shadow** ([[Arthur_De_Moray|Arthur De Moray]]) roaming the forest in pain, endangering people
- **Cult activity** spotted by [[Sir_Goodwin_De_Moray|Sir Goodwin's]] spies -- likely signaling a nearby Prince dungeon
- Liora's prophecy about the forest burning

## Session 27 - Arrived in Neverwinter

The party traveled to [[Neverwinter]] via [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar's]] gauntlet portal (no Void passage). They are now inside the city, disguised and heading toward the docks / Thirsty Goat tavern. [[New_Sharandar|New Sharandar]] in northern Neverwinter Forest remains the next destination to reach the Swiftclock family.

## Session 28 - Reunited and Re-Captured

### Liora Found at New Sharandar
The party arrived at New Sharandar and [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] reunited with [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]]. She confirmed she and Arthur had fled Neverwinter when Cora got too close, that Arthur turned violent when he crossed the Mythal ward and was subdued by the eladrin, and that she had been sheltered in the camp since. She had not been allowed to visit Arthur.

> "Oh my god, Milo, I was so glad to see you. I thought something was happening to you." - Liora to Milo

Liora was weaving a bouquet with an elf friend when Milo found her. She was physically safe and well, though shaken by what she had witnessed with Arthur.

### Liora Re-Captured by Cora
During the cultist assault on New Sharandar at the end of the session, [[Cora]] appeared in the chaos and grabbed Liora, fleeing with her out of the burning camp. The session ended before the party could respond. Liora's current location is unknown -- Cora fled using the confusion of the burning Temple of Leaves and a wave of attacking cultists to make her escape.

This repeats the pattern of Session 1-early campaign: Cora was involved the first time Liora went missing. She remains a recurring obstacle in Liora's safety.

### Prophecy Connection
The fourth message from Session 26 -- *"The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun"* -- came true in this session. The Temple of Leaves, the golden heart of New Sharandar, was set ablaze by the cult. The second clause -- *"The chains will wither all you hold"* -- remains ominous.

## Session 29 - Pursuit into the Feywild

### Chase Through Burning New Sharandar
The party chased [[Cora]] through the burning ruins of New Sharandar while the [[Mythal]] failed visibly overhead. Clover dropped a 100-foot [[Plant Growth]] ten feet ahead of Cora, which she cleared in a single flash-teleport -- a new capability. Milo was downed twice in the chase and fight. Modred killed the "blue" cultist with an Action Surge. [[Merrisara_Winterwhite|Merrisara]] personally arrived with a soldier to hold the line at the portal.

### Liora Taken Into the Feywild
Cora reached the oak portal tree first and vanished through it carrying Liora. The party finished the portal fight and ran through after her, entering the [[Feywild]]. Merrisara shouted something as they crossed -- unheard, possibly survival-critical Feywild information.

### Arthur's Fate -- Unknown
[[Arthur_De_Moray|Arthur De Moray]] was in the quarantine tent outside the Mythal when the Session 28 attack began, under eladrin restraint because he turned violent crossing the ward. The party did not reach the quarantine tent during the escape and did not follow up on him before crossing the portal. His status is unknown: he could have been killed, captured, freed by the chaos, or still be under eladrin care (Merrisara was still alive and fighting when the party left).

### Prophecy Progress
The first clause of Liora's Session 26 prophecy -- *"The forest will burn in the destruction of Tharizdun"* -- is fully realized: New Sharandar and the Temple of Leaves burned to the ground in Sessions 28-29. The second clause -- *"The chains will wither all you hold"* -- remains ominous and pending.

## Session 30 - No Updates on Either Swiftclock

### Liora -- Still Missing
The party reached [[The Moonlit Pool]] in the Feywild via wine-fueled emotion travel but did not see [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] at the camp. [[Cora]] was there with [[House Castellers]] cultists and Clover's parents, but not Liora. The [[Queen of Darkness]] manifested a shadow-puppet of Liora earlier in the session pleading *"They have captured me. Please, you need to find me."* Milo tested it by asking about a conversation outside their house -- the shadow glitched. It was an illusion, not real contact. Liora's current location remains unknown; most likely she was moved below the dry pool with Clover's parents, or held elsewhere in the Queen's domain. See [[Find_Liora]] for full detail.

### Arthur -- Still No Update
[[Arthur_De_Moray|Arthur]]'s fate is unchanged from Session 29. He was last seen in the quarantine tent outside the Mythal when the Session 28 attack began. The party is in the Feywild and cannot check on him. The Queen of Darkness's shadow-puppet of Arthur (*"Brother, come to me. The place is burning. I've been here for a while. Why haven't you come?"*) was tested with Modred's favorite-dish question and failed. The real Arthur was not contacted. His status: unknown.

### New Dimension to the Threat
The party now knows [[Cora]] is the **daughter of the Queen of the Unseelie Court** -- the crown princess of the Winter Court. This means every Swiftclock kidnapping since Session 01 has been an operation run by (or at least known to) the Queen of Darkness herself. The Queen explicitly claimed Milo's loved ones are *"in my care"* and threatened that the party would never find them. This dramatically escalates the threat level against the Swiftclock family from "ambitious rogue cultist" to "foreign crown princess with the full backing of the Winter Court."

## Urgency Assessment
**CRITICAL** -- Liora is now in the [[Feywild]] in Cora's hands. Arthur's status is unknown. The eladrin refuge at New Sharandar is destroyed; the family no longer has a safe location. The party must catch Cora in the Feywild and, eventually, return to determine what happened to Arthur.
