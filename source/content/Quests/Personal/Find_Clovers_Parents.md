# Find Clover's Parents

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (Deaths Confirmed Deliberate)
**Priority:** Medium (Personal Quest - New Revelations)
**Quest Giver:** [[Clover]] (Personal Discovery)
**Started:** Session 23
**Last Updated:** Session 24

## Objective
Investigate the voices Clover heard in the Tharizdun pool and determine if her parents are truly in The Depths.

## Background

### The Moonlit Pool Connection
Clover's family served as Protectors of the Moonlit Pool - a sacred site connected to their protector deity. When the party discovered the Tharizdun ritual pool beneath House Castellers, Clover immediately recognized it:

> The pool had the same markings, the same earthly feel with vines, the same magic his parents used to safeguard their relic.

This suggests a network of connected pools tied to various divine or dark entities.

### The Parents' Fate (Previously Understood)
- Clover's parents were killed when cultists attacked their home
- Clover saw them traveling to the afterlife in a vision (Session 21 or earlier)
- He told Thistle their parents were dead
- Their physical remains should be gone

### The Voices
While meditating by the Castellers pool, Clover heard:

> **"Save us! We're down here! Please save us!"**

The voices belonged to her parents. On an Insight check of 7, they sounded exactly like them.

Clover told the party: **"Guys, you will not believe this. I'm 99% sure that my parents are down in that pool."**

## Key Questions

### Is This Real or a Trap?
- Clover saw her parents' spirits departing - how could they be alive?
- The Depths is controlled by an evil Water Sphere Entity that enjoys killing adventurers
- Could this be a lure to draw victims deeper?
- Does Tharizdun's corruption allow mimicking voices?
- Could their souls be trapped rather than their bodies?

### Pool Network Theory
- The Moonlit Pool and Castellers pool share identical magic and markings
- Are all such pools connected?
- Did Tharizdun's cult corrupt an existing divine network?
- Could Clover's parents have been pulled through the pool network?

### Afterlife vs. Imprisonment
- If Clover saw them passing to the afterlife, they should be dead
- Could their souls have been intercepted by Tharizdun?
- Are they trapped in The Depths rather than resting peacefully?
- Is this connected to the "binding ritual" the cultists mentioned?

## Session 24 Revelations

The entity possessing Lord Neverember in The Depths directly addressed Clover (who was absent -- player not present) and revealed critical information about his parents:

### "They Were Just in the Way"
The entity confirmed that Clover's parents' deaths were **deliberate and planned**, not collateral damage:

> **Entity:** "They were just in the way."

When Milo confronted the entity about the parents, and Modred accused them of killing Clover's parents, the entity dismissed it casually -- their deaths served the cult's purposes.

### "A Prince of Tharizdun"
The entity also said something deeply disturbing to/about Clover:

> **Entity:** "I should really be thanking you, because you managed to get this prince of Tharizdun to us. It was so easy."

This suggests Clover **unwittingly delivered** something important to the cult -- a "prince of Tharizdun." This connects to **The Princes of Tharizdun** book found in Session 23. The parents may have been killed specifically because they were obstacles to obtaining this "prince."

### Implications
- The parents' deaths were not random cult violence -- they were targeted because they were "in the way"
- As Protectors of the Moonlit Pool, the parents may have been guarding something the cult needed
- Clover unknowingly helped the cult's plan, possibly by carrying or delivering whatever "prince" the entity refers to
- The voices Clover heard in the pool may have been a deliberate lure connected to this manipulation
- Clover was not present to hear any of this -- the party will need to tell him

## Current Situation
