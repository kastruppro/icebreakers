# Reclaim Neverwinter from Sebastian

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (JUST ARRIVED -- Situation Worse Than Expected)
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Quest Giver:** [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]]
**Started:** Session 27
**Last Updated:** Session 27

## Objective
Remove [[Sebastian]] from power in [[Neverwinter]] and restore [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] as rightful ruler. Break Sebastian's mass memory enchantment on the city's populace.

## Background
[[Sebastian]] won an election and took control of Neverwinter while [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] was imprisoned in The Depths by Tharizdun cultists. Sebastian is allied with the cult of Tharizdun and has fully corrupted hands (black). Upon the party's arrival in Session 27, they discovered Sebastian's hold on the city is far deeper than political -- he has magically rewritten the memories of the populace.

## The Problem

### Mass Memory Alteration
Sebastian has cast some form of mass enchantment on Neverwinter's citizens:
- Guards at the front gates did not recognize Lord Neverember
- A guard stated Sebastian has been lord *"as long as I've been born, I think"*
- Another guard declared: *"The Lord of Neverember is the best"*
- This is not simple mind control -- memories appear to have been completely rewritten
- The depth and scope of the enchantment are unknown (entire city? key personnel only?)

### Propaganda
- Banners bearing Sebastian's face are posted throughout the city
- An **eye symbol** appears above Sebastian's head on the banners (possibly connected to the Elemental Eye from Session 26)
- The visual propaganda reinforces the magical memory alteration

### Sebastian's Resources
- Former head of the [[Mages_of_the_Clock_Tower|Clock Tower]] -- has arcane power and political connections
- Allied with Tharizdun's cult -- may have cult soldiers and magical support
- Controls the city guard through enchantment
- Fully corrupted (black hands) -- may possess corruption-enhanced abilities

## Session 27 - Arrival and Assessment

### Entry
The party entered Neverwinter disguised as "6 humble merchants" wearing cloaks and hoods. [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] noted he cannot easily disguise his Harengon appearance. Sebastian knows the party are his enemies after previous encounters.

### Initial Plan
- Head to the **Thirsty Goat** tavern in the docks -- [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar's]] family and contacts are there
- Sebastian's influence is likely weakest in the working-class dock district
- Avoid the castle -- guards may be enchanted to capture Lord Neverember on sight
- Find allies who remember the real Lord Neverember

### Neverember's Intel
Before departing Waterdeep, Neverember identified potential allies:
- **[[House_Timore|House Timore]]** -- could lend soldiers for retaking the city
- **[[Liset]]** (masked woman from the floating bar) -- intelligence asset, clever and knowledgeable about the city, but not a fighter
- **Gate guards** -- Neverember believed they were loyal to him (this proved wrong)
- Neverember also has contacts within Neverwinter who may have escaped the enchantment

### Jaspar's Docks Proposal
While walking toward the Thirsty Goat, [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]] pitched [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] a business proposal: if Neverember ever wanted to reward a noble for saving him, Jaspar would handle the docks and give Neverember a share of profits. Neverember seemed cautiously interested but the conversation was cut short.

### Dispel Magic Discussion
- [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] suggested using Dispel Magic on individual guards' minds
- Milo pointed out they would need to dispel potentially the entire city -- impractical one by one
- A more efficient method of breaking the enchantment is needed
