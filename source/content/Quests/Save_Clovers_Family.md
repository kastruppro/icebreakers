# Save Clover's Family

## Quick Reference
**Status:** Active (PARENTS CONFIRMED ALIVE AT THE MOONLIT POOL, cooperating with Cora)
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Quest Giver:** [[Clover]] (personal)
**Started:** Session 30
**Last Updated:** Session 31

## Objective
Determine whether [[Clover]]'s parents -- the former Protectors of [[The Moonlit Pool]] -- are coerced, deceived, compelled, or voluntarily aligned with [[Cora]] and the Unseelie Court, then extract them if possible. Discover what **Okramok** is and why Clover's parents are protecting it.

## Background
Clover's entire origin was built on the belief that his parents were killed when cultists attacked the Moonlit Pool years before he joined the party. He grieved them. He told his sister [[Thistle]] they were dead. He has been a Protector of the Moonlit Pool carrying his family's legacy for 29 sessions.

Session 24-26 already destabilized this. The Tharizdun entity possessing [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] claimed the parents were alive in the [[Void]]. Modred glimpsed them in the Void soul procession on [[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina's]] boat (Session 26). The party believed the Void was where to rescue them. See [[Personal/Find_Clovers_Parents]] for the prior quest.

Session 30 broke the picture open. The parents are **not** in the Void. They are at [[The Moonlit Pool]] in the Feywild. Alive. With Cora. The Moonlit Pool was not destroyed. The cult narrative was a lie (or a separate event that has been misunderstood for years).

## The Session 30 Confrontation

### The Queen of Darkness's Shadow-Puppets
Inside the [[Queen of Darkness]]'s diamond chamber, the Queen manifested shadows of Clover's parents. They said:
> *"Clover, you need to save us, please. We're at the Moonlit Pool right now."*

Clover, instead of trusting the emotional hook, tested them. He asked them to tell him the first spell he cast at age 4. They answered it back to him **word for word**. Nat-20 Insight from Clover read them as genuine. The Queen confirmed with her own words: *"We are real, but these are not. We are alive. Leave this place and just -- try to find us."*

The Queen's follow-up threat was directed to all three PCs: *"You will never find peace. You will never find your family, even though they are alive and in my care."*

### The Moonlit Pool Scene
Wine-fueled emotion travel dropped the party at the tree line above the Moonlit Pool itself. Clover said one word: *"I'm home."* Through the trees the party saw:
- The Moonlit Pool, **dry** (water gone)
- [[House Castellers]] tents around the pool
- Cages of captive fae creatures of all kinds
- Two perimeter-guard cultists and one cultist standing with Cora
- [[Cora]] speaking calmly with two older harengons -- Clover's **parents**

Clover read Cora's lips from the tree line:
> *"I've been followed in here. We need to protect **Okramok**. You need to jump down into the pool now."*

Without hesitation, Clover's parents jumped into the dry pool and vanished. Not to the bottom -- they simply vanished. The pool is a passage down. Okramok is below.

## Key Questions

### Why do they believe they should take orders from Cora?
They jumped into the dry pool the moment Cora told them to. No hesitation. No argument. No visible compulsion effects. Options:
- **Coerced** -- something is held over them (Thistle? Clover himself? a threat the party cannot see)
- **Deceived** -- they believe Okramok must be protected for a reason that was lied to them
- **Compelled** -- a geas, charm, or Unseelie-court binding forces obedience
- **Voluntary** -- worst case; they have aligned with the Unseelie Court willingly, for reasons yet unknown

### What is Okramok?
New proper noun. Introduced Session 30. Being "protected" by Cora and Clover's parents below the dry Moonlit Pool. Candidates:
- A Prince of Tharizdun hidden in the Feywild (Corellon said the remaining Princes cannot be sensed in this land -- because they are not in this land)
- One of Tharizdun's remaining three chains, moved to the Feywild for safekeeping
- A Feywild-specific cult asset
- An entity or being (less likely given the word structure and the "protect" framing)

### Why was Clover told his parents were dead?
For 29 sessions, Clover has believed his parents died during the cultist attack on the Moonlit Pool. That story is false. Possibilities:
- Fae deception (someone lied to him directly, possibly as a small child)
- Memory alteration (cult or Unseelie magic)
- Misinterpretation of a real event (they may have been taken rather than killed, and Clover saw something he read as death)
- The Session 22 Void vision of them on a boat was an illusion or a planted memory

### What about Session 26's Void sighting?
Modred saw them in the Void soul procession. Either:
- That sighting was an illusion (the entity or the Boatman showed the party what they wanted to see)
- The sighting was real -- they were briefly in the Void before being recovered into the Feywild (unlikely mechanically)
- There are two separate entities (also unlikely)
