# Through [[The_Void|the Void]] to [[Waterdeep]]

**Date:** 2024-09-25
**Party Present:** [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo Swiftclock]], [[Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]], [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar Starshade]], [[Clover]]

## Quick Summary
> The party prepares to depart [[Neverwinter]] for [[Waterdeep]] via triangular portal. [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] delivers a prophecy to [[Clover]] about choosing between family and power. [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] learns the dark truth about his sister's visions from their mother. The party travels through [[The_Void|the Void]] where each member faces individual trials - [[Clover]] refuses a divine entity's offer of power, [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]] meets his celestial "father" who gives him a sample of power, and [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]]/[[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]]/[[Clover]] encounter the ghost [[Sabrina_Nightgale]] on a boat sailing through the underworld. They emerge in a forest near [[Waterdeep]] where they rescue [[Raenar_Neverember]] from his own hunting trap.

## Revelations & Lore

- **[[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]]'s Visions Origin:** Dark hands with chains emerged from grandfather's ritual portal and tried to grab young [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]]; her visions started after this incident
- **The Cyst:** A floating object in [[The_Void|the Void]] that may be [[Tharizdun]]'s "shell" or where he originated from - unbreakable by any means
- **Shadow Mechanics:** The Shadow is summoned via ritual; its purpose is to possess people and serve [[Tharizdun]]
- **Freeing the Possessed:** The only way to free someone possessed by the Shadow is to bring them to [[The_Void|the Void]] and perform a binding ritual (extremely risky)
- **Void Danger:** If the Shadow is freed in [[The_Void|the Void]], it would break [[Tharizdun]]'s chains and release everything from the underworld
- **[[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina]]'s Party:** Consisted of Edward Hammel (human), Elise Mayak (firbolg), and Ron Tusk (young dwarf) - all likely dead except possibly Ron
- **Souls Trapped:** [[Tharizdun]] has paused the flow of souls to the afterlife; millions are stuck in the underworld
