# Session 25: The Depths: Old Hydra

**Date:** [[2026-04-03]]
**Party Present:** [[Milo Swiftclock]], [[Sir Modred De Moray]], [[Jaspar Starshade]], [[Clover]]

## Quick Summary
> Deep beneath [[House Castellers]] in [[The Depths]], The Icebreakers faced the possessed [[Lord Neverember]] and his Water Elemental guardian, [[Old Hydra]], in a grueling boss fight. The elemental's acid body cycle and devastating storm pillar environmental hazards nearly caused a total party kill -- a blizzard knocked out three of four party members simultaneously. [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] delivered the killing blow on Old Hydra with the [[Sunfire Mace]], but the acid retort knocked him unconscious at the same moment. [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]] landed the final hit on Neverember, freeing him from [[Tharizdun]]'s possession as the corruption flowed out and formed a triangular portal to [[The Void]]. Through it, a massive chain of Tharizdun hangs waiting. The freed Neverember revealed hearing about "resurrecting the Four Princes," and the entity's parting words to [[Clover]] changed everything: "Parents are still alive." The session ended with Neverember recognizing the chain visible through the portal -- "It looks like this weird place that I went to."

## Characters Met

### New NPCs
- **[[Old Hydra]]** - Water Elemental - Guardian summoned by the possessed [[Lord Neverember]] in [[The Depths]]. Also called "Bullhydra." Had an acid body cycling mechanic (alternating blue and green forms) and could generate environmental weather hazards through the room's stone pillars. Killed by [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]]'s [[Sunfire Mace]]. Status: Dead.

### Returning NPCs
- **[[Lord Neverember]]** - Freed from [[Tharizdun]]'s possession after the party defeated him in combat. The corruption (purple/red goo) left his body and formed a triangular portal to [[The Void]]. Regained consciousness disoriented, with fragmented memories. Revealed hearing a vile voice talking about "resurrecting the Four Princes." Recognized the Void chain at session's end. Status: Alive, freed from possession, injured but stabilized.

## Places Visited

### [[The Depths]]
**Water Sphere Chamber (Boss Arena):**
- The central chamber where three water spheres from Session 24's challenge rooms merged into a typhoon that formed [[Old Hydra]]
- Three stone pillars generated environmental hazards during combat: thunderstorm circles dealing lightning damage (Con save DC 17) and blizzard circles dealing flat cold damage (no save)
- Dead bodies of various races littered the room, some ancient and some recent
- After combat, the [[Tharizdun]] corruption substance left [[Lord Neverember]]'s body and formed a triangular portal to [[The Void]]

### [[The Void]]
**Visible Through the Triangular Portal:**
- A massive chain hangs from the air and ends at the floor
- Screams echo from within
- This is one of the chain locations where the [[Gauntlets of Tyr]] must be used to strike [[Tharizdun]]'s chains
- [[Lord Neverember]] recognized it as a place he visited during his possession

## Key Events Timeline

1. **The Entity Taunts Clover** - Possessed [[Lord Neverember]] addressed [[Clover]] directly, thanking him for "bringing Old Hydra back to us" and referencing killing "The Druid of the Forest" and slitting throats. Clover fired back: "You know what else is gonna feel good? When I yank your head off."
2. **Modred Challenged** - The entity pointed at [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]]'s gloved hand covering his blackened fingers: "I see you for who you really are. There's so much more power to you." Modred refused: "Who refuses to fight is a fearful, and I can smell your fear."
3. **Combat Begins** - The entity called out "Bullhydra, I guess we will teach them a lesson" and the Water Elemental roared. Initiative: Clover 21, Jaspar 21, Milo 20, Old Hydra, Lord Neverember, Modred 16.
4. **Clover's Early Setback** - Clover cast Elemental Weapon on Modred's weapon, then Wild Shaped into a Giant Constrictor Snake. However, Old Hydra's attack broke his concentration immediately, dropping the buff from Modred.
5. **Milo Hastes Modred** - Milo cast Haste on Modred, granting him double speed, +2 AC, advantage on Dex saves, and an additional weapon attack per turn.
6. **Modred's Assault on Neverember** - Modred charged with Haste active, landing multiple hits including a natural 20 for 29 damage and using Action Surge. He dealt approximately 38 damage to Neverember in the first round alone.
7. **Old Hydra's Acid Body Cycle** - The elemental alternated between blue and green forms. When green, any melee attacker took acid damage in retort, punishing the party's front-line fighters throughout the battle.
8. **Jaspar's Devastating Shot** - Jaspar fired his prototype gauntlet with sneak attack for 35 total fire damage (19 fire + 16 sneak attack) against Lord Neverember, shouting "Hold him, Clover!" and "I smell piss!"
9. **Milo Stabs Himself** - Attempting Booming Blade on Neverember, Milo rolled two natural 1s and stabbed himself for 3 damage. Multiple dice were sent to dice jail.
10. **Jaspar's Faerie Fire** - Jaspar cast Faerie Fire on both enemies, granting the party advantage on attacks as at least one target failed the save.
11. **Milo's Sapping Sting** - Milo successfully landed Sapping Sting on Lord Neverember (after a failed attempt where even Chronal Shift could not force a failure), knocking him prone and reducing his effectiveness.
12. **Thunderstorm Hazard** - Old Hydra raised its hands and the stone pillars generated a thunderstorm. Con save DC 17; everyone except Jaspar failed, taking 10 lightning damage. Modred used Absorb Elements to halve his damage and store the energy.
13. **The Blizzard Nearly TPKs** - The pillars generated a blizzard dealing 24 flat cold damage to everyone in the circles. Milo went down (even with his [[Ring of Cold Resistance]] halving the damage). Modred took exactly 24 damage and went down with 2 death save failures. Clover's snake form dropped and excess damage carried over. Only Jaspar remained at reasonable health.
14. **Clover Heals Modred** - Clover cast Cure Wounds (upcasted to level 2) for 16 HP, bringing Modred back, then Wild Shaped into a Giant Elk.
15. **Jaspar Revives Milo** - Jaspar rushed to unconscious Milo and used a healing device to restore him, then dashed out of the hazard zone.
16. **Modred Kills Old Hydra** - Modred attacked Old Hydra with the [[Sunfire Mace]], its blinding light evaporating the Water Elemental completely. But the acid retort from the killing blow knocked Modred unconscious again at 0 HP. He went down for the second time.
17. **Modred's Nonlethal Intent** - Before the killing blow on Old Hydra, Modred had attacked Neverember with the flat of his sword: "Time to sleep, Lord Neverember!" -- choosing nonlethal damage to preserve Neverember's life.
18. **Milo Freezes Neverember** - Milo cast Temporal Stasis on Lord Neverember (Con save DC 15, Neverember rolled 5), incapacitating him completely.
19. **Clover's Devastating Elk Charge** - Clover (Giant Elk) rammed Lord Neverember for 22 bludgeoning damage, knocking him prone and breaking Milo's Temporal Stasis.
20. **"Parents Are Still Alive"** - Wounded and prone, Lord Neverember spoke in a faint voice: "Parents are still alive. You should come with me." Clover was hit with a wave of emotions.
21. **Clover Calls for Ceasefire** - Clover dismissed elk form, returned to harengon, raised his hand: "Everyone, we need to stop. He says my parents are alive."
22. **Jaspar Ends It** - Despite Clover's plea, Jaspar did not trust Neverember with a sword still raised. He landed a dagger hit for 10 damage. Lord Neverember went down as the corruption (purple/red goo) crunched out of his chest, forming a triangular portal to [[The Void]]. Tharizdun's voice echoed: "Come to the Void."
23. **Clover Heals and Kicks Neverember** - Clover cast Cure Wounds on the freed Neverember, then kicked him in the ribs: "Wake up, you lazy bastard!"
24. **Neverember's Fragmented Memories** - The freed lord was confused, thought weeks had passed (it was one day), and did not know why he was in Waterdeep. He revealed hearing "a very vile voice that talked about the Four Princes. Something about resurrecting the Four Princes."
25. **Neverember Recognizes the Chain** - As the party prepared to leave, Neverember stared at the portal: "Is that a chain? It looks like this weird place that I went to." The party demanded he tell them everything. Session ended.

## Revelations & Lore

- **Clover's Parents Are Alive:** The entity (through Neverember) stated "Parents are still alive" and "Come to the Void" -- suggesting Clover's parents are imprisoned in [[The Void]], not dead as previously believed.
- **The Four Princes / Champions of the Four Princes:** Lord Neverember heard a vile voice ([[Tharizdun]]) talking about "resurrecting the Four Princes" or "Champions of the Four Princes." This connects to the "Princes of Tharizdun" from the book found in Session 23.
- **Chain of Tharizdun Located:** The space visible through the Void portal contains one of [[Tharizdun]]'s chains that must be struck with the [[Gauntlets of Tyr]]. The DM will no longer refer to these locations as "Ruins."
- **Neverember Visited the Void:** At session's end, Neverember recognized the chain and the Void space -- confirming he was taken to [[The Void]] during his possession and may have crucial information about it.
- **"The Druid of the Forest":** The entity referenced killing "The Druid of the Forest" in connection with Clover's parents, suggesting Clover's parents had a specific role or title.
- **Old Hydra's Mechanics:** The Water Elemental alternated between blue (normal) and acid green (melee retort damage) forms, and could generate environmental weather hazards (thunderstorm and blizzard) through the room's stone pillars.
