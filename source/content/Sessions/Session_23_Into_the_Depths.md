# Session 23: Into the Depths

**Date:** [[2026-02-04]]
**Party Present:** [[Milo Swiftclock]], [[Sir Modred De Moray]], [[Jaspar Starshade]], [[Clover]]

## Quick Summary
> The Icebreakers infiltrated House Castellers' basement at 4am, discovered an empty ritual chamber, and fireballed six sleeping cultists. After raiding the family mausoleum for the powerful Sunfire Mace, Clover heard her dead parents' voices calling from within a magical pool. The party descended into a dimension beneath the pool - a death trap dungeon controlled by a sentient water sphere entity. Trapped in a room filling with blood, Milo and Jaspar were dragged underwater by unknown creatures as the session ended on a cliffhanger.

## Characters Met

### New NPCs
- **[[Water Sphere Entity]]** - Unknown - Massive sentient water sphere that controls the dungeon beneath House Castellers, divides into three smaller spheres, and fills rooms with blood. "More adventurers has come to die. Well, let the games begin."

### Returning NPCs
- **[[Ammalia Castellers]]** - Dismissed all guards and escorted the party into the basement. Provided information about the mausoleum mace. Left behind when the party fell into the pool.
- **[[Raenar Neverember]]** - Arrived armored and armed, ready for battle. Left behind when the party fell into the pool.
- **[[Daggers of the North]]** - Stood guard at the basement entrance, confirmed no one left during the night. Still on watch above ground.

## Places Visited

### [[House Castellers]]
**Basement Ritual Chamber:**
- Massive statue of [[Tharizdun]] depicted unchained (free), holding a chain going into a whirlpool
- A swirling pool of water identical to the [[Moonlit Pool]] that Clover's family protected
- Ritual circle with runes, altar tables with wine bottles, daggers (one bloodied), candles
- The chamber was empty - no cultists, no [[Lord Neverember]]

**Storage/Sleeping Quarters:**
- Six cultists sleeping in beds
- Hidden passage behind a wardrobe, revealed after Milo's Fireball burned everything
- All six cultists killed in the fire

**Hidden Shrine:**
- Small prayer room with another unchained Tharizdun statue
- Candles and a rock for meditation
- Triangular burn mark on wall indicating portal use
- Modred shattered the statue with his [[Gauntlets of Tyr]]

**Castellers Mausoleum:**
- Iron-gated tomb reading "Here lies the remains of House Castellers"
- Sarcophagus containing a body clutching the [[Sunfire Mace]]

### [[The Depths]]
**Whirlpool Passage:**
- Violent water current pulled the entire party down when the statue leg broke
- Clover (as giant octopus) tried to catch falling friends: Milo (20 - caught), Modred (8 - missed), Jaspar (16 - caught)

**Shore of the Dead:**
- Sandy beach littered with dead adventurers - humanoids, dwarves, gnomes in various states of decay
- Massive staircase leading up from the shore

**Water Sphere Chamber:**
- Cave-like space with a huge floating water sphere within a ring of three stone pillars
- Purple-black corruption liquid floating around the sphere
- The sphere divided into three smaller spheres, each floating down separate stairways

**Fountain Room:**
- Tall fountain (100+ feet) with a water sphere on top
- Three floor drains, a door 120 feet up in the ceiling
- Became a death trap when drains were clogged - entrance sealed, sphere turned red and poured blood instead of water

## Revelations & Lore

- **The Princes of Tharizdun:** A book detailing Tharizdun and his "princes" (including one called "Bull Hydra") - confirms earlier mentions of four princes and four hideouts
- **The Castellers Pool:** Identical to the Moonlit Pool that Clover's family protected - same markings, same earthly feel with vines, same magic
- **Corruption Attraction:** The purple-black corruption liquid is drawn to and absorbed by Tharizdun-related pools and rituals
- **Lord Neverember Missing:** He was not in the basement - the ritual chamber was empty, and he has been moved somewhere unknown
- **Triangular Portals:** The cultists used triangular portals to escape (burn marks found in the hidden shrine)
- **The Depths:** A pocket dimension or underworld exists beneath the Castellers pool, filled with the bodies of dead adventurers
