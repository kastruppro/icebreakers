## Quick Summary

> The party snapped back from [[Corellon Larethian|Corellon]]'s vision into a burning [[Temple of Leaves]], with [[Cora]] already sprinting out of the camp carrying [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]]. The DM ran the pursuit as a chase scene with Athletics checks in place of initiative. [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] spent the whole chase lagging behind — eating a sword edge, diving under a falling burning tree, and watching the [[Mythal]] dome visibly fade from the sky — while [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] and [[Clover]] closed on [[Cora]]. [[Clover]] dropped a perfectly placed [[Plant Growth]] ten feet in front of her; [[Cora]] flash-teleported clear of it in a single move. The party ran her down to the oak fey-crossing, where cultists with glowing forehead-eyes poured out of the portal itself. A brutal fight followed: [[Clover]] went in as a dire wolf, [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] dropped Fog Cloud and then dropped to 0 through it, [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] took a nat-20 arrow through the stomach (Silvery Barbs'd to a normal hit) and action-surged to split the lead cultist in two. [[Merrisara Winterwhite]] arrived personally to hold the line. [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] came up from 0 a second time, burned a 3rd-level slot to Fireball two bloodied cultists out of pure spite, and the party sprinted into the portal tree without hearing [[Merrisara Winterwhite|Merrisara]]'s final shout. They ended the session inside the [[Feywild]], in pursuit, with two pieces of guidance now cut off mid-sentence — [[Corellon Larethian|Corellon]]'s and [[Merrisara Winterwhite|Merrisara]]'s.

## Revelations & Lore

- The [[Mythal]] protecting [[New Sharandar]] is not invincible — a concentrated cult assault burned it down in real time. Milo watched it visibly fade from the sky during the chase; by the time the party left camp, the ward was effectively gone.
- [[Cora]] has a short-range flash-teleport beyond her triangular portal exits — she cleared a 100-foot [[Plant Growth]] in a single move rather than walk it.
- The cult's [[Tharizdun]]-aligned troops at New Sharandar are each marked with an open eye in the forehead that lights up when they cast. Eldritch Blast came out as a direct beam from the forehead-eye with no somatic or verbal motion — a glare, not a cantrip cast. This suggests a shared binding or conditioning, not just robed cultists.
- At least one cultist can cast Spirit Weapon — a purple-black gooey summoned sword.
- Cultists emerged from inside the portal tree itself during the fight, confirming [[Corellon Larethian|Corellon]]'s Session 28 warning that the cult is entering the Material Plane from the [[Feywild]] side. The portal tree is their actual route.
- The oak portal tree does not flicker visually when the portal is active — cultists just run "through" it as if through a door, making it nearly impossible to read as a portal on sight from the forest side.
- Cultists preferred capture to kill with Milo — they reversed their swords and flat-bladed him unconscious rather than run him through, consistent with the Cora / Liora kidnapping pattern.
