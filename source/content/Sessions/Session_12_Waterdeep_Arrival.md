# [[Waterdeep]] Arrival and Dark Revelations

**Date:** 2025-10-09
**Party Present:** [[Milo Swiftclock]], [[Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]], [[Jaspar Starshade]], [[Clover]]

## Quick Summary
> The party arrives in [[Waterdeep]] with [[Raenar_Neverember]], overwhelmed by the massive city ten times larger than [[Neverwinter]]. After meeting local merchants at [[Statue_Protectors_Square]], [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] confronts his broken father [[Godwin_De_Moray|Godwin]] at [[House_De_Moray]], learning his family has fallen apart since he left. At the [[Sated_Satyr_Inn]], the party officially names themselves "The [[The_Icebreakers]]" while discussing the upcoming masquerade. That night, [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] discovers his corruption from the portal has spread dramatically, and he races to the temples with [[Clover]] seeking help as [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] and [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]] sleep.

## Places Visited

### [[Waterdeep]]
**First Impressions:**
- City is 10 times larger than [[Neverwinter]]
- Overwhelming sensory experience: crowds, merchants, dogs, constant noise
- Entered through eastern gates

### [[Statue Protectors Square]]
**Central Hub:**
- Two massive protective statues that would animate to defend [[Waterdeep]] in war
- Forest-Pointing Statue (Elven woman with bow, flowers in hair) gives cryptic riddle about the Pilgrim's Road
- Sea-Pointing Statue (Human warrior with sword) speaks of treasure and faith
- Starting point for the Pilgrim's Road - traditional journey for treasure hunting, religious experiences, and coming-of-age trials

### [[House De Moray]]
**[[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]]'s Family Estate:**
- Located in North Ward noble district
- Tall, imposing building with blue banners featuring two crossed swords, one aflame
- Interior pitch black, windows covered
- [[Godwin_De_Moray|Godwin]] sits askew on throne in deplorable state - unkempt beard, unwashed, empty bottles scattered, foul smell
- Empty throne beside him marks mother's absence

### [[Sated Satyr Inn]]
**Party Lodging:**
- Fancy inn comparable to [[Neverwinter]]'s [[Moonstone_Mask]]
- Satyr bartender with hooves
- Room cost: 5 gold (includes meal, bath, room)
- Masquerade poster on wall
- Where party officially named themselves "The [[The_Icebreakers]]"

## Key Events Timeline

1. **Portal Aftermath** - After drawing the portal with the corrupted dagger, [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] feels "something has been lost." Rolls d100 (52) and gains 12 temporary HP. Notices fingers starting to turn black at tips - conceals with gloves.

2. **[[Waterdeep]] Arrival** - Party enters through eastern gates, overwhelmed by the city's scale (10x [[Neverwinter]]). [[Raenar_Neverember|Raenar]] guides them.

3. **[[Statue_Protectors_Square]]** - Meet [[Gigz]] (gnome inventor), [[Savan]] (mage), and [[Drox]] (minotaur armorsmith). Learn about diamond dust sources and the Pilgrim's Road.

4. **[[House_De_Moray]] Confrontation** - [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] returns home to find his father [[Godwin_De_Moray|Godwin]] broken and mad. [[Godwin_De_Moray|Godwin]] casts Wall of Fire on the party (20 damage). [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] uses Chronal Shift to reroll and successfully Dispel Magic. [[Godwin_De_Moray|Godwin]] breaks down sobbing.

5. **[[Ulrik]]'s Revelation** - Guard explains timeline: After [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] left, [[Godwin_De_Moray|Godwin]] focused on [[Arthur_De_Moray|Arthur]]. They investigated a cult and [[Arthur_De_Moray|Arthur]] returned "changed." [[Arthur_De_Moray|Arthur]] vanished. Mother left to become priestess of Tyr at [[House_of_Justice]].

6. **Sated Satyr Evening** - Party meets [[Percy]] ([[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]]'s hedonistic childhood friend) who proposes a heist on a rumored "necromancer." [[Clover]] speaks with [[Raenar_Neverember|Raenar]]'s dog [[Pogo]]. Party debates [[Field_of_Triumph]] and masquerade entry.

7. **Party Naming** - The group officially becomes "The [[The_Icebreakers]]" while discussing arena registration.

8. **Night Crisis** - [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] wakes to find corruption has spread halfway down all fingers. [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]]'s Identify and Detect Magic find nothing. [[Clover]]'s Lesser Restoration fails. Only [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] and [[Clover]] rush to temples while [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] and [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]] return to sleep.
