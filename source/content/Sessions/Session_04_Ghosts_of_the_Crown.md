# Session 4: Ghosts of the Crown

**Date:** [[2024-XX-XX]]
**Party Present:** [[Milo Swiftclock]], [[Sir Modred De Moray]], [[Jaspar Starshade]], Auren

## Quick Summary
> The party encountered seven ghosts in Neverwinter Castle who were the ancient king's guard, slain by the same cursed power Jaspar's sword carries. The ghosts offered a draining crown containing their spirits to aid against the cult. After visiting the House of Knowledge, Milo discovered a hidden room in his parents' home containing a spellbook and multi-generational research journal about the cult and the Luxon Beacon portal magic.

## Characters Met

### New NPCs
- **[[Seven Ghosts]]** - Ghosts - King's Guard. Protectors of Neverwinter's rulers across generations, killed by cult magic. "The sword's cursed power was what killed us before. It's dangerous."
- **[[Elira Moonshadow]]** - High Elf Female - Divination Wizard/Archivist. Milo's friend at the House of Knowledge. Warned about Blackthorn's strange behavior.
- **[[Master Tiberius Gearwhistle]]** - Gnome Male - Artificer/Wizard. Milo's former mentor who examined the cursed sword and revealed the Swiftclocks researched it.
- **[[Professor Thaddeus Blackthorn]]** - Human Male - Dunamancy Wizard. Acting suspiciously since the ice came, advocating for Sebastian.
- **[[Brost]]** - Human Male - Tavern Owner. Owns The Thirsty Goat, offered the party rooms.

### Returning NPCs
- **Injured Guard** - Transported back to safety with the other guards

## Places Visited

### [[Neverwinter Castle]] - Underground
**Ghost Chamber:**
- Found one ghost impaled by ice, in visible pain
- Seven ghosts appeared total - matching the seven graves below
- They speak in unison, having died at different times protecting kings killed by the cult
- The sword's cursed power is what killed them; they urge Jaspar to bury it
- Revealed cult camp location: forest north of Waterdeep (month's travel)
- Offered the Crown of the Guardians to aid the party

### [[House of Knowledge]]
**Main Hall:**
- Crowded with citizens, politician speaking about finding new ruler
- Elira found examining a frozen person, researching the phenomenon
- Gearwhistle and Blackthorn inside discussing solutions

**Private Room with Gearwhistle:**
- Examined the cursed sword
- Revealed Milo's parents should have told him about this
- Stated the Swiftclocks have researched this for generations
- Suggested Milo's parents "are probably bad people"
- Revealed the sword is connected to time travel magic

### [[Swiftclock Home]]
**Library (Downstairs):**
- Found book about teleportation from Milo's childhood memories

**Parents' Bedroom:**
- Found a magic ring in mother's bed
- Detected magic emanating from inside the closet
- Discovered hidden door activated by pulling a specific hanger

**Hidden Room:**
- Spellbook on a table
- Locked drawer (broken open by Auren) containing research journal
- Journal written across multiple generations in different handwriting
- Contains research on the slime creature and cult

### [[The Thirsty Goat]]
- Party reconvened to discuss findings
- Milo requested a room to study the spellbook
