# Session 28: The Moon's Gift

**Date:** [[2026-04-01]]
**Party Present:** [[Milo Swiftclock]], [[Sir Modred De Moray]], [[Jaspar Starshade]], [[Clover]]

## Quick Summary

> After a long rest in Waterdeep and a brief meeting with Lord Neverember in Neverwinter, the party set out on foot through Neverwinter Wood toward [[New Sharandar]] to find [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] and [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]]. They ambushed a cult scouting party stalled before a shimmering Mythal boundary — eleven cultists dead in two brutal rounds, with three eladrin dropping from the trees to finish what [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]]'s Fireball started. [[Pelendethas Everstride]] recognized [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] and escorted the party into New Sharandar, where [[Merrisara Winterwhite]] confirmed both [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] and [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]] had arrived days before — Arthur in quarantine after his shadow possession turned violent when he crossed the Mythal. That night, the crescent moon ceremony at the [[Temple of Leaves]] brought the party before [[Corellon Larethian]] himself, who gifted them the [[Moonlight Longbow]] and warned that the cult besieging the forest may be entering from the Feywild. As Corellon's warning reached its end, his face changed and he spoke his last words: "Oh no, they're here." The party dropped back into a burning temple — [[Cora]] sprinting out of the camp with [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] in her arms.

## Characters Met

### New NPCs

- **[[Pelendethas Everstride]]** - Eladrin Male - Military coordinator of New Sharandar. Golden hair, ornate armor, a circlet-like headpiece, and combat speed unlike anything the party had ever seen. Knew [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] from the Old Sharandar restoration year. Greeted him with mild exasperation before leading the party through the Mythal. Alive, at New Sharandar.
- **[[Merrisara Winterwhite]]** - Eladrin Female - Commander of New Sharandar. Red-haired, armored, perpetually bent over war maps charting cult encroachment. Barely looked up until she registered Milo's face, then softened. Confirmed Liora and Arthur's arrival, Arthur's violent crossing of the Mythal, and the deaths of one of her soldiers at his hands. Alive, at New Sharandar.
- **[[Emrae Firesky]]** - Eladrin Female - Priestess of [[Corellon Larethian]] at the [[Temple of Leaves]]. Golden robes, prophetic visions. Had foreseen the party's arrival on this crescent moon. Led the Lateu'quor ceremony. Alive, at New Sharandar (status unclear after the attack).
- **[[Thradisar Starwise]]** - Eladrin Male - Wizard-merchant and scholarly figure from [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]]'s year at Old Sharandar. Found drinking in a corner of the temple. Has been attempting to help [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]] — his magic failed as completely as Kyr Vicen's did. Offered to resume collaborative research with Milo on the shadow and the Mythal's interaction. Has magic items to sell. Alive, at New Sharandar.
- **[[Corellon Larethian]]** - God of Elves, Craftsmanship, and the Moon. Appeared to the party through the crescent moon and the golden tree during the Lateu'quor. Acknowledged the killing of a Prince of Tharizdun, warned the cult is entering Neverwinter Wood from the Feywild, directed the party toward the oak tree fey crossing, and gifted them the [[Moonlight Longbow]].

### Returning NPCs

- **[[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]]** - Reunited with [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] at New Sharandar. Confirmed she fled Neverwinter when [[Cora]] got too close and brought [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]] with her. Described Arthur turning on her in the forest before the eladrin subdued him. Had not been permitted to visit Arthur since. At session's end, [[Cora]] seized her and fled the burning camp with her.
- **[[Arthur De Moray]]** - Still held in a quarantine tent outside the Mythal. His shadow possession triggered violently on crossing the ward — he killed one eladrin soldier. Confirmed: eyes go black, shadow aura visible. Both [[Thradisar Starwise|Thradisar]] and Kyr Vicen's magic have failed on him. [[Modred]] has volunteered Arthur for further experimentation "as long as my brother survives."
- **[[Cora]]** - Appeared at the end of the session, leading the cult assault on New Sharandar. Grabbed [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] and sprinted out of the burning camp as the temple fell. Still alive. Still infuriating.

## Places Visited

### [[Neverwinter Wood]] — Forest Road, Ambush Clearing

**Cultist Ambush Site:**
- Eleven robed humanoids in the now-familiar eye-symbol robes, three prominent leaders among them, halted before the shimmering Mythal wave
- [[Clover]] Wild Shaped into a deer to scout; circled for a better angle and read their discussion — they had stopped because they did not understand what the wave of light was
- [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] identified the wave as a Mythal, confirming Fireball would function normally against targets outside the ward
- [[Clover]] cast Elemental Weapon (acid) on [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]]'s sword, then Wild Shaped into a Dire Wolf; combat opened with Milo's Fireball catching the clustered cultists
- Three eladrin dropped from the trees, whistling the signal, and executed the surviving burning cultists with precise dagger strikes — all eleven dead within two rounds
- [[Clover]] reverted from Dire Wolf form and cast Sleet Storm to douse the spreading forest fire

### [[New Sharandar]]

**The Eladrin Camp:**
- A 10-15 minute walk from the ambush site, deeper into the forest
- Treehouses woven into the living canopy, huts grown around living trunks, paths of packed earth between them
- A massive golden-leafed tree at the center forming a natural dome — the [[Temple of Leaves]]
- Protected by the Mythal; a quarantine tent for [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]] sits outside the ward's boundary
- A full crescent moon rising with a shadow already beginning to cross it

**The Mythal Tests:**
- [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] crossed the boundary: felt his certainty about magic and his father's lessons begin to unravel — not pain, but doubt, as if spells were slipping out of reach. He stepped back and the feeling vanished
- [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]] pushed his corruption battery through the veil: the purple substance reacted violently, trying to break out, his whole arm went hot and heavy. He yanked it back
- [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]], marked by the Mythal during his Sharandar year, passed through with no ill effect
- [[Pelendethas Everstride|Pelendethas]] confirmed: crossing the Mythal while possessed or carrying certain substances produces dangerous reactions — this is why Arthur is quarantined outside it

### [[Temple of Leaves]]

**The Lateu'quor — Forest Communion of the Crescent Moon:**
- The massive golden-leafed tree at the center of New Sharandar, its branches forming an open-roofed canopy over the ceremony space
- A stone statue of [[Corellon Larethian]] with a bow stands at the tree's base; the leaves wrap around the statue
- [[Emrae Firesky]] explained that each attendee must bring a gift; [[Clover]] crafted leaf-wrapped offerings of forest berries, useful plants, and fibers for the whole party, [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] adding calligraphy with words like "family" and "friendship"
- [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] rolled a nat 1 on his attempt to help, destroying [[Clover]]'s work; his solo attempt produced a clump of dirt with a gold coin pressed on top; an elven woman passing by laughed; [[Clover]] made him a proper replacement and pocketed the coin
- The party laid their gifts at the base of the tree alongside dozens of eladrin offerings; eladrin hummed and sang; each person received a bowl of red elven wine ([[Modred]] immediately asked for a second)
- The moonlight bathed the tree; the golden leaves wrapped around the party and lifted them upward into a sky-space above the moon

**Corellon's Vision:**
- [[Corellon Larethian]] spoke in a calm, unhurried voice from the sky above the moon
- Acknowledged the party's gift and their killing of one of Tharizdun's Princes
- Warned he could no longer feel the remaining three Princes anywhere in this land
- Suspected the cult besieging the forest was entering from the Feywild itself — something neither he nor the eladrin could explain
- The moon melted liquid through the sky and formed into the [[Moonlight Longbow]], which floated down to the party
- Directed them to an oak tree in the forest — a known fey crossing — and warned the Feywild will change them, scare them, and make them different; they should not give their names to any fey who asks
- His final words, as smoke rose and stars began turning to ash: "Oh no, they're here."

**The Attack:**
- The party dropped back to the [[Temple of Leaves]] to find the great golden tree in flames
- Eladrin screaming; dead eladrin on the ground before them
- A wave of cultists closing in
- [[Cora]] at the far end of the clearing, seizing [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] and sprinting out of the camp
- The [[Moonlight Longbow]] now in [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]]'s hands as the session ended

## Revelations & Lore

- The shimmering waves of light the party has seen before — around [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]] after his battery experiment, and now around [[New Sharandar]] — are Mythals: eladrin-crafted magical boundaries that alter how magic functions inside them
- The Mythal reacts violently to Tharizdun-corrupted substances; [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]]'s battery nearly burst; [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]]'s shadow possession went feral when he crossed the ward, resulting in the death of an eladrin soldier
- The Mythal can also induce intellectual doubt in those who carry magical certainty — [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] felt his confidence in his training and his father's teachings dissolve when he stepped through
- [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] is marked by the Mythal from his year at Old Sharandar and can cross it freely
- [[Corellon Larethian]] can no longer sense any of the three remaining Princes of Tharizdun anywhere in this land
- [[Corellon Larethian]] suspects the Tharizdun cult besieging Neverwinter Wood is entering from the Feywild itself — something even he does not fully understand
- There is an oak tree in Neverwinter Wood that is a fey crossing to the Feywild; [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] knew of it from his Sharandar days but had never crossed
- In the Feywild, you do not give a fey your real name; a false name you declare aloud becomes your name to them regardless (per [[Clover]])
- [[Emrae Firesky]] receives rare prophetic visions; she foresaw the party's arrival on this crescent moon and the image of chains, darkness, and a bow creating light
- Eladrin do not mine; their fine crystals and metalworking materials come from the Feywild
