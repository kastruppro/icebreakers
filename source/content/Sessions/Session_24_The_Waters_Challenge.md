# Session 24: The Water's Challenge

**Date:** [[2026-02-25]]
**Party Present:** [[Milo Swiftclock]], [[Sir Modred De Moray]], [[Jaspar Starshade]] ([[Clover]] absent)

## Quick Summary
> Picking up from Session 23's cliffhanger, The Icebreakers fought their way free of the tentacles in the blood-filled Fountain Room, then conquered two more challenge rooms in The Depths: an Illusion Room where Milo's Detect Magic exposed fake water spheres, and a Brazier Room where Modred climbed the walls naked with daggers to find hidden ceiling mechanisms. With all three pillars lit, a Water Elemental rose from the vortex and a possessed [[Lord Neverember]] emerged, corrupted by a [[Tharizdun]]-connected entity. The entity taunted the party and tried to recruit [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]], but he refused. When Modred showed Neverember the [[Crown of the Guardians]], the lord briefly broke free and cried "Run!" before the entity seized control again. The session ended on a cliffhanger as the entity called for initiative, with the boss fight postponed for [[Clover]]'s player.

## Characters Met

### New NPCs
- None this session

### Returning NPCs
- **[[Lord Neverember]]** - Found in [[The Depths]], possessed by a [[Tharizdun]]-connected entity. Red/purple corruption goo emanates from him. Necromancy magic detected. Briefly broke free when shown the [[Crown of the Guardians]] and said "Run!" before the entity reclaimed control.
- **[[Water Sphere Entity]]** - Continued controlling The Depths' three challenge rooms. Created illusions of Lord Neverember as a fake-out, divided into multiple copies. Defeated in each room's puzzle. After all three rooms were completed, summoned a Water Elemental and revealed the possessed Lord Neverember.

## Places Visited

### [[The Depths]]
**Fountain Room (completed):**
- Tentacles emerged from the floor drains and grappled [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] and [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]], pulling them underwater
- [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] fought from his barrel raft, pulling Milo to safety
- Milo used Chronoshift to re-roll a failed acrobatics check, then landed Booming Blade on a tentacle for devastating damage
- Jaspar freed himself with a solid firebolt hit and swam to the surface
- The blood-water rose to the ceiling door; the party was sucked through a water-slide tube back to the Water Sphere Chamber
- First stone pillar lit up upon completion

**Illusion Room (center/straight path, completed):**
- An illusion of [[Lord Neverember]] floated in the center of an empty room
- Milo's Detect Magic immediately identified it as illusion magic; [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] confirmed by throwing a ball bearing through the image
- The [[Water Sphere Entity]] revealed itself and split into many small copies (one real, the rest illusions)
- Hitting illusion spheres caused 4 acid damage
- Milo used Detect Magic to identify the real spheres; the party destroyed them across three rounds of escalating difficulty
- Milo's Magic Missile forced concentration checks that broke the illusion spell
- Second stone pillar lit up upon completion

**Brazier Room (last/left path, completed):**
- Five braziers in the center kept relighting, but rain from the ceiling constantly extinguished them
- [[Jaspar Starshade|Jaspar]] first covered a brazier with his cloak as proof of concept
- [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]] stripped completely naked, took two daggers, and climbed the slippery walls to find a hidden brick button that controlled sliding wooden planks on ceiling rail tracks
- Jaspar found a second hidden button on the opposite wall
- Milo used Mage Hand to find and press a third button
- The sliding panels covered four braziers from the rain; Modred's cloak covered the fifth
- Third stone pillar lit up upon completion

**Water Sphere Chamber (final area):**
- All three stone pillars lit up, creating a massive water vortex spinning between them
- A Water Elemental was summoned from the vortex
- Possessed [[Lord Neverember]] emerged from within the Water Elemental, emanating red/purple corruption goo
- The entity addressed the absent [[Clover]] about his parents and tried to recruit [[Sir Modred De Moray|Modred]]
- Boss fight about to begin as the session ended

## Revelations & Lore

- **Lord Neverember is possessed:** Found in The Depths, controlled by an entity connected to [[Tharizdun]]. Necromancy magic is involved in the possession, detected by Milo's Detect Magic.
- **Neverember is conscious inside:** The [[Crown of the Guardians]] briefly broke through the possession, allowing him to speak in his own voice ("Run!"). He is still alive and fighting the control.
- **The entity's layered voice:** A second presence is audible beneath Neverember's normal speech, confirming something else is speaking through him.
- **Clover's connection:** The entity claims Clover unwittingly brought "a prince of Tharizdun" to the cult, connecting to the book found in Session 23. This was described as "so easy."
- **"The Maw":** The entity referred to Tharizdun as "the Maw" and "the almighty" - a new title for the chained god.
- **Clover's parents killed deliberately:** The entity confirmed their deaths were intentional: "They were just in the way." This was not collateral damage.
- **The Depths' structure:** Three challenge rooms must be completed to progress. Each room returns a water sphere to one of three stone pillars. All three lit pillars trigger the final confrontation.
- **Illusion sphere acid:** Attacking fake water spheres in the Illusion Room causes 4 acid damage.
