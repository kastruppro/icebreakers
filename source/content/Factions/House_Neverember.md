# House Neverember

## Overview
The ruling house of [[Neverwinter]], currently in crisis with the lord imprisoned by Tharizdun cultists. The party is allied with this house and working to rescue Lord Neverember.

## Members

| Name | Role | Status |
|------|------|--------|
| [[Lord Neverember]] | Ruler of Neverwinter | Imprisoned |
| [[Raenar Neverember]] | Son, hunter | Alive, allied with party |
| Loyal Guard | Personal guard | Alive (injured, lost arm) |

## History with the Party

### Early Encounters (Sessions 3-4)
The party first encountered Lord Neverember during the Neverwinter lockdown when the city was frozen. His loyal guard cut off his own arm to protect the lord from cultist attacks.

### The Seven Ghosts (Session 4)
In Neverwinter Castle, the party discovered the [[Crown_of_the_Guardians|Crown of the Guardians]] - containing spirits of seven royal guards who protected Neverember's predecessors across generations.

### Discovery of Imprisonment (Session 21)
During the infiltration of [[House_Castellers]], the party discovered Lord Neverember chained in the basement in a summoning circle, with 8-10 cultists performing a ritual. He was teleported away before rescue.

### Rescue Mission (Session 22+)
[[Raenar Neverember]] has joined forces with the party to rescue his father. The mission to save Lord Neverember is currently active.
