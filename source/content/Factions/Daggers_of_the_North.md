# Daggers of the North

## Overview
An adventuring party investigating cult activity, led by the elven paladin [[Morguna]]. They wear distinctive salt-symbol patches marking their connection to [[Daggerford]].

## Members

| Name | Role | Status |
|------|------|--------|
| [[Morguna]] | Leader, Paladin | Alive |
| [[Leena]] | Member | Alive |
| [[Merin]] | Scout/Lookout | Alive |
| [[Rafael]] | Former member | Deceased |

## What They Know
- The Castellers are connected to the cult
- Cult ruins exist in the marsh south of Daggerford
- Rafael had nightmares about chains and demons before his death
- They possess a vial of "The Substance" (corruption material)

## History with Party
- **Session 17:** First encountered The Icebreakers
- **Session 19:** Suggested visiting [[Temple of Desna]] for help with Thistle
- **Session 19-20:** Shared intelligence about marsh ruins; showed corruption substance
- **Session 20:** Positioned Merin as lookout at House Castellers

## Notes
- Morguna is considered "too trusting" by Leena
- They take a collaborative approach to fighting the cult threat
- Currently operating in [[Waterdeep]]

## Connections
- [[Temple of Desna]] - Recommended to the party
- [[The Chained]] - Their enemy, target of investigation
- [[House Castellers]] - Under surveillance
