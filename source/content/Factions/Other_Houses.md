## House Adabrent

**Location:** [[Waterdeep]]
**Status:** Active
**Relationship:** Friendly (to Jaspar)

A wealthy noble house that controls the Waterdeep docks.

### Members
- **Isabella Adabrent** - Noblewoman with romantic interest in [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]]; sent him pepper nuts and a pink perfumed note

### Notes
- Professional dock guards denied party entry
- One guard ate a pepper nut and farted snow while turning to stone
- Family appears to have significant wealth and influence in Waterdeep
