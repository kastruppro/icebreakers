# House De Moray

## Overview
A noble house in [[Waterdeep]] and the family of party member [[Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]]. The house has fallen into dysfunction following the madness of its patriarch and the departure of key family members.

## Members

| Name | Role | Status |
|------|------|--------|
| [[Godwin De Moray]] | Lord, patriarch | Alive (mad) |
| [[Morgane]] | Former Lady, Modred's mother | Left family, now Priestess of [[Tyr]] |
| [[Modred_De_Moray\|Sir Modred De Moray]] | Son, knight | Adventuring (party member) |
| [[Arthur De Moray]] | Younger son | Alive (possessed/recovering) |
| Ulrik | Guard | Alive |

## Family Drama
- Godwin descended into madness and alcoholism
- Morgane left the family years ago to serve Tyr
- Modred left to become a knight and distance himself from his father
- Arthur became possessed by a shadow entity connected to Tharizdun

## Key Events

### Arthur's Possession
Arthur was found frozen with a cult necklace in early sessions. A shadow entity had possessed him, which the party battled in the Chapel of Oghma (Session 10). Arthur was left with the [[Swiftclock_Home|Swiftclock family]] in Neverwinter for protection.

### Godwin's Madness
- Attacked the party with Wall of Fire spell
- Later appeared sober at the Temple of Tyr asking for Modred
- His mental state remains unstable

### Morgane's Path
Modred's mother left the dysfunctional household to become a priestess of Tyr. She now serves at the [[House_of_Justice|Temple of Tyr]] in Waterdeep and has aided the party with divine quests.

## Current Status
- House is in decline due to Godwin's madness
- Modred has distanced himself from family affairs
- Arthur is recovering from possession
- Morgane serves Tyr and supports the party
