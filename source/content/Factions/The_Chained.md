# The Chained (Cult of Tharizdun)

## Overview
A secretive cult dedicated to freeing [[Tharizdun]], the Chained One - a Betrayer God so dangerous that all other gods united to imprison him. The cult has infiltrated noble houses and operates through triangular portals.

## Members

| Name | Role | Status |
|------|------|--------|
| [[Cora]] | Field Agent | Alive, at large |
| [[Sebastian]] | Cloak Tower Leader | Alive, corrupted |
| [[Victor Castellers]] | Noble Conspirator | Claimed by Tharizdun |
| Unknown cultists | Foot soldiers | Various |

## Goals
- Free Tharizdun from his eight divine chains
- Spread corruption through "The Substance"
- Perform binding rituals requiring strong spellcasters
- Acquire powerful artifacts and hosts

## Symbols & Methods
- **Triangular Portals:** Black triangular portals used for escape and travel
- **Corruption:** Black veins spreading through hands/body (Sebastian fully corrupted, Modred partially)
- **The Substance:** A corruption material collected in vials
- **Chains:** Imagery of chains appears in their rituals and Tharizdun's binding

## The Four Princes
Ammalia mentioned Victor made a deal with "the four princes" of Tharizdun. Their identities and nature remain unknown.

## Gift of Faisten
Cora recognized Modred's corruption as the "gift of Faisten" - the source and meaning of this term is unknown.

## Known Locations
- [[House Castellers]] basement - ritual chamber with [[Lord Neverember]] imprisoned
- Marsh ruins south of [[Daggerford]] - one of four Tharizdun temples
- Three other ruins around [[Waterdeep]] (locations unknown)

## Connections
- [[Tharizdun]] - The god they worship
- [[Tyr]] - Opposes them; his gauntlets can destroy Tharizdun's chains
- [[Desna]] - Opposes them; provided Dream Staff to counter their curse
