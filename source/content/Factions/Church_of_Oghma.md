# Church of Oghma

## Overview
The organized worship of [[Oghma]], God of Knowledge, Bards, and Inspiration. Their temple, the House of Knowledge, is located in [[Neverwinter]] and serves as a repository of wisdom and magical knowledge.

## House of Knowledge
A temple-library in Neverwinter that serves as the center of Oghma's worship. Contains:
- Vast archives of knowledge
- Magical research facilities
- Scholars and priests dedicated to preserving wisdom

## Divine Intervention
In Session 10, when Milo confronted Oghma's statue in anger, the god answered directly:
- Transported Milo to a pure white throne room
- Explained celestial rules forbid direct intervention
- Despite this, granted Milo the **Fey Touched** feat
- Confirmed the party's plan to travel to Waterdeep was correct
