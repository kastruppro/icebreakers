# Ulrik

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Guard at House De Moray
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Waterdeep]] (House De Moray)

## Description
A loyal guard who has served House De Moray long enough to remember Modred before he left home. Recognized Modred immediately upon his return.

## Personality
Loyal to the De Moray family. Willing to share information with Modred about what happened to his family. Sympathetic to the family's tragedy.

## Key Quotes
> "Oh come on, don't you see who it is? This is the son of Godwin." - Recognizing Modred, Session 12
> "He had lost both of his sons." - Explaining Godwin's descent into madness

## History with Party
- **Session 12**: Recognized Modred at the gates of House De Moray. After Godwin's violent outburst, explained the timeline of events to the party - how Godwin and Arthur investigated the cult, Arthur returned changed, and eventually vanished.

## Information Provided
Timeline of De Moray family tragedy:
1. After Modred left, Godwin focused entirely on Arthur
2. Father and Arthur investigated cult rumors together
3. Found cult performing ritual in nearby forest
4. Arthur returned "changed" - aggressive, not himself
5. Godwin tried everything: mages, healers, searching for cures
6. Arthur vanished one day without trace
7. Godwin searched desperately, found nothing
8. Spiraled into depression
9. Modred's mother left and joined House of Justice as priestess of Tyr
