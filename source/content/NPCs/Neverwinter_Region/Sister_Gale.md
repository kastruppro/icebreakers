# Sister Gale

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Half-Elf Female
**Role:** Cleric of Tymora at Shrine of Luck
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Phandalin]] (Shrine of Luck)

## Description
A half-elven cleric who maintains the Shrine of Luck in Phandalin, a 30-foot statue of Tymora with a crown. She serves the Goddess of Luck faithfully.

## Personality
Devout, compassionate, and humble. Wishes she could do more to help but trusts in Tymora's guidance.

## History with Party
- **Session 1**: Met at Toblen's tavern after the dragon attack. Attempted to dispel the ice magic on frozen townsfolk but failed - her divine magic couldn't help.
- **Session 2**: Provided 30 goodberries and a minor healing potion at the Shrine of Luck. Auren comforted her: "Tymora has done all she can through you, Sister."

## Key Quotes
> "Tymora has done all she can through you, Sister." - Auren, comforting Sister Gale

- [[Toblen]] - Fellow Phandalin resident
