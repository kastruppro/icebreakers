# Emrae Firesky

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Eladrin Female
**Role:** Priestess of Corellon Larethian, Temple of Leaves
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[New Sharandar]] (Temple of Leaves, assuming survived the attack)

## Description
Eladrin woman in golden robes, the presiding priestess at the Temple of Leaves -- the massive golden-leafed tree at the heart of New Sharandar. She has led the monthly Lateu'quor ceremony for years, including during Milo's time at Old Sharandar. She receives rare prophetic visions from Corellon, distinct from the everyday religious experience of her congregation.

## Personality
Warm and unhurried in contrast to Merrisara's urgency. The kind of person who greets you by name and asks a question before she says anything else. Her prophetic gift doesn't make her grandiose -- she delivers the vision matter-of-factly and then tells people where to sit.

## Key Quotes
> "Days ago I had a vision -- a party would arrive on this crescent moon. A party whose fight Corellon wanted to aid. I saw chains, darkness, and a bow creating light." - Session 28, describing her vision to the party

## History with Party
- **Backstory (Milo)**: Led the Lateu'quor ceremony during Milo's year at the Old Sharandar restoration. Invited him to attend as a guest. The ceremony -- watching eladrin shift between seasonal aspects as they sang -- left a lasting impression on Milo, who follows Oghma rather than Corellon.
- **Session 28**: Greeted Milo warmly at New Sharandar and told him of her vision predicting the party's arrival. Explained that eladrin use crystals and Feywild materials in their metalwork (not mined ore) when Jaspar asked about the smiths. Directed the party to sit by the central tree and the statue of Corellon when they were ready for the ceremony -- which led directly to Corellon's appearance and the gift of the Moonlight Longbow.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Her prophetic visions are rare and she takes them seriously; the fact that this one specifically named "a bow creating light" proved literally accurate
- Unknown whether she survived the attack on the temple at session end; the building was on fire when the party returned from Corellon's vision
