# Merrisara Winterwhite

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Eladrin Female
**Role:** Commander of New Sharandar
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[New Sharandar]]

## Description
Red-haired eladrin in armor, sword always at her side. When the party first saw her she was bent over maps of cult activity. She barely looked up when Pelendethas announced "Outsiders" -- a quick scan of the group -- until she clocked Milo, and her manner softened immediately. Commands the whole New Sharandar operation: military, research access, quarantine decisions, and the eladrin metalsmith teams.

## Personality
Blunt and time-pressured. Her default is short sentences and half-attention while doing three things at once. People she trusts or recognizes get the full version of her. She approved Milo's access levels during his Sharandar year without ever explaining why; that same quiet respect carried into Session 28. She sets firm limits -- "if it goes wrong, we're leaving" -- but within those limits is willing to help.

## Key Quotes
> "I don't have time for this. I have other things to care about." - Session 28, before recognizing Milo
> "Oh, Milo, what are you doing here?" - Session 28, softening upon seeing him

## History with Party
- **Backstory (Milo)**: Commanded the Old Sharandar restoration. Milo didn't deal with her often but she approved his access levels and once watched him work on a stasis-field around a cache of old Iliyanbruen blades without saying a word.
- **Session 28**: Met the party when Pelendethas brought them in. Confirmed [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] and Arthur had arrived days ago. Explained that Arthur had killed one of her soldiers when he first crossed the Mythal and was being held in a quarantine tent outside the veil. When Jaspar showed her his containment battery, she had a guard draw his weapon and asked what it was; accepted the explanation but drew a firm line. Offered to connect Jaspar with eladrin metalsmiths. Noted the party's connection to Tharizdun's Princes and invited them to the night's Lateu'quor ceremony.
- **Session 29**: Personally arrived at the Feywild portal fight with one of her soldiers and two extra bows for eladrin archers. Held the line long enough for the party to clear the last cultists; nodded with visible respect at Modred after his Action Surge kill of the "blue" cultist. Shouted something unheard to the party as they ran through the portal tree after Cora. Status: alive, last seen fighting at the Feywild portal outside the ruins of [[New_Sharandar|New Sharandar]].

## Secrets/Motivations
- Cultists are probing the perimeter harder every day and she doesn't know why -- the escalation is visibly alarming her
- Pre-session had planned to ask the party to help investigate the cult activity; the attack on the temple may have overtaken that plan entirely
