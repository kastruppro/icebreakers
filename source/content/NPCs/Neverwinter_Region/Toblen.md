# Toblen

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Mayor of Phandalin, Innkeeper
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Phandalin]] (Stonehill Inn)

## Description
The human mayor of Phandalin who runs the Stonehill Inn. A helpful and hospitable man who has provided lodging and support to the party throughout their early adventures.

## Personality
Grateful, hospitable, and community-minded. Works tirelessly to help his town through the crisis of the ice plague.

## History with Party
- **Session 1**: Met the party when they arrived in Phandalin during the dragon attack. Offered lodging at his inn. His friend had seen similar freezing at Mount Hotenow.
- **Session 2**: Provided breakfast and continued lodging, grateful for the party's help.
- **Session 8**: Greeted the party upon their return from Icespire Peak.
- **Session 9**: Served breakfast as the party prepared to investigate Cora.
