## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male

**Current Location:** [[Neverwinter]] (House of Knowledge)

## Description
A wizard specializing in Dunamancy who works at the House of Knowledge in Neverwinter. Since the ice dragon's arrival, his behavior has become noticeably different - more hectic, more impatient, and politically motivated.

## Personality
Before the ice: Unknown previous demeanor
After the ice: Hectic, impatient, dismissive of those he considers unimportant. Pushy about seeing magical artifacts. Politically active in advocating for Sebastian's leadership.

## Key Quotes
> "Milo is not important." - Session 4, dismissing Milo when the party wouldn't show him the sword

## History with Party
- Session 4: First encountered at House of Knowledge; warned about by Elira; tried to convince party to show him the sword; dismissed Milo as "not important" when refused
- Sessions 4-9: Continued advocacy for Sebastian

## Suspicious Behavior
- Changed personality since the ice dragon came
- Actively advocates for Sebastian to become Neverwinter's leader
- Attempted to get the party to show him the cursed sword
- Dismissive of Milo specifically
- Elira warned the party about his strange behavior
