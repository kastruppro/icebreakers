# Thradisar Starwise

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Eladrin Male
**Role:** Wizard-Merchant, Scholarly Leader
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[New Sharandar]]

## Description
Eladrin wizard-merchant who ran the scholarly side of the Old Sharandar restoration -- Milo's direct supervisor during his year working the Hidden Vaults. Sharp and unsentimental; he clocked the practical value of Milo's Luxon fragment quickly and put it to work. Found drinking in a corner of the Temple of Leaves during the Lateu'quor ceremony when Jaspar encountered him, which suggests his relationship with religious ritual is comfortable but relaxed.

## Personality
Pragmatic and direct. Didn't care about enthusiasm during the restoration, only results. Warm in his own way -- he greeted Milo warmly in Session 28 -- but always pivots to the problem at hand. Modred's casual mention that they had "killed around 30 people in the past two weeks" didn't faze him. He has things to sell and things to study.

## Key Quotes
> (To Milo, after Modred mentioned their kill count) Unruffled -- moved straight on to business.
> "We killed around 30 people. In the past 2 weeks." - Modred, to Thradisar, Session 28 (Thradisar's response was to continue the conversation without blinking)

## History with Party
- **Backstory (Milo)**: Ran the scholarly side of the Old Sharandar restoration. Supervised Milo's work in the Hidden Vaults. Recognized the Luxon fragment's value immediately and oriented Milo's work around it. Milo learned to trust the fragment's resonance under Thradisar's supervision, which is foundational to how his chronomancy developed.
- **Session 28**: Found at the Temple of Leaves during the Lateu'quor ceremony. Told Milo he had been trying to help Arthur De Moray but his magic had failed on the possessed man -- the same result as Kyr Vicen. Offered to collaborate again on studying the shadow and the Mythal's interaction with it. Mentioned he had magical items to sell the following day. Modred volunteered Arthur for the experiments.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Wants to understand the Tharizdun shadow and how it interacts with the Mythal -- this is genuine scholarly interest as much as it is helping the party
- Has magical items in stock; what they are is unknown until the party meets with him
