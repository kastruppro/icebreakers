# Pelendethas Everstride

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Eladrin Male
**Role:** Military Coordinator of New Sharandar
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[New Sharandar]]

## Description
Golden-haired eladrin warrior in full armor, distinguishable by a circlet-like ornament he wears. Moves with extraordinary speed in combat -- the party noted he was "faster than anyone they had ever seen" when he closed on a cultist and slashed him down. One of three eladrin who dropped from the trees during the ambush, whistling a coordinated signal before executing three burning cultists with precise dagger stabs.

## Personality
Exasperated but professionally warm with Milo. Direct and duty-focused. Not given to small talk when the forest is dangerous. His first instinct upon recognizing Milo was to tell him to leave, which says everything about how seriously he takes the threat assessment around New Sharandar.

## Key Quotes
> "Milo, what are you doing here? These forests are not safe. You need to turn back, all of you." - Session 28, upon recognizing Milo after dropping from the trees

> "Outsiders." - Session 28, introducing the party to Merrisara Winterwhite

## History with Party
- **Backstory (Milo)**: Served as military coordinator during Milo's year at Old Sharandar. Did not care about Milo's research directly, but valued having someone who could identify which wards were active and which were safe, especially near the Mended Grove.
- **Session 28**: Dropped from a tree during the cultist ambush, helped kill three cultists, then escorted the party into New Sharandar. Explained the Mythal to the group and introduced them to [[Merrisara_Winterwhite|Merrisara Winterwhite]].

## Secrets/Motivations
- Entirely focused on the security of New Sharandar; the cultists probing the perimeter have clearly been escalating and he is running a serious military operation
- May know more about the cult's Feywild entry point than he's shared
