# Corellon Larethian

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Deity (Male presentation)
**Role:** God of Elves, Craftsmanship, and the Moon
**Status:** Active Deity
**Current Location:** Divine Realm (accessible through the Lateu'quor ceremony)

## Description
The creator god of the elves and eladrin. Appeared to the party during the Lateu'quor ceremony at the Temple of Leaves: the golden tree's leaves wrapped around the party and lifted them into a sky-space where an elven face formed in the moon itself. His voice was described as calming. He manifested the Moonlight Longbow by melting the moon itself into liquid that descended and solidified into the weapon.

## Personality
Measured and grateful. He thanks people before he warns them. His warnings are honest rather than comforting -- the Feywild "might change you, it might scare you, it might make you different" -- but his overall manner is that of someone who believes the people he is speaking to are capable of handling the truth. His last recorded words before the vision collapsed were not a call to battle but a quiet expression of hope: "You're not the first of your kind trying to do this. I hope you will be the last."

## Key Quotes
> "Travelers, I thank you all for the gifts that you bring with you on this night. And I also congratulate you on killing one of the Princes of Tharizdun." - Session 28

> "It might change you. It might scare you. It might make you different." - Session 28, warning the party about the Feywild

> "Oh no, they're here." - Session 28, final words before the vision collapsed as the Temple burned

## History with Party
- **Backstory (Milo)**: The Lateu'quor ceremony was held during Milo's year at Old Sharandar; Emrae Firesky invited him to attend as a guest. Milo, who follows Oghma, described it as the closest he had come to understanding what the Feywild actually is. The party had never encountered Corellon directly before Session 28.
- **Session 28**: Pulled the party into a sky-vision through the golden tree and the crescent moon during the Lateu'quor ceremony. Confirmed the party had killed one of Tharizdun's Princes. Warned that he cannot feel any remaining Princes in this land, and suspects the cult besieging New Sharandar is entering from the Feywild itself. Directed the party to an oak tree fey crossing in Neverwinter Wood as their next step. Gifted the [[Moonlight_Longbow|Moonlight Longbow]] to the party (it was given to Jaspar). The vision was cut short when the attack on the temple began.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Knows the cult's Feywild infiltration is happening but does not understand the mechanism -- something neither he nor the eladrin can account for
- Cannot currently locate the three remaining Princes of Tharizdun in this land
- The Moonlight Longbow was not a casual gift; Emrae Firesky's vision specifically foresaw "a bow creating light" -- this was planned
