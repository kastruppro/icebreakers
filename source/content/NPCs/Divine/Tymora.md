# Tymora

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Goddess
**Role:** Goddess of Luck and Fortune
**Status:** Divine
**Domain:** Luck, Fortune, Good Fortune

## Description
Tymora is the goddess of luck, known also as Lady Luck. She is worshipped throughout the Sword Coast, with her followers seeking good fortune in their endeavors.

## Worship
- **Shrine of Luck** in [[Phandalin]] - A 30-foot statue with a crown, tended by [[Sister_Gale|Sister Gale]]
- Followers recognize each other by their faith symbols

## Connection to Campaign
[[Sister_Gale|Sister Gale]] serves Tymora at the Shrine of Luck in Phandalin. She attempted to use Tymora's divine magic to dispel the ice plague but was unsuccessful.

## Connections
- [[Sister_Gale|Sister Gale]] - Cleric who serves her
- [[Phandalin]] - Location of her shrine
