# The Father

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Unknown entity (presents as male divine being)
**Role:** Claims to be [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar's]] creator
**Status:** Unknown
**Current Location:** [[The Void]] (appeared near [[Tharizdun]]'s cyst)

## Description
An entity that communicates with Jaspar through prophetic dreams and Void encounters. Has appeared as a divine being but its true nature is unconfirmed. Appeared in close proximity to Tharizdun's cyst (his "shell"), which raises questions about its true allegiance.

## Key Quotes

> "Jaspar my child, soon we will conquer the world." - Session 5 (prophetic dream)

> "You were so little when I found you. You might not remember, but this is where it all started." - Session 7 (first Void encounter)

> "It's gonna be okay, it's gonna be okay Jaspar." - Jaspar's mother in the Session 7 vision (shown by the Father)

> "Don't trust Cora - she'll use shadow to destroy everything." - Session 11 (second Void encounter, gave Jaspar power)

## History with Party

- **Session 4:** Jaspar had his first prophetic nightmare -- falling, burning, burning fields. Possibly the Father's first contact.
- **Session 5:** Voice in a prophetic dream with fire, divine wings, and Selune. Spoke directly: "Soon we will conquer the world."
- **Session 7:** First direct Void encounter. Jaspar saw a familiar house in Neverwinter with a crib and a sweetly laughing child. A woman entered, picked up the baby, and sang "It's gonna be okay, it's gonna be okay Jaspar" — this was Jaspar's mother. The Father emerged from a portal with spreading wings. He said he met Jaspar's mother early and let Jaspar find his own way. Claimed to be Jaspar's **creator**, revealed Jaspar is an **Aasimar** made "in his image," and said together they could "end hunger" and "save the world." He would teach Jaspar everything if he followed. Jaspar said he wanted to control his own power first. The entity offered time to think. As Jaspar walked away, he felt a hug, then appeared on the mountaintop.
- **Session 11:** Second Void encounter in a dark throne room near Tharizdun's floating cyst. Gave Jaspar a **sample of power** (warm orange-red energy). Warned about Cora using shadow to destroy everything.

## Claims Made

1. Created Jaspar (is his "father")
2. Jaspar is an Aasimar, made in the Father's image
3. Jaspar was created specifically to save the world
4. Found Jaspar when he was very small
5. Met Jaspar's mother early and let Jaspar find his own way
6. Together they could "end hunger"
7. Would teach Jaspar everything if he followed
8. Cora will use shadow to destroy everything

## Related: Desna's Confirmation (Session 19)

The goddess [[Desna]] (Goddess of Dreams) read Jaspar's dreams and confirmed they are **prophetic** and exist in a crossover between the dream realm and something else. Even she cannot affect them -- they are **immune to divine interference**. This suggests the Father operates outside normal celestial/divine mechanisms.
