# Savan

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Mage, Owner of Sevarna's Magic Shop
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Waterdeep]] (near Raenar's house)

## Description
A human mage who owns and operates Sevarna's Magic Shop in Waterdeep, located near Raenar Neverember's residence.

## Personality
Dismissive of non-magical technology and inventions. Argues for the superiority of magic over devices. Respects competent magic users.

## Key Quotes
> "You look very competent." - To Milo, Session 12
> "We can just have mages learn devices instead of using this little ball." - Dismissing Gigz's inventions
> "If you're a good mage, then magic always works." - On magic superiority

## History with Party
- **Session 12**: Met party at Statue Protectors Square. Debated with Gigz about magic versus technology. Complimented Milo's competence. Mentioned diamond dust is rare and must be found in the wild or at Sophie's jewelry shop.
- **Session 14**: Party visited shop, sold items to Milo.

## Services
- Magic shop near Raenar's house
- Knowledge about rare materials (diamond dust sources)
- Potential source for magical items and components

- [[Gigz]] - Rival philosophically (magic vs. technology debate)
- [[Milo Swiftclock]] - Recognizes his magical competence
