# Erik (Waterdeep)

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Dwarf Male
**Role:** Gem Crafter, Conduit Magic Expert
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Waterdeep]] (Southern District)

## Description
A dwarven gem crafter in Waterdeep who specializes in conduit magic - the art of channeling magic through gems and items. Thoughtful and knowledgeable about magical theory.

## Personality
Scholarly, patient, and intrigued by innovative magical concepts.

## History with Party
- **Session 14**: Met Jaspar at his gem shop. Explained conduit magic and was fascinated by Jaspar's revolutionary concept of using himself as a conduit rather than items. Accepted Jaspar as an apprentice, with training to begin after the masquerade. Holds a necromantic gem worth 300 gold.

## Key Quotes
> "If you present the magic with the opposite of what it already is, then perhaps the gem will change its very nature." - Explaining conduit magic
> "I have never heard someone ask about this before." - On Jaspar's self-conduit concept

## Services
- Gem crafting
- Conduit magic expertise
- Magical item appraisal
