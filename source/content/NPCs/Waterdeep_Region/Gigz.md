# Gigz

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Gnome Male
**Role:** Inventor, Owner of Gigz Gears
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Waterdeep]] (Southern District)

## Description
A gnomish inventor who runs Gigz Gears, a shop specializing in experimental mechanical devices in southern Waterdeep. His workshop is filled with contraptions, gizmos, and the occasional cloud of smoke from his latest experiments.

## Personality
Enthusiastic about his craft and dismissive of over-reliance on magic. Gigz believes in the reliability of well-crafted mechanical devices over arcane solutions. He's helpful to customers and willing to share information, making him a valuable contact in Waterdeep. His inventive mind is always working on new creations.

## Key Quotes
> "When magic doesn't work, then the device would still work!" - Gigz's philosophy on the superiority of mechanical solutions
