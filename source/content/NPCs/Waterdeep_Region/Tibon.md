# Tibon

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Owner/Bartender of the Sated Satyr Tavern
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Sated_Satyr_Inn|Sated Satyr]] (Waterdeep)

## Description
Human male who owns and operates the Sated Satyr tavern in Waterdeep. Despite the establishment's name, Tibon himself is human (there is also a Satyr bartender who works at the establishment). He was named for the first time in Session 22, having previously been referred to simply as the tavern owner.

## Personality
Runs a busy establishment that caters to adventurers and travelers. The Sated Satyr offers quality rooms (5 gold per night) and serves food and drinks with live music in the evenings.

## History with Party
- **Session 22**: Named for the first time when Milo arrived with Ammalia Castellers. The tavern serves as the party's base of operations in Waterdeep, and they have rooms rented there.

## The Sated Satyr
- Popular evening destination with music, dining, and waiters
- The party rents rooms here as their Waterdeep base
- Raenar Neverember is frequently found here with his dog Pogo
- When Milo entered with Ammalia Castellers (a notable noble), the entire tavern noticed - music stopped briefly, patrons reacted with either disgust or excitement at her presence
