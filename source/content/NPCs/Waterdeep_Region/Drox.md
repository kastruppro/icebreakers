# Drox

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Minotaur Male
**Role:** Armorsmith, Owner of The Peppermint Minotaur
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Waterdeep]] (Southern District)

## Description
A massive minotaur armorsmith who towers over most other beings, especially compared to the gnome Gigz. Skilled craftsman who has made armor for nobles including Raenar Neverember.

## Personality
Professional craftsman. Takes pride in his work.

## History with Party
- **Session 12**: Met party at Statue Protectors Square. Had previously made Raenar's armor "before it broke." Modred promised to visit for new armor.
- **Session 13**: Sold splint armor to Jaspar.

## Services
- Custom armor crafting
- High-quality metalwork
- Previous client: Raenar Neverember
