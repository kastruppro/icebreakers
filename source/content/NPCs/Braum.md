# Braum

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Dwarf Male
**Role:** Last Unfrozen Defender of Axeholm
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Axeholm]]

## Description
A dwarf warrior found fighting a stone giant in Axeholm. The last unfrozen dwarf remaining in the fortress after the ice plague and the theft of the healing hammer.

## Personality
Initially suspicious and quick to anger, especially toward harengon after being robbed by one. Can be convinced with logic and persuasion. Loyal defender of his home and people. Deeply attached to his clan's heirloom hammer.

## Key Quotes
> "How dare you show up after stealing from me!" - Session 7, upon seeing Milo

## History with Party
- Session 7: Found fighting a stone giant in Axeholm. Party saved him. Accused Milo of being the thief who stole the healing hammer, but was eventually convinced it was someone else (actually Liora). Helped party clear ice skeletons from the forge. Remained in Axeholm when party went through the void portal.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Wants to recover the stolen healing hammer, his clan's heirloom
- Concerned about the fate of the other dwarfs who went missing when the hammer was taken
- Doesn't know the full truth about Liora being the thief

## Connections
- [[Axeholm]] - Home fortress, defender
- [[Liora Swiftclock]] - The actual thief who stole his hammer (unknown to him)
- [[Healing Hammer]] - His clan's stolen heirloom
