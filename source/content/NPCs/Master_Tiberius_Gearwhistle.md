# Master Tiberius Gearwhistle

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Gnome Male
**Role:** Artificer-Wizard, Milo's Former Mentor
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Neverwinter]] (House of Knowledge)

## Description
A gnome of considerable magical and technical expertise, bearing the marks of decades spent in arcane study and artifice. His workshop reflects his dual nature - magical tomes share space with intricate mechanical devices.

## Personality
Patient and methodical as befits a teacher and researcher. Takes problems seriously and applies both magical and technical solutions. Maintains genuine care for his former students, particularly Milo.

## Expertise and Abilities
- **Artificer**: Creates magical items and devices
- **Wizard**: Commands significant arcane power
- **Magical Analysis**: Examined the cursed sword
- **Ward Crafting**: Placed protective wards on [[Arthur De Moray]]
- **Luxon Knowledge**: Knows about the mysterious Luxon

## Important Quotes
> "Your parents are probably bad people." - Session 4

> "It's used for time travel magic." - Session 4, about the Luxon

## Key Contributions
- Examined the corrupted substance on the party's weapons
- Created magical containment wards for Arthur's shadow possession
- Provided consultation on various magical threats
- Knows about Milo's Luxon fragment (and was furious when Milo disobeyed orders about it)
