# Auren Thalos

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Former Party Member, Sorcerer
**Status:** Departed from party
**Current Location:** Unknown

## Description
A noble-looking man with black hair and a scar below his cheek. Arrived in Phandalin with the confidence of nobility, carrying a two-handed sword. Connected to the Feywild through his friendship with Thistle.

## Personality
Confident, noble-bearing, and deeply connected to fey magic. Capable of singing in Celestial with captivating effect.

## Key Moments
- **Session 1**: Arrived in Phandalin and joined the forming party.
- **Session 2**: Comforted Sister Gale: "Tymora has done all she can through you, Sister."
- **Session 5**: Sang in Celestial at the Moonstone Mask, captivating the entire crowd.
- **Session 7**: Received a warning from [[Thistle]]: "If a man is released, the forest will burn." Saw [[Tharizdun]] directly in a vision - a massive demon in 8 chains.

## Connection to Thistle
Auren has a special connection to [[Thistle]] in the Feywild. She delivered omen warnings to him during meditation.
