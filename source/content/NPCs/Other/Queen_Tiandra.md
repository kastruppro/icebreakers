# Queen Tiandra

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Archfey Female
**Role:** Queen of the Feywild (co-ruler alongside [[Queen Meb]])
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** The Queens' Court (Feywild) -- exact venue not yet named

## Description
No physical description provided by the DM summary -- the audio for Session 31 was lost. The party first heard her name in Session 31 when [[Cora]] transported them into the Queens' audience chamber. She holds the Feywild jointly with [[Queen Meb]], who per rumor was formerly her enemy.

## Personality
Unknown from direct observation. In the audience scene she presents as a diplomatic co-signer of a joint Feywild policy with Meb. The tone of the audience was framed as a serious strategic brief.

## Strong Working Inference (UNCONFIRMED)
**Tiandra is most likely the Seelie / Summer Queen** -- the highest authority of the court whose **golden-flower mark** [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] and [[Clover]] took at the [[Sphinx Guardian]] in Session 30. The inference rests on:

1. D&D Feywild canon: Tiandra is the standard name for the Seelie / Summer Queen, paired against Maeve / Meb as the Unseelie / Winter Queen.
2. If [[Queen Meb]] is the Unseelie ruler (see her file), the symmetry of "two Queens, formerly at war" cleanly resolves to Seelie + Unseelie.
3. The Seelie golden flower marks Milo and Clover carry from the Sphinx Guardian would then trace upward to Tiandra as the issuing authority -- which would explain why Cora's delivery of the party was accepted and the party was granted an audience rather than detained.

**This identification is NOT confirmed by the DM summary.** Treat as strong-but-unconfirmed.

## History with Party
- **Session 31**: First named. Held joint audience with [[Queen Meb]] and the party (Milo, Modred, Clover) in the Queens' court. Co-delivered the joint Feywild brief: (1) the [[Tharizdun Cult]] is operating inside the Feywild at sustained scale and destroying it, (2) the Queens want to close the Feywild off entirely, and (3) this matters because [[Tharizdun]] may be released. The Queens together asked for [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]]'s help specifically to close the Feywild, and raised the **Shadow containment option** -- that whoever holds the Shadow (currently [[Jaspar Starshade]], not present) could be left in a sealed Feywild forever. No decision was given.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Likely (unconfirmed) the Seelie / Summer Queen, and thus the issuing authority behind the golden-flower marks Milo and Clover took at the [[Sphinx Guardian]].
- The cause of the rumored former war with [[Queen Meb]] is not stated. The cause of the present alliance is given as the shared threat from the [[Tharizdun Cult]].
- Why she co-signs a containment plan targeting [[Jaspar Starshade]] -- an outsider, not a fae -- and what the Seelie Court's specific equity in sealing the realm is, is not explained.
