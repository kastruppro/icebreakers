# The Door

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Feywild sentient door, male voice
**Role:** Transit node -- can teleport travelers anywhere in the Feywild
**Status:** Alive / sentient / **locked**
**Current Location:** [[Mr Plant]]'s basement (peaceful bridge chamber), Feywild

## Description
A single closed **wooden door in a frame**, set against apparent open ocean at the far end of a bridge in Mr. Plant's basement. 40-50 feet below the bridge is a clear, peaceful ocean with jumping fish; broken bridges trail off to either side (which the Door later confirmed used to lead other places before they collapsed). Above, the clouds part on [[Mr Plant]]'s sky-eye watching Modred.

The door's **handle transforms into a human hand** when touched without courtesy. It slapped Clover's hand away when he reached for it.

## Personality
Prim, offended, courteous -- a prissy nobleman in door form. Insists on being addressed properly; reacts to every presumption with theatrical outrage (*"How dare you touch me?"*, *"You never ask anything, you just presume."*). Amused by the party's emotional overflow. Audibly pleased by the idea of them coming back to open him.

## Powers / Mechanics
The Door is a **point-to-point travel node** for the Feywild:
- Names the target: a person or a place.
- The Door opens onto that target.
- Unlimited destinations, provided they exist in the Feywild.

**Currently locked.** A fairy flew through the basement and **stole the Door's key**. Location of the fairy unknown. The Door refuses to be lockpicked (*"how dare you even ask?"*).

Accepts tribute for its inconvenience:
- **Duck oil** (preferred) -- "If you squeeze them, they will--"
- **Whale oil** (acceptable alternative)

Also confirmed the in-universe rules of Feywild travel: emotion is the default transit fuel, but **anxiety jams it**, and the fix is the [[Pool of Wisdom]]. With no cleric or bard in the party, Calm Emotions is off the table.

## History with Party
- **Session 30**: Met in the basement of [[Mr Plant]]'s cottage. Slapped Clover's hand off its handle. Negotiated as a prissy noble. Confirmed it can transport them to any named person or place in the Feywild. Accepted [[Modred De Moray|Modred]]'s false name for Liora (*"Lyra Swiftclaw"*) without complaint. Revealed it is locked, that a fairy stole its key, and that duck oil is its preferred tribute. The party left to consult Mr. Plant; the stairs up did not return them to the cottage but instead delivered them into the [[Queen of Darkness]]'s Diamond Chamber.

## Secrets/Motivations
- True motives unclear. On its face: a locked travel node with a gift economy. Mechanically: the fastest way the party could reach [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]], [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]], or any named destination in the Feywild if the key were recovered.
- The "fairy who stole the key" is an open side quest.
- It cheerfully anticipated the party returning. It did not anticipate -- or warn about -- the stairs up delivering them to the Queen's domain.
