# Sphinx Guardian

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Feywild Sphinx, Female voice
**Role:** Guardian of the oak portal / processes all traffic entering the Feywild
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** The Guardian's Corridor, Feywild side of the oak portal tree

## Description
A massive sphinx-like creature housed in a high-ceilinged stone chamber at the Feywild end of the oak portal. She has:
- A **humanoid face**
- **Griffin wings**
- A **scorpion-like tail**
- **Feline (cat) claws**
- A **neck that stretches serpent-long** -- her head slithered down to meet Milo at eye level

She speaks in a soothing female voice.

## Personality
Calm, curious, and impartial. She processes the corridor line one traveler at a time. She is not malicious -- she handles cultists and party the same way -- but she is distinctly fae, and in private she told Clover that the people of the Material Plane are exhaustingly emotional and that his friends' emotions were *"delicious."*

## Role & Mechanics
Every traveler coming through the oak portal is processed by her:
1. She reads the traveler's emotional state aloud.
2. She asks a question -- this session it was *"Do you like summer or winter the most?"*
3. She marks the traveler with a **court sigil** based on the answer:
   - **Summer** -> **Seelie** golden flower (body/armor mark)
   - **Winter** -> **Unseelie** black rose (body/armor mark, bleeds through any cover)
4. Marks are permanent and read on sight by every fae in the Feywild.

Any traveler who draws a weapon on her is **flicked out of existence** with a swat of her paw. She dispatched several cultists this way, mid-sword-swing, in the party's presence.

## History with Party
- **Session 30**: Processed all three PCs. Read Milo as "disturbed, angry, worried." Marked Milo Seelie (summer). Marked Clover Seelie (summer). Marked Modred Unseelie (winter) after he chose winter on the grounds that *"Waterdeep summer stinks like piss."* Wisped them into the Feywild proper. Later, during the party's wine-fueled rebound out of the Queen's domain, they briefly appeared back in her chamber -- she looked at them confused and asked how they were even there.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Neutral. Her job is to process traffic and mark it for court allegiance. She does not appear to serve either court directly.
- Explicitly recognized Clover as one of her own (Feywild-born eladrin).
- Her statement that the party's emotions are "delicious" echoes the Queen of Darkness's later framing -- fae feed on emotion generally.
