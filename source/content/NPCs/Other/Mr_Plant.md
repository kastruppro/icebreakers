# Mr. Plant

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Feywild flower-entity, male voice
**Role:** Keeper of the Spiral Garden and Cottage / host of the party's long rest
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** The Spiral Garden and Cottage, Feywild
**True Name:** Unknown -- "Mr. Plant" is a fake name he offered in exchange for Modred's fake name

## Description
A tall, thin, **Bellsprout-bodied** flower creature: impossibly thin limbs, a humanoid face, **petal-ears**. Tends a large spiral flower bed of smaller face-bearing flowers ("[[The Little Flowers]]"), each of which can decompose corpses on command. Lives in a cottage at the center of the spiral.

## Personality
Polite on the surface, easily offended under it. Gets visibly angry when the flowers in his spiral are tickled or stepped on. Kills cultists who trespass and feeds their corpses to his flowers -- demonstrated this to the party by having one of his head-flowers whistle, gape its mouth, and vomit a decomposing red-hooded cultist onto the ground for the little flowers to bury.

Hospitable on his own terms: offered the party a long rest in his cottage, *insisted* they visit his basement ("not going into the basement is mean"), and sheltered them overnight without incident. He was gone by morning -- his eye remained.

> "Are you talking about the daughter of the Unseelie?" - Session 30, revealing [[Cora]]'s identity to the party

> "I'm having my eye on you." - Session 30, immediately followed by an actual giant eye opening in the sky above [[Modred De Moray|Modred]]

> "It's the circle of life." (attributed to Milo's reaction, Mr. Plant demonstrating) - Session 30

## History with Party
- **Session 30**: Met tending his spiral. Confirmed red-cloaked cultists had come through -- some stepped on his flowers and were killed for it; others continued on. Revealed that **[[Cora]] is the daughter of the Unseelie**, the session's load-bearing reveal. When Modred attempted to explain the winter-mark, Mr. Plant opened a permanent **sky-eye** on him that still follows Modred through the Feywild and winks back when waved at. Gave his name as "Mr. Plant" -- Modred on a nat-20 Insight realized it was a fake in exchange for Modred's fake name, Feywild rules holding. Invited the party to take a long rest in his cottage. They did. He was gone the next morning; the eye remained overhead.

## Powers / Effects
- Commands [[The Little Flowers]], who bury and decompose corpses.
- Vomits dead bodies on cue as a demonstration of his kill count.
- Opened a **tracking sky-eye** on Modred that follows him regardless of location. Winks when waved at. Unclear whether it carries sound or only sight.
- Has inserted micro-civilizations into his blankets -- thousands of tiny people walking around inside the fabric.
- Cottage windows show **night even in daytime**, and vice versa -- the interior and the exterior run on separate Feywild day-night cycles.

## Secrets/Motivations
- **True name unknown.** He gave a fake name freely, which is itself suspicious in the Feywild.
- Killed cultists (aligned outcome for the party), fed the party (neutral-ally gesture), *and* permanently tagged Modred with a surveillance eye (hostile or at least controlling move). Could be an ally, a passive observer, or a long-game manipulator.
- Referred to some of the cottage beds as being *"for my parents."* If true, there are older flower entities sleeping in that cottage who have not been introduced.
- Confirmed Cora's identity and the Unseelie connection without hesitation -- implying either he is not allied with the Winter Court, or he has no reason to protect its secrets.
