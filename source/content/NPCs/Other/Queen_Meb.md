# Queen Meb

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Archfey Female
**Role:** Queen of the Feywild (co-ruler alongside [[Queen Tiandra]])
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** The Queens' Court (Feywild) -- exact venue not yet named

## Description
No physical description provided by the DM summary -- the audio for Session 31 was lost. The party first heard her name in Session 31 when [[Cora]] transported them into the Queens' audience chamber. She holds the Feywild jointly with [[Queen Tiandra]], who per rumor was formerly her enemy.

## Personality
Unknown from direct observation. In the audience scene she presents as a diplomatic co-signer of a joint Feywild policy with Tiandra. The tone of the audience was framed as a serious strategic brief, not a hostile summons.

## Strong Working Inference (UNCONFIRMED)
**Meb is most likely the [[Queen of Darkness]] the party met in Session 30** -- i.e., [[Cora]]'s mother, the ruler of the Unseelie / Winter Court. The inference rests on:

1. Session 30 confirmed Cora is the daughter of the Queen of Darkness (Unseelie ruler).
2. Cora delivered the party in S31 to the Queens' court personally -- she has direct access to them, which fits a daughter walking the party into her own mother's audience chamber.
3. By D&D Feywild canon the Winter / Unseelie Queen is typically named **Maeve** ("Meb" is a contracted form).
4. [[Modred De Moray]] still bears the Unseelie black rose mark from S30 -- if Meb is the Queen of Darkness, that mark is now in front of its issuer in formal court mode.

**This identification is NOT confirmed by the DM summary.** The party has not been told. Treat as strong-but-unconfirmed until next session.

## History with Party
- **Session 31**: First named. Held joint audience with [[Queen Tiandra]] and the party (Milo, Modred, Clover) in the Queens' court. Briefed the party on three points: (1) the [[Tharizdun Cult]] is operating inside the Feywild at sustained scale and destroying it, (2) the Queens want to close the Feywild off entirely to seal the realm against external access, and (3) this matters especially because [[Tharizdun]] may be released. **Asked [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] specifically for his help** to close the Feywild (reason / mechanism not stated). Also raised, as a separate strategic possibility, that **whoever carries the Shadow could be left inside a sealed Feywild forever** so the Shadow could never be used to release Tharizdun -- a proposal that points directly at [[Jaspar Starshade]], who was not present to hear it. The audience ended with no decision given.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Likely (unconfirmed) the same entity as the [[Queen of Darkness]] -- the Unseelie ruler whose deal Clover refused in S30, now presenting in formal court alongside the Seelie Queen.
- Likely (unconfirmed) the mother of [[Cora]].
- The cause of the rumored former war with [[Queen Tiandra]] is not stated. The cause of the present alliance is given as the shared threat from the [[Tharizdun Cult]].
- The Queens' interest in [[Milo Swiftclock|Milo]] specifically is not explained -- a working (unconfirmed) hypothesis is that closing the Feywild is a chronurgy / dimensional-anchor operation.
