# Sabrina Nightgale

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Elf Female
**Role:** Adventurer from 200 years ago
**Status:** Deceased (ghost in the Void)
**Current Location:** [[The_Void|The Void]] / Underworld

## Description
An elven adventurer who was banished to the Void approximately 200 years ago (from the year 1478). She now exists as a ghost on a boat sailing through the underworld, surrounded by millions of trapped souls.

## Personality
Even after 200 years of imprisonment in the Void, Sabrina retained her clarity and helpfulness. She shared critical information with the party despite her own hopeless situation.

## Important Quotes
> "The shadow must never be freed in the void - it would break the chains and release everything from the underworld." - Session 11

> "The only way to free someone possessed by the Shadow is by taking them into the Void and performing a binding ritual." - Session 11

> "The Elemental Eye has opened. Your friends don't have that much time." - Session 26, warning about the consequences of destroying the first chain

> "The Void is basically where you go when you die. The afterlife." - Session 26, explaining the nature of the Void

> "Tharizdun has control over it, which means that no one is going to the higher planes. Everything is at a standstill." - Session 26, about the Boatman of the Void

## History with Party
- **Session 11**: Met the party on the underworld boat; shared critical information about the Shadow, Tharizdun, and how to free possessed individuals; warned them about the dangers of freeing the Shadow in the Void
- **Session 26**: Party returned to her boat in the Void after being trapped when the portal closed. Provided major lore revelations about the Void being the afterlife, the Boatman of the Void, and the Elemental Eye. Gave [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] anti-corruption bread that contains but does not cure the corruption. Gave [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] a recipe for the bread (requires Waterdeep ingredients and forest elf magic). Revealed her party killed 3 of 4 Princes of Tharizdun but failed the last. Suggested Edward Hamel may have left items or knowledge at his house in Waterdeep. Warned that the Elemental Eye opening means time is running out.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Her party (Edward Hammel, Elise Mayak, Ron Tusk) pretended to help the shadow entity but were discovered
- All her party members are dead, though Ron Tusk (dwarf) might have descendants in Waterdeep
- She possesses knowledge about the binding ritual that could free possessed individuals
- Killing the Boatman of the Void might free all trapped souls, including Sabrina herself

## Lore Provided
Sabrina is a crucial source of information about:
- The mechanics of the Shadow and possession
- The binding ritual to free possessed individuals
- Why the Shadow must never be freed in the Void
- Tharizdun's ability to pause souls' passage to the afterlife
- **The Void is the afterlife** where all dead souls go, meant to be judged and sent to higher planes or the Nine Hells
- **The Boatman of the Void** is a massive cloaked entity that serves as the Judge of the dead; Tharizdun now controls it, halting all soul traffic
- **The Elemental Eye** opened above Tharizdun's head after the first chain was destroyed — a red eye that scanned and fixated on the party
- **Prince dungeons move locations** over time; cult activity near a location signals a nearby dungeon
- **Void portal mechanics**: from inside the Void, draw a triangle and specify a destination to create a targeted portal; the boat's rudder exits to a random location
- **Anti-corruption bread recipe**: made with Waterdeep ingredients and forest elf magic; contains corruption but doesn't cure it
- Her party killed 3 of 4 Princes but failed the last
