# Liset

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Female
**Role:** Politician, Runs the Moonstone Mask
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Neverwinter]]

## Description
A human woman who runs the Moonstone Mask establishment in Neverwinter. She previously ran for political office but lost the election to [[Sebastian]]. Notably, she speaks Celestial, an unusual linguistic ability that hints at either scholarly pursuits or deeper connections.

## Personality
An advocate for the common people of Neverwinter, Liset entered politics to give voice to ordinary citizens. Despite losing the election to Sebastian, she continues her work through the Moonstone Mask, maintaining her influence and connections in the city. She is approachable and genuinely concerned with the welfare of Neverwinter's populace.
