# Sebastian

![[attachments/sebastian.png]]

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Powerful Mage, Leader of Cloak Tower
**Status:** Alive (severely corrupted, ruling Neverwinter)
**Current Location:** [[Neverwinter]] (Neverwinter Castle, presumably)

## Description
Powerful mage who won election in Neverwinter. Uses chronurgy magic. ENTIRE HANDS are black with corruption - significantly worse than Modred's fingers.

## Personality
Secretive, paranoid. Reacted urgently when Clover exposed his blackened hand at the masquerade.

## Key Information
- **Corruption Level**: Entire hands black (vs Modred's halfway down fingers)
- **Travel Anomaly**: Arrived in Waterdeep impossibly fast from Neverwinter (7+ day journey)
- **Conspiracy**: Working with [[Victor_Castellers|Victor Castellers]] on something secret
- **Mass Memory Enchantment**: Has magically rewritten the memories of Neverwinter's populace — guards and citizens genuinely believe Sebastian has always been their lord ("As long as I've been born, I think"). This is deeper than mind control; memories appear to have been fully replaced.
- **Propaganda Campaign**: Banners with his face and an ominous eye symbol are posted throughout the city

## Important Quotes
> "Lord Neverember wouldn't let me, but now I can." - Session 5

> "We have been compromised." - Session 17, to Victor Castellers (lip-read by Clover)

## History with Party
- **Session 5**: Won election against [[Liset]], became Leader of Cloak Tower
- **Session 17**: Appeared at masquerade ball. [[Clover]]'s fake trip exposed his blackened hand. Retreated "downstairs" with [[Victor_Castellers|Victor]] after saying "We have been compromised."
- **Session 18**: Party infiltrated [[House_Castellers_Estate|House Castellers]], discovered more about his involvement
- **Session 27**: Not encountered directly. Party arrived in [[Neverwinter]] to discover Sebastian has cast a mass memory enchantment on the city — guards at the gates do not recognize [[Lord_Neverember]] and believe Sebastian has always been their lord. Propaganda banners bearing his face with an eye symbol are displayed throughout the city. The party is now in Neverwinter trying to find allies not under his enchantment.

## Mysteries
- How did he travel from Neverwinter to Waterdeep so fast?
- What is the source of his severe corruption?
- What magic did he use for the mass memory enchantment — is it reversible?
- What is the eye symbol on his propaganda banners?
- Are there any citizens who escaped the memory rewrite (e.g., in the docks)?
