# Cora

![[attachments/cora.png]]
## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Female (publicly) -- **confirmed Daughter of the [[Queen of Darkness]], crown princess of the [[Unseelie Court]]** (Session 30)
**Role:** Tharizdun Cultist / Primary Antagonist / Unseelie Princess
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[The Moonlit Pool]] (Feywild), with [[House Castellers]] tents and cages of captive fae, guarding the dry pool and whatever **"Okramok"** is beneath it

## Description
Dark-cloaked cultist who serves Tharizdun. Known for her acrobatic escapes. **As of Session 30, confirmed to be the daughter of the [[Queen of Darkness]] -- the ruler of the Unseelie / Winter Court of the Feywild.** Her triangular portals, flash-teleport, and ability to move between the Material Plane and the Feywild all recontextualize through this: the party has been fighting an Unseelie princess this entire campaign.

## Personality
Mocking, confident, taunting. Takes pleasure in the party's struggles. Dreams full of destruction and hate according to Desna - "not a good person."

## Important Quotes
> "The chained one has been released." - Session 8

> "Why did you break me free?" - Session 8

> "Ah, so it has begun already. I see that you have taken the gift of Faisten. I see that you have embraced it." - Session 17, upon seeing Modred's blackened fingers

> "I think I know where your brother is anyway." - Session 17, before escaping through portal

## History with Party
- **Session 7**: Freed the Chained One (Tharizdun)
- **Session 17**: Waiting outside Tharizdun temple after party emerged. Fought party with four cultists. Escaped through triangular portal despite taking Spike Growth damage.
- **Session 29**: Recaptured [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]] during the cult attack on [[New_Sharandar|New Sharandar]]; shook off Clover's staff DC effect on a nat-18 Wisdom save; flash-teleported clean over Clover's 100-ft [[Plant Growth]] in a single move rather than walk the difficult terrain; ran through the Feywild portal tree with Liora squirming under her arm.
- **Session 30**: **MAJOR REVELATION -- Cora is the daughter of the [[Queen of Darkness]], the ruler of the Unseelie Court.** Confirmed by [[Mr Plant]] (*"Are you talking about the daughter of the Unseelie?"*) and by the Queen herself in her Diamond Chamber. Located at [[The Moonlit Pool]] in the Feywild, running a [[House Castellers]] camp with cages of captive fae and at least three cultist guards. **She knows she has been followed into the Feywild** -- she said so aloud to [[Clovers Parents|Clover's parents]] (read off her lips by Clover from the tree line): *"I've been followed in here. We need to protect Okramok. You need to jump down into the pool now."* Clover's parents -- whom Clover believed were dead -- jumped into the dry Moonlit Pool on her command without hesitation and vanished. Cora turned from the pool toward the fae cages at session end, back still to the party. Clover on sight: *"She dies today."*
- **Session 31**: **Detected the party's planned ambush from the tree line before it could land** -- the party's emotions were still raw from the S30 Queen of Darkness encounter and the wine-blur exit, and the Feywild reads emotion as a tactical signal. The ambush they had spent the end of S30 planning collapsed at the moment of execution because the party themselves were lit up like beacons. **Cora did not fight back.** Instead, she pitched a deal: she had "valuable information" to give them, and **she knew where [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] was**. The party did not kill her; they went with her. She then **transported the party to the audience chamber of the Queens of the Feywild -- [[Queen Meb]] and [[Queen Tiandra]]** -- and **is now functioning as a (suspect) intermediary** between the party and the Queens' court. **This is new information about her**: she can be made to talk when surprise is on the party's side, and she has direct access to the highest authorities of the Feywild. Whether her intermediary role is genuine alliance, compulsion, a doubled play, or some combination is unresolved. The audio for this session was lost -- no verbatim quotes available.
