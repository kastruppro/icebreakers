# Water Sphere Entity

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Unknown Entity
**Role:** Guardian of The Depths
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[The Depths]]

## Description
A massive sentient water sphere that hovers within a ring of three stone pillars. The sphere appears to be alive and aware, capable of movement and observation. The purple-black corruption liquid associated with Tharizdun floats around the sphere, indicating a direct connection to the Chained God's power.

The entity can divide itself into three smaller spheres, each capable of independent movement and action. In its divided form, each sphere controls a different section of the dungeon labyrinth beneath House Castellers.

## Abilities Observed
- **Division:** Can split from one massive sphere into three smaller spheres
- **Water/Blood Manipulation:** Controls water flow in The Depths; can transform water into thick, gooey blood
- **Trap Mastery:** Controls dungeon mechanisms (doors sealing, drain traps, sliding ceiling panels, rain generation)
- **Corruption Affinity:** The Tharizdun corruption liquid orbits and responds to it
- **Tentacle Summoning:** Commands tentacles from floor drains that grapple and constrict targets (Session 23-24, Fountain Room)
- **Illusion Magic:** Creates convincing illusions - full illusion of [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] floating in mid-air; can divide into many copies with only some being real (illusion copies deal 4 acid damage when struck)
- **Water Elemental Summoning:** Can summon a full Water Elemental from its vortex (Session 24, after all three rooms completed)
- **Rain Control:** Generates constant rain from ceilings to douse fire (Brazier Room puzzle)

## Personality
Sadistic and theatrical. Takes pleasure in the suffering of adventurers who enter its domain. Refers to killing as "games" and laughs at victims' struggles. Speaks with a dark, booming voice.

## Key Quotes
> "More adventurers has come to die. Well, let the games begin." - Session 23, upon the party's arrival

> "I guess I underestimated you all." - Session 24, in the Illusion Room after the party saw through the fake Neverember

## History with Party
- **Session 23:** Encountered the party after they descended through the whirlpool beneath House Castellers. Divided into three spheres and retreated to different dungeon sections. One sphere activated a trap in the Fountain Room, turning water to blood and sealing the party inside. Laughed as Milo and Jaspar were pulled underwater by unknown creatures.
- **Session 24:** The party completed all three of its challenge rooms:
  - **Fountain Room:** Tentacles from the drains grappled party members in the rising blood-water. The party fought them off and escaped through a ceiling door/water slide.
  - **Illusion Room (center path):** Created a convincing illusion of [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] floating in mid-air. After [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]]'s Detect Magic revealed the illusion, the entity divided into many small copies (one real, rest illusions). The puzzle escalated from one real sphere to two, but the party used Detect Magic to identify real targets each time.
  - **Brazier Room (last path):** Generated rain from the ceiling to douse five braziers. The party discovered hidden brick buttons controlling sliding ceiling panels to shield the braziers.
  - **Water Sphere Chamber (final):** After all three pillars were lit, the entity created a massive water vortex between the pillars and summoned a Water Elemental. The possessed [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] emerged from within the elemental. Boss fight pending next session.

## Three Challenge Rooms (Session 24)
The entity's dungeon consists of three challenge rooms, each returning a water sphere to a stone pillar upon completion:
1. **Fountain Room** - Survive rising blood-water and tentacle grapples to reach the ceiling exit
2. **Illusion Room** - Identify and destroy the real sphere among illusion copies (Detect Magic is key; illusions deal 4 acid damage when struck)
3. **Brazier Room** - Keep five braziers lit by finding hidden ceiling panel controls and blocking rain

Once all three pillars are lit, the entity summons a Water Elemental and reveals the possessed [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]].

## Combat Notes
The entity prefers indirect combat through traps, puzzles, and summoned creatures. It has been defeated in each individual room challenge but was able to summon a Water Elemental for the final confrontation. Direct combat with the entity/elemental is pending (Session 24 cliffhanger).
