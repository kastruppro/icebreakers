# Queen of Darkness

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Fae (Unseelie) Female
**Role:** Ruler of the Unseelie Court / Queen of the Winter Court / Mother of [[Cora]]
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** Her Domain (the Diamond Chamber) in the [[Feywild]]

## Description
Manifests as a **massive floating black diamond** bleeding black fog in midair. She has no body visible to the party -- only her voice, soothing and feminine, speaking from the diamond. In Feywild childhood scare-tales told to eladrin children, she is described exactly as "a black diamond." Clover recognized her on sight from stories he grew up with.

The black fog around the diamond can condense into shadow-figures that mimic specific people -- voice, posture, emotional hook. The illusions can recreate emotional presence convincingly but **cannot fake specific private facts** (favorite dish, childhood spell, etc.).

## Personality
Patient, poised, and malevolent. Addresses travelers with warm hospitality even while working to trap them. Reads emotional state directly and aloud -- she describes the party's feelings as "so vivid, so fresh... so delicious." Takes the party's refusal of her offer personally and registers it as a standing grievance.

## Powers & Mechanics
- Pulls travelers into her domain when their emotions are ungoverned (the Feywild's emotion-based transit network feeds into her when anxiety runs loose).
- Manifests **shadow-doubles** of loved ones from the fog.
- Amplifies the target's own anxiety to pin them in her chamber -- two rounds of Wisdom saves barely dented her grip.
- Her grip on the party was broken only by Feywild wine -- intoxication at triple speed flattened their emotions enough to slip out.

## History with Party
- **Session 30**: First encounter. The party climbed the stairs out of [[The Door]]'s chamber and, instead of arriving back in [[Mr Plant]]'s cottage, were dumped into her Diamond Chamber. She presented shadow-puppets of [[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]], [[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]], and [[Clovers Parents|Clover's parents]]. The first two shadows failed private-fact tests; Clover's parents' shadow *passed* the age-4 spell test word-for-word, revealing his parents are actually alive and "in her care." She offered to take the party to their loved ones at "no cost." Clover read the offer on a nat-20 Insight as malevolence and refused. She made clear the refusal was remembered. The party broke her grip by getting drunk on Feywild wine and were vomited back into the transit network.
- **Session 31**: **Not on-screen by this name.** However, the strong working inference (unconfirmed by the DM summary) is that **she is sitting in the Queens' audience chamber as [[Queen Meb]]**, co-ruling the Feywild alongside [[Queen Tiandra]]. If true, the party is now standing in front of the same entity whose offer Clover refused last session -- now in formal-court mode with her former enemy as co-signer, pitching a Feywild-sealing plan against [[Tharizdun]]. The S30 refusal is implicitly being relitigated. **Last_seen intentionally left at 30** because the Meb identification has not yet been confirmed.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Mother of [[Cora]]. The party's primary antagonist has been the crown princess of the Unseelie Court this entire campaign.
- Holds [[Clovers Parents|Clover's parents]] "in her care" -- the exact nature of that care (coercion, alliance, compulsion) is unresolved.
- Now knows the party exists, has refused her offer, and carries a [[Modred De Moray|Modred]] bearing her court's **Unseelie black rose** mark.
- Almost certainly tied to the Tharizdun plot via her daughter and the [[House Castellers]] operation at the [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]].
