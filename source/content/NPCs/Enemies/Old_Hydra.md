# Old Hydra

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Water Elemental
**Role:** Guardian of The Depths, Boss Enemy
**Status:** Dead (destroyed by [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]]'s Sunfire Mace)
**Current Location:** [[The Depths]] (beneath [[House_Castellers_Estate|House Castellers]])

## Description
A massive Water Elemental also referred to as "Bullhydra." Formed when three water spheres in the Water Sphere Chamber merged into a typhoon. Summoned and commanded by the possessed [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] as a guardian in [[The Depths]].

## Abilities
- **Acid Body Cycle:** Alternated between a normal blue water form and an acid green form. When green, any melee attack against it dealt acid damage back to the attacker.
- **Storm Pillars:** Could raise its hands to activate the stone pillars in the chamber, generating environmental hazards -- first a thunderstorm (lightning damage in a circle, Con save DC 17), then a blizzard (flat cold damage in circles, no save).
- **Water Ball Attacks:** Fired murky water balls at range.
- **Slam Attacks:** Melee slam attacks against multiple targets.
- **Ungrappleable:** Could not be grappled due to its water form.

## Key Quotes
> "Bullhydra, I guess we will teach them a lesson." - Possessed [[Lord_Neverember|Neverember]], commanding Old Hydra into battle

## History with Party
- **Session 25:** Fought the party in the Water Sphere Chamber alongside possessed [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]]. Its acid body cycle punished melee attackers, and its storm pillar attacks nearly caused a TPK -- a blizzard dealing 24 cold damage knocked out three of four party members simultaneously. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] destroyed it with the Sunfire Mace's radiant damage, though the final acid retort knocked him unconscious at the same moment.
