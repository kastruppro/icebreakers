# Liora Swiftclock

![[attachments/liora.jpeg]]
## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Harengon Female
**Role:** Milo's Younger Sister, Prophet
**Status:** Missing (captive of Cora)
**Current Location:** [[Feywild]] (dragged through the portal tree by [[Cora]])

## Description
A 22-year-old harengon with adventurous spirit and prophetic abilities. Liora has always been headstrong and empathetic, drawn to stories, dreams, and omens rather than the structured life of her clockmaker family.

## Personality
Adventurous, headstrong, and deeply empathetic. While Milo seeks answers in logic and theory, Liora chases them through visions and instinct. Her prophetic visions have driven her to make dangerous choices.

## Key Quotes
> "Do not trust the shadow, trust that I know best. I beg of you not to go look for me. If you find me, do not free me, for it will mean the end, the chained released." - Cryptic note

> "A time will come when you have to choose between family and power. If you're not ready, it will be the end of the world." - Session 11 prophecy to Clover

> "Oh my god, Milo, I was so glad to see you. I thought something was happening to you." - Session 28, reuniting with Milo at New Sharandar

## History with Party
- **Session 7**: First mentioned; party discovered she had stolen the hammer with [[Cora]]
- **Session 8**: Found frozen on the mountain top, freed by the party when the gem was removed from the hammer
- **Session 11**: Delivered prophecy about choosing between family and power; currently staying at Swiftclock home in Neverwinter
- **Session 28**: Reunited with Milo at New Sharandar, where she had taken refuge with Arthur after fleeing Neverwinter when Cora got too close. Confirmed she and Arthur had to flee, and that Arthur turned on her in the forest before the eladrin subdued him -- she had not been allowed to visit him since. She was with an elf friend weaving a bouquet when Milo found her and threw herself at him in a hug. At the session's end, during the cult attack on the temple, [[Cora]] grabbed Liora and fled the burning camp with her. Milo's brief reunion ended almost immediately.
- **Session 29**: Dragged through the Feywild portal tree by [[Cora]] at the end of Session 29 after the party failed to catch them through the burning camp. Still alive and visibly struggling in Cora's grip when last seen -- squirming under her arm as Cora ran into the portal.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Her prophetic visions led her to work with [[Cora]], believing it was necessary
- The full meaning of her prophecy about "family and power" remains unclear
- May have deeper knowledge about [[Tharizdun]]'s cult than she has revealed
