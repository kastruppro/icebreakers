# Clover's Parents

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Harengon, one man and one woman -- an older couple (DM: *"your parents, a man and a woman"* / *"two older people"*)
**Role:** Druids / Protectors of the [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]] / [[Clover]]'s parents
**Status:** **Alive** (contrary to Clover's established backstory)
**Current Location:** Somewhere below the dry Moonlit Pool in the Feywild -- jumped in on [[Cora]]'s command and vanished without hitting bottom
**Names:** Currently unknown

## Description
Two older harengons -- Clover's father and mother. Druids of the Moonlit Pool, the sacred site that was the heart of Clover's childhood in the Feywild. Clover has lived **21 sessions believing they died** the night the cultists destroyed the original Moonlit Pool. He was wrong.

## The Revelation (Session 30)
Clover's entire backstory premise collapsed in a single scene.

1. In the [[Queen of Darkness]]'s Diamond Chamber, the Queen summoned shadow-puppets of each PC's loved ones. Modred's shadow-[[Arthur De Moray|Arthur]] failed the favorite-dish test. Milo's shadow-[[Liora Swiftclock|Liora]] glitched on the house-test. But Clover, applying the same test, asked his parents' shadows to tell him **the first spell he cast at age 4**.

    They answered it back to him **word for word.**

2. The shadows spoke to him directly:
    > "We are real, but these are not. We are alive. Leave this place and just -- try to find us."

    The Queen later confirmed: *"You will never find your family, even though they are alive and in my care."*

3. Minutes later, after the party's wine-powered escape from the Queen's domain, they landed outside the [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]] -- **intact** (not destroyed as Clover's backstory held), currently **dry**, surrounded by [[House Castellers]] tents and cages of captive fae. And at the edge of the dry pool stood **Cora**, speaking calmly to two older harengons. Clover's parents. Alive.

4. Clover read Cora's lips from the tree line: *"I've been followed in here. We need to protect **Okramok**. You need to jump down into the pool now."*

    **His parents jumped in without hesitation.** They vanished into the dry basin, not hitting bottom -- the pool is a passage to somewhere deeper.

## Personality
Unknown on direct contact -- the party has not yet spoken to them. Observed behavior: **instant obedience to Cora.** No hesitation, no questions, no negotiation. Whether this is coercion, compulsion, deception, or genuine alignment with the Unseelie / Tharizdun cause is the central open question.

## Key Quotes
> "We are real, but these are not. We are alive. Leave this place and just -- try to find us." - Session 30 (via the Queen of Darkness's shadow-puppets, confirmed real by passing Clover's age-4 spell test word-for-word)

## History with Party
- **Pre-campaign (Clover's backstory)**: Protectors / druids of the [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]] in the Feywild. Believed dead in the cultist attack that Clover thought destroyed the Pool.
- **Session 22**: Clover received a Void-vision of them on a boat, which he read as the afterlife. That vision is now retroactively suspect.
- **Session 30**: **Confirmed alive.** Spoke to Clover (via shadow) and passed the private-fact test the Queen's illusions could not fake for Modred or Milo. Later that session, the party saw them in the flesh at the Moonlit Pool, in calm conversation with [[Cora]]. Jumped into the dry Pool on Cora's command to protect **[[Okramok]]**. Last seen vanishing into the basin.
- **Session 31**: **Jumped into the dry [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]] again at the start of the session and vanished below.** Whether this was a fresh jump after returning, or the same vanishing the party had witnessed at the end of S30 viewed again, is not clarified by the DM summary -- it treats it as a fresh in-session event. They were not seen for the rest of the session. The party's planned ambush on [[Cora]] (the next beat after the parents' jump) collapsed immediately, and [[Cora]] transported the party away from the Moonlit Pool to the Queens' court, leaving the parents below the pool unobserved.

## Secrets/Motivations
- **Alive, when Clover was told they were dead.** Who lied, and why?
- **Cooperating with Cora** -- coerced, deceived, compelled, or voluntary? Open question.
- **In the Queen of Darkness's "care"** -- per the Queen's own words.
- **Protecting "Okramok"** -- brand-new proper noun introduced this session. No description, no pronoun, nothing beyond that they are defending it below the pool.
- The [[The Moonlit Pool|Moonlit Pool]] itself was **not destroyed** -- also contradicting Clover's backstory. Whatever story Clover grew up with, major pieces of it were a lie or a misunderstanding.
