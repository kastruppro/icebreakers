# Godwin De Moray

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Patriarch of House De Moray, Modred's Father
**Status:** Alive (Recovering)
**Current Location:** [[Waterdeep]] (House De Moray / House Timori)

## Description
Previously a broken, drunken shell of a man. As of Session 26, Goodwin has undergone a dramatic transformation: clean-shaven, sober, put-together, and standing tall. The great hall of House De Moray is now full of light with open windows, in stark contrast to the dark, oppressive atmosphere during the party's first visit.

## Personality
Previously consumed by guilt and shame, Goodwin has turned a corner. He is now apologetic, cooperative, and actively trying to make amends with his family. He maintains a spy network and wants to help the party, though he recognizes he needs to rebuild his own reputation in Waterdeep first.

## Important Quotes
> "I have no son. My son is gone. Leave." - Session 12, to Modred (in his dark period)

> "I was not in a good space. I would like nothing more to reunite our family again." - Session 26, apologizing to Modred

> "I also need you to know that my spies have found cult activity in the forest." - Session 26, providing intelligence about Neverwinter Forest

## History with Party
- **Session 12**: Party visited House De Moray in Waterdeep. Found Goodwin in a deteriorated state. He attacked them with Wall of Fire when Milo tried to introduce the party.
- **Session 26**: Dramatic transformation. Modred, Milo, and [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] visited. Goodwin was clean-shaven, sober, and welcoming. He apologized sincerely for his previous behavior and expressed his desire to reunite the family. Gave the party **350 gold** and a **Sending Stone** to Modred for continued communication. Revealed his spies found cult activity in Neverwinter Forest. Shared his account of Arthur's Shadow possession: he and Arthur went to a cult ruin in the forest outside Waterdeep, fought cultists performing a ritual, an explosion happened, and something shadowy flew into Arthur. No healer could fix it afterward. Offered horses for the party's journey. Wanted to join them but needs to regain his reputation first.

## Mental State
### Session 12 (Revealed by Desna)
Goodwin's dreams were full of:
- **Guilt**: Something he has done haunts him
- **Hatred**: Directed at himself or others
- **Shame**: The De Moray legacy carries terrible weight

### Session 26 (Recovery)
Goodwin has clearly worked through much of his despair. He is sober, presentable, and proactively trying to help. His guilt over Arthur's possession remains, but he is channeling it into action rather than self-destruction.

## Family Tragedy
- [[Modred_De_Moray|Modred De Moray]] left home years ago — now reconciling as of Session 26
- [[Arthur_De_Moray|Arthur De Moray]] was possessed by a shadow entity after Goodwin took him to investigate a cult ruin outside Waterdeep
- His wife [[Morgane]] left him for the House of Justice
- He "lost both his sons" and spiraled into madness — now working to undo this
