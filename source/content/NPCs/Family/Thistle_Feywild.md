# Thistle (Feywild)

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Eladrin Male
**Role:** Auren's Friend, Delivers Omens
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Feywild]]

## Description
An eladrin from the Feywild who shares a deep friendship with Auren. Has the ability to enter shared meditations with Auren, allowing them to communicate across planes.

## Personality
Calm and mystical. Speaks in poetic observations about nature. Carries warnings from the Feywild folk and dwarfs. Genuinely cares about Auren's wellbeing.

## Key Quotes
> "It's beautiful out here isn't it." - Session 7, greeting Auren in meditation
> "If a man is released, the forest will burn." - Session 7, delivering the omen

## History with Party
- Session 7: Contacted Auren during meditation while the party rested in Axeholm. Warned that the ice had come to the forest and delivered an omen from the dwarfs: "If a man is released, the forest will burn." Also mentioned a harengon who might be involved (likely referring to Liora) and advised capturing her if seen.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Connected to prophecy/omens from the Feywild
- Knows more about "the man who must not be released" than revealed
- May have information about the Chained cult's activities affecting the Feywild

## Connections
- [[Auren]] - Close friend, can share meditations
- [[Feywild]] - Home plane
- [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora Swiftclock]] - Warned Auren about a harengon involved in events
