# Pogo

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Golden Retriever (Male)
**Role:** Raenar Neverember's Pet Dog
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Waterdeep]] (with Raenar)

## Description
A friendly golden retriever belonging to Raenar Neverember. Energetic and loving. Can apparently do handstands (a trick taught by Raenar).

## Personality
Enthusiastic and loving toward Raenar. Excited when someone can understand him (via Speak with Animals). Dislikes cats. Enjoys treats, walks, baths, and rolling in mud.

## Key Quotes
> "Oh my god, you understand me!" - When Clover used Speak with Animals, Session 12
> "He gives me treats, and he walks me, and gives me baths. Very nice." - About Raenar
> "Except for the cats. I don't like the cats." - On city life

## History with Party
- **Session 12**: Clover spoke with Pogo using Speak with Animals at the Sated Satyr Inn. He was excited to finally be understood and shared his love for Raenar.

## Dreams (Revealed by Desna, Session 19)
Pogo's dreams are filled with:
- Treats
- Simple, pure happiness
- A good boy's contentment

## Connections
- [[Raenar Neverember]] - Owner and beloved master
- [[Clover]] - Can communicate with via magic
