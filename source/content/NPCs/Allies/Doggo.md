# Doggo

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Feywild golden retriever, male, long-haired, speaking
**Role:** Keeper of the Meadow Tree / Feywild 101 guide
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** The Meadow Tree, in the sunlit meadow beyond the Guardian's Corridor

## Description
A friendly, long-haired **golden retriever**, found barking furiously up into the hollow of a massive tree at a bone-thieving rabbit. He speaks in a clear voice. Party nicknamed him **"Doggo"** on sight; Modred tried to introduce himself to the dog as *"Black"* ("Black, like my armor").

## Personality
Direct, friendly, and -- like everything in the Feywild -- literal. He asked Modred to *"lend me a hand, please"*, and when Modred offered, took the hand off at the wrist in his mouth. He spat it back out covered in flowers, apologetically, and Modred reattached it with no permanent damage. Lesson delivered.

Warm and helpful once the party stopped being useful meat. Openly worried about the party's "emotions all over the place" and pointed them to the help they needed.

## Key Quotes
> "Lend me a hand, please." - Session 30

> "Your emotions are everywhere. You need to get them checked or the fae will use them against you." - Session 30 (paraphrased)

## History with Party
- **Session 30**: Met in the meadow beyond the Sphinx Guardian. Staged the *"lend me a hand"* lesson on Modred. Milo solved the bone-in-the-tree problem with Mage Hand -- the first time in 29 sessions of play he'd used the cantrip -- and the table cheered. Doggo warned the party their emotions were *"everywhere"* and would be weaponized against them by any fae who noticed. He directed them to the **[[Pool of Wisdom]]** (also called the "Hot Springs of Wisdom") to calm their emotions, and pointed out the road the red-cloaked cultists had taken -- through the bushes toward [[Mr Plant]]'s spiral.

## Gift to the Party
- Granted [[Modred De Moray|Modred]] a new ability: while in the [[Feywild]], Modred can cast **Mage Hand** once per short rest. Modred can physically detach his own hand as the somatic component -- it floats and manipulates at will. Treated as a party trick for the rest of the session.

## Secrets/Motivations
- Genuinely friendly so far, but a talking golden retriever in the Feywild is still a fae. True name and true nature unknown.
- He was the party's first piece of practical advice about emotion-based travel -- advice they did not act on until it was nearly fatal in the Queen of Darkness's domain.
