# Raenar Neverember

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Son of Lord Neverember, Hunter
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Neverwinter]] (docks area, with Lord Neverember)

## Description
Found hanging in a trap in the forest. Led party to Waterdeep. Owns a golden retriever named Pogo.

## Personality
Helpful, trustworthy. Dreams of reuniting with his father (per Desna).

## Important Quotes
> "My father is missing? Where have you been living? Under a rock?" - Session 11

## Key Information
- Father ([[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]]) was missing but has been rescued and reunited (Session 26)
- Dreams of being with his father again (confirms trustworthy via Desna)
- Good with hunting and survival
- Has unresolved resentment toward his father for being absent during his childhood

## History with Party
- **Session 11**: Found hanging in trap, rescued by party
- **Session 12+**: Traveled with party, helped navigate
- **Session 19**: Present at Sated Satyr Inn, turned invisible from pepper nut
- **Session 26**: Reunited with his rescued father [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]]
- **Session 27**: Argued with his father about being absent during his childhood — the party overheard the heated exchange at his place before Raenar put on a fake smile. Traveled through [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]]'s portal to [[Neverwinter]] alongside Lord Neverember. Screamed in pain during the crossing but suffered no visible corruption. Now heading to the docks area in disguise with the party.
