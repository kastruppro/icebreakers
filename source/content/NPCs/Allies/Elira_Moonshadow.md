# Elira Moonshadow

![[attachments/elira.png]]
## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** High Elf Female
**Role:** Divination Wizard, Archivist/Ink-Scribe of Oghma
**Status:** Alive
**Current Location:** [[Neverwinter]] (House of Knowledge)

## Description
An elegant High Elf scholar with the refined bearing of Old Sharandar. Soft-spoken, observant, and insightful. Her eyes hold the distant look common to practitioners of divination magic.

## Personality
Scholarly and methodical, Elira approaches problems through research and careful analysis. As Milo's friend and mentor, she balances academic rigor with genuine care. She values knowledge for its own sake but understands some knowledge carries responsibility.

## Role and Expertise
- **Divination Wizard**: Specializes in seeing what is hidden
- **Archivist of Oghma**: Keeper of records at the House of Knowledge
- **Ink-Scribe**: Creates and preserves magical texts
- **Researcher**: Conducts investigations for the party

## History with Party
- **Session 4**: First met by the party at the House of Knowledge
- **Sessions 4-11**: Provided ongoing research assistance
- Warned Milo about Professor Blackthorn's strange behavior since the ice

## Origins
Born in Old Sharandar in the Feywild, she relocated to Neverwinter seeking knowledge and stability. She encouraged Milo to explore Old Sharandar's ruins on Toril.
