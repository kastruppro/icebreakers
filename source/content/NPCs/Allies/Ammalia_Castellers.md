# Ammalia Castellers

## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Female
**Role:** Lady of House Castellers (former)
**Status:** Alive, Allied with Party
**Current Location:** [[House_of_Justice|Temple of Tyr]], Waterdeep

## Description
Wife of [[Victor_Castellers|Victor Castellers]] and mother of their children. Unaware of her husband's dealings with [[Tharizdun]]'s cult until the party's infiltration revealed the truth.

## Personality
Protective mother, desperate to save her children. Willing to ally with former enemies when confronted with the truth about her husband's dark dealings.

## Key Quotes
> "The four princes..." - Revealing what Victor called the entities he dealt with

## History with Party
- **Session 17**: Present at masquerade ball
- **Session 20**: Allied with [[The_Icebreakers]] after [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]]'s natural 29 Persuasion convinced her to negotiate
- **Session 21**: Witnessed Victor's fate when chains dragged him through a portal after the party rescued her children. Provided intelligence about the basement ritual.
- **Session 22**: Staying at [[House_of_Justice|Temple of Tyr]] under protection. Faced hostility from Waterdeep citizens ("Child killer!"). Participated in strategy meeting.

## Key Intelligence Provided
- Victor made a deal with "the four princes" of [[Tharizdun]]
- The cult seeks someone/something called "Thassa"
- 8-10 cultists guard [[Lord_Neverember]] in the basement ritual chamber
- The binding ritual requires "strong spellcasters"

## Connections
- [[Victor_Castellers|Victor]] - Husband, claimed by Tharizdun
- [[House_Castellers]] - Former estate
- [[The_Icebreakers]] - Allied after discovering the truth
- [[Lord_Neverember]] - Knowledge of his imprisonment location
- [[Thistle]] - Her children were connected to Thistle's magical trance

## Current Situation
Protected at the Temple of Tyr but facing public hatred. The rumors about House Castellers' involvement in child disappearances have spread through Waterdeep, making her a target of mob anger despite her innocence in the conspiracy.
