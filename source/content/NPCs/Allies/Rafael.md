# Rafael

![[attachments/rafael.png]]
## Quick Reference
**Race/Gender:** Human Male
**Role:** Warlock, former member of the Daggers of the North
**Status:** Deceased
**Current Location:** Daggerford Cemetery (to be buried by Daggers of the North)

## Description
Deceased warlock who was a member of the Daggers of the North adventuring party. His body was being transported in a coffin when the party first met the Daggers at the Sated Satyr Inn.

## Personality
Unknown - only learned about through stories from surviving Daggers members.

## Key Quotes
> "Rafael our friend had a lot of nightmares about chains and some weird demon." - Moguna, about Rafael

## History with Party
- Session 19: The party learned about Rafael from the Daggers of the North. He was plagued by nightmares about chains and demons (likely connected to Tharizdun). He died fighting a corrupted beast in the marsh south of Daggerford.

## Secrets/Motivations
His nightmares about Tharizdun's chains may have indicated a deeper connection to the cult or the god himself. The cause of his nightmares remains unexplained.
