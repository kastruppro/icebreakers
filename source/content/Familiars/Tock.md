## Familiar Abilities

As Milo's familiar (via Find Familiar spell):
- Milo can communicate telepathically with Tock within 100 ft.
- Milo can see through Tock's eyes and hear what Tock hears as an action
- Milo can deliver touch spells through Tock
- Tock can be dismissed to a pocket dimension and resummoned within 30 ft.
- If Tock dies, can be resummoned with another casting of Find Familiar (1 hour, 10 gp incense)

## Temporal Quirks

As a chronurgy wizard's familiar, Tock exhibits subtle temporal anomalies:
- Occasionally hoots slightly before events happen
- Feathers sometimes shimmer with faint dunamantic energy
- Head rotations appear to skip frames

## Notes

Tock serves as Milo's eyes and ears for scouting, with exceptional darkvision (120 ft.) and the Flyby ability making him perfect for reconnaissance without provoking attacks.
