# Mr. Plant's Eye

*Surveillance sigil / tracking effect, involuntary*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]] (involuntary)
**Acquired:** Session 30
**Source:** Opened in the sky above Modred by [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant]] after Modred revealed his Unseelie mark
**Status:** Active, permanent until removed by means unknown

## Description

A **giant disembodied eye** permanently opens in whatever sky is over [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]], fixing on him and following him wherever he goes. It is visible in the sky from any outdoor location and **through ceilings or cloud cover** when he is indoors -- the eye was still visible from inside [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant's]] basement, watching down through the rafters and the clouds above the ocean chamber.

The eye is [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant's]] surveillance tool. It opened the moment [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] tried to explain his Unseelie mark to [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant]]. [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant's]] own words were: *"I'm having my eye on you."* -- said literally, as the eye opened overhead.

It **winks back when acknowledged**. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] has waved at it and received a wink in response.

It is **unclear whether [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant]] can hear through the eye or only see**. The party is operating under the assumption that anything said within its line of sight may be overheard.

## Properties

### Persistent Surveillance
- Open permanently in any sky overhead of [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]]
- Tracks him across locations (confirmed: Spiral Garden, Cottage interior, Basement, Queen of Darkness's domain -- may persist across planes; untested on Material Plane)
- Visible through overcast, clouds, and interior ceilings (confirmed in the Basement)

### Interactive
- **Winks back when waved at or otherwise acknowledged** -- implying consciousness, not just a passive scry

### Feature: Hearing Unknown
- It is not known whether the eye transmits audio or visual-only
- Party should assume it hears as well

### Not Removable
- No known method to remove the eye
- Has survived travel through the Feywild's transit network (Door, Queen's domain, wine-fueled exit, Moonlit Pool tree line)

### Social Consequence
- Any creature who looks up will see a giant eye above [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] -- not subtle
- Marks [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] as "watched by something important" to any observer

## How It Was Acquired

In [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant's]] Spiral Garden, [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] -- trying to explain that his Unseelie mark didn't mean what [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant]] thought it meant, that in his world "summer" and "winter" are only seasons -- triggered [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant's]] direct surveillance response.

A giant disembodied eye opened in the sunny sky above him and fixed on him. [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant]] said, literally: *"I'm having my eye on you."* [[Clover]] later warned [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] specifically that the eye was going to be on him from now on.

[[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] waved at the eye in the Basement. It winked back.

## Notes

- The eye is [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant's]] -- not the [[Queen_of_Darkness|Queen of Darkness's]] and not the [[Tharizdun_Cult|cult's]] (so far as known)
- [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant's]] motives remain unclear: he killed cultists, sheltered the party, and may be an ally -- but he is also clearly a Feywild operator with his own agenda and he is watching [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] specifically, not the whole party
- Conversations within the eye's line of sight should be considered potentially compromised
- The eye may be useful: if [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant]] is paying attention, a distress signal toward the sky could reach him
- Not yet tested whether the eye persists when [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] leaves the Feywild
- Pairs uncomfortably with [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] other Feywild marks: the Unseelie Black Rose that can't be hidden, plus a giant eye in the sky above him -- he is now the most surveilled character in the party
