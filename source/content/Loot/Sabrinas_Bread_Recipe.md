# Sabrina's Bread Recipe

*Wondrous item (recipe), rare*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo Swiftclock]]
**Acquired:** Session 26 — given by [[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina Nightgale]] in the Void
**Rarity:** Rare

## Description
A handwritten note containing the recipe and ingredient list for Sabrina's anti-corruption bread. The bread itself is very thin and distinctly bitter in taste. When consumed, it immediately stops the burning and stinging of Void corruption, though it does not reverse the physical signs (blackened skin/fur).

## Properties
- **Contains Corruption:** The bread halts the painful effects of Void corruption but does not cure it. The physical corruption (blackened skin, fur, etc.) remains.
- **Requires Special Ingredients:** Needs specific ingredients sourced from Waterdeep.
- **Requires Forest Elf Magic:** Preparation requires innate forest elf racial magic — not just any Feywild connection. [[Moguna]] (leader of the [[Daggers_of_the_North|Daggers of the North]]) is a forest elf paladin who may be able to help.
- **Temporary Effect:** The bread's relief wore off when [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] left the Void, suggesting it may need regular consumption.

## History
[[Sabrina_Nightgale|Sabrina]] baked this bread during her time trapped in the Void. When [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] corruption spread to cover his entire hand after the Elemental Eye opened, Sabrina gave him a piece of the bread to ease his pain. She then gave [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] the recipe so the party could make more on the material plane.

## Notes
- The party needs to find a forest elf willing and able to assist with the magical preparation
- [[Morgaine]] has run out of ingredients for the mushroom cream (the other anti-corruption remedy), making this recipe especially important
- [[Clover]] was sent to gather mushroom ingredients in Waterdeep as a separate resupply effort
- This is a management tool for corruption, not a cure — the party still needs a permanent solution
