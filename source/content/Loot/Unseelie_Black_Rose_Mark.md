# Unseelie Black Rose Mark

*Court sigil, Winter Court / Unseelie (Daughter's line)*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]] (imprinted on armor)
**Acquired:** Session 30
**Source:** The Sphinx Guardian at the oak portal corridor into the [[Feywild]]
**Status:** Active, not removable, cannot be hidden

## Description

A black rose burned itself into [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] armor the moment he chose winter at the Sphinx Guardian's gate. The mark is starkly visible -- a black rose silhouette -- and reads to any fae on sight as loyalty to the **Winter Court / Unseelie**, and specifically to the **Daughter's line** (i.e., [[Cora]]'s line -- the line of the [[Queen_of_Darkness|Queen of Darkness]]).

This is, in hindsight, a catastrophically bad mark to be wearing. [[Cora]], the party's primary antagonist, is the daughter of the ruler of the Unseelie Court. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] is now -- visually, to every fae -- her ally.

## Properties

### Court Loyalty Reading
- Any fae who sees the mark reads the bearer as **loyal to the Winter Court / Unseelie**
- Specifically marks the bearer as aligned with the **Daughter's line** ([[Cora]]'s line)
- The mark is visible to all fae on sight

### Cannot Be Hidden
- **Bleeds through any cloth laid over it.** [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] attempted to drape his cape over the armor as a toga; the rose's outline showed through the fabric, intact.
- Cannot be covered, concealed, painted over, or obscured by any means tried so far
- The mark displays itself whether the armor is worn or removed

### Social Consequences
- Hostility or suspicion from Seelie-aligned fae
- Presumed ally status from Unseelie-aligned fae -- including, dangerously, [[Cora]]'s mother
- Splits [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] from [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] and [[Clover]], who both bear the Seelie mark

## How It Was Acquired

Upon stepping through the oak portal tree at the end of Session 29's cliffhanger, the party was processed one by one by a massive Sphinx Guardian. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] came up second, visibly pulling his head back when the Sphinx's neck stretched down toward him.

When asked how he felt, he replied: *"Angry, because the backflipping bitch was here a few moments ago, and confused because a cat with a very, very long neck is asking me questions. And my friend just disappeared."*

On the season question he hedged, then answered **winter** -- because Waterdeep summers *"stink like piss,"* and *"in the winter it's nice, it snows."*

A black rose burned itself into his armor. The DM privately told the table he had just chosen the **Unseelie Court** mark. [[Clover]] sighed: *"You chose the Unseelie. Well -- what's done is done."*

The full implications landed later, when [[Mr_Plant|Mr. Plant]] revealed [[Cora]] is the **daughter of the Queen of the Unseelie**. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] -- who had just accidentally chosen her mother's court -- received the news with understandable distress.

## Notes

- The mark is a court binding, not a cosmetic flourish -- it mechanically reads to fae on sight
- [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] attempt to cover it with his cape failed: the rose bled through the cloth, outline intact
- This mark pairs badly with [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] ongoing corruption storyline (blackened fingers) -- he is increasingly marked by dark powers
- Any Seelie-aligned entity, settlement, or court will read him as an enemy on sight
- Conversely, this may grant unexpected access or safe conduct in Unseelie territory -- though at the cost of being *seen* there, by the Queen who already knows the party refused her offer
