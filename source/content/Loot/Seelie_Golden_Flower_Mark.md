# Seelie Golden Flower Mark

*Court sigil, Summer Court / Seelie*

## Quick Reference
**Holders:** [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo Swiftclock]] (imprinted on chest), [[Clover]] (imprinted on armor)
**Acquired:** Session 30
**Source:** The Sphinx Guardian at the oak portal corridor into the [[Feywild]]
**Status:** Active, not removable

## Description

A warm, golden flower bloomed into existence on [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo's]] chest and on [[Clover|Clover's]] armor the moment each of them chose summer at the Sphinx Guardian's gate. The mark is visibly golden and radiates a warm, pleasant quality to fae who see it.

The Sphinx processes every traveler through the oak portal with the question *"Do you like summer or winter the most?"* Choosing summer binds the traveler with the Seelie (Summer Court) mark. The answer is a court binding, not a preference -- neither Milo nor Clover understood this at the time of choice, though Clover, Feywild-born, recognized what was happening after the fact.

## Properties

### Court Loyalty Reading
- Any fae who sees the mark immediately reads the bearer as **loyal to the Summer Court / Seelie**
- The mark is visible to all fae on sight

### Not Removable
- The mark is permanently bound to the bearer (body for [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]], armor for [[Clover]])
- Cannot be covered, hidden, or concealed from fae

### Social Consequences
- Friendly reception from Seelie-aligned fae
- Potentially hostile reception from Unseelie-aligned fae (including, notably, [[Cora]] and her mother, the [[Queen_of_Darkness|Queen of Darkness]])
- Puts Milo and Clover on a different footing from [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]], who bears the opposite mark

## How It Was Acquired

Upon stepping through the oak portal tree at the end of Session 29's cliffhanger, the party found themselves queued in a stone corridor behind a line of [[Tharizdun_Cult|Tharizdun cultists]] being processed by a massive Sphinx Guardian. The Sphinx asked each traveler about their emotional state and then posed the seasonal question.

- **[[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]]** chose summer: *"winter is cozy if you have a home, but you do more in summer."* A warm golden flower bloomed on his chest.
- **[[Clover]]** chose summer without hesitation, chatting comfortably with the Sphinx as one of her own. A golden flower bloomed on his armor.

The Sphinx confided privately to [[Clover]] that his two friends had been *"a bit foolish,"* and that emotions are the currency of the Feywild -- his friends' emotions were *"delicious."*

## Notes

- [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] chose winter and received the opposing [[Unseelie_Black_Rose_Mark|Unseelie Black Rose Mark]] -- splitting the party across the two courts
- Seasonal choice is a court binding: Summer = Seelie, Winter = Unseelie
- The mark is a mechanical reality in the Feywild, not a cosmetic flourish -- fae read it on sight and respond to it
- Milo and Clover now have a natural entry point into any Seelie settlement or court, at the cost of Unseelie hostility
