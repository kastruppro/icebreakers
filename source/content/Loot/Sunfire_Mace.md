# Sunfire Mace

*Weapon (mace), rare (requires attunement)*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]]
**Acquired:** Session 23
**Rarity:** Rare
**Attunement:** Required

## Description
A holy mace recovered from the Castellers family mausoleum beneath House Castellers in Waterdeep. The weapon was found clutched in the hands of a deceased family member, interred in a sarcophagus within the iron-gated crypt.

## Properties

### Base Damage
- **1d6 bludgeoning damage**

### Radiant Smite
- **Extra 2d6 radiant damage** against fiends, undead, or elementals

### Instant Destruction
- Creatures with **25 HP or less** that are hit must make a **Wisdom saving throw** or be **instantly destroyed**
- On a successful save, the creature is **frightened** instead

### Holy Light
- Sheds **bright light in a 20-foot radius**
- Sheds **dim light for an additional 20 feet**

## Location Found
The Castellers Mausoleum - a gated crypt bearing the inscription "Here lies the remains of House Castellers" located in the basement of House Castellers. [[Ammalia_Castellers|Ammalia Castellers]] mentioned there was a powerful mace inside, leading the party to investigate.

## Acquisition
After [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar Starshade]] failed to pick the lock on the mausoleum gate (rolled 11), [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo Swiftclock]] succeeded (rolled 22 with help). Inside, the party found family tombs and one sarcophagus containing a body clutching this mace. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] claimed it for fighting fiends.

## Notes
- Particularly effective against Tharizdun cultists and the undead/fiendish creatures associated with them
- The instant-kill ability makes it devastating against weaker minions
- The radiant light properties could be useful in dark dungeons like The Depths
- A fitting weapon for a holy knight fighting against forces of darkness
- **Session 24:** Modred drew the Sunfire Mace for the confrontation against possessed [[Lord_Neverember|Lord Neverember]] in [[The_Depths|The Depths]], declaring "Prepare to face my wrath!" - its radiant damage and anti-undead/elemental properties make it ideal against necromancy-based possession and the accompanying Water Elemental
- **Session 25:** The Sunfire Mace proved decisive in killing [[Old_Hydra|Old Hydra]], the massive Water Elemental guarding the boss arena. Its extra 2d6 radiant damage against elementals allowed [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] to deal devastating hits throughout the fight. In the final round, Modred struck Old Hydra twice with the mace -- its blinding light cut through the elemental, evaporating it completely. However, the acid retort damage from the killing blow knocked Modred unconscious at the same moment, making it a heroic sacrifice. The Sunfire Mace also channeled stored lightning energy from Absorb Elements earlier in the fight, combining radiant and thunder damage for massive burst rounds
