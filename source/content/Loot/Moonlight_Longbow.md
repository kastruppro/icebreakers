# Moonlight Longbow

*Weapon (longbow), legendary*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar Starshade]]
**Acquired:** Session 28
**Rarity:** Legendary
**Source:** Divine gift from [[Corellon_Larethian|Corellon Larethian]]

## Description

A longbow of impossible beauty, formed from liquid moonlight that poured down through the sky during the Lateu'quor -- the monthly Forest Communion of the Crescent Moon -- at [[New_Sharandar|New Sharandar]]'s [[Temple_of_Leaves|Temple of Leaves]]. The bow was shaped by [[Corellon_Larethian|Corellon Larethian]] himself as a direct gift to the party, his face forming in the full moon above the golden tree before the moonlight descended and solidified into the weapon's form.

## Properties

### Attack Bonus
- **+2 to attack and damage rolls**

### Moonlit Arrows
- All arrows fired from this bow are lit in moonlight
- Moonlit arrows **reveal shapechangers and illusions** on hit

### Corellon's Shot (1/Day)
- Once per day, an arrow can be fired at a target up to **5,000 feet away**
- The shot **cannot miss**, provided the target is visible to the archer
- On hit, deals **1d8 + 2d10 damage**

## How It Was Acquired

During the Lateu'quor ceremony, the party laid offerings at the base of the golden tree alongside the eladrin of New Sharandar. The tree's leaves wrapped around them and carried them upward into a sky-vision where [[Corellon_Larethian|Corellon Larethian]] spoke to them directly. He thanked them for their gifts and for killing one of [[Tharizdun|Tharizdun's]] Princes, then warned that the cult besieging Neverwinter Wood appeared to be entering from the Feywild itself.

As his final act before the vision collapsed, Corellon caused the moon itself to melt and flow downward, solidifying into the longbow. It floated down to the party as he said: *"Once again, I thank you for what you are doing. I know it must be difficult. You're not the first of your kind trying to do this. I hope you will be the last."*

The DM handed the bow to [[Jaspar_Starshade|Jaspar]] at the end of the session, just as the temple erupted in flames and [[Cora|Cora]] fled the camp with [[Liora_Swiftclock|Liora]].

## Notes

- The moonlit-arrow property is particularly relevant given the party's ongoing encounters with shapechangers, illusions, and Tharizdun-linked corruption
- The daily long-range shot could be decisive against targets who use distance or portals to escape -- including [[Cora|Cora]]
- [[Emrae_Firesky|Emrae Firesky]], the Corellon priestess, had a vision days before the party arrived that described "chains, darkness, and a bow creating light" -- this bow was what she foresaw
