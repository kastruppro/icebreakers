# De Moray Sending Stone

*Wondrous item, uncommon*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]]
**Acquired:** Session 26 — given by [[Sir_Goodwin_De_Moray|Sir Goodwin De Moray]] at House De Moray (Timori) in Waterdeep
**Rarity:** Uncommon
**Attunement:** Not required

## Properties

Sending stones come in pairs, with each smooth stone carved to match the other so the pairing is easily recognized.

**Cast Sending.** While you touch one stone, you can use an action to cast the *Sending* spell from it. The target is the bearer of the other stone. If no creature bears the other stone, you know that fact as soon as you use the stone and don't cast the spell.

**Limitation:** Once *Sending* is cast through the stones, they can't be used again until the next dawn.

**Destruction:** If one of the stones in a pair is destroyed, the other one becomes nonmagical.

## Current Pairing

Stone A: [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]]
Stone B: [[Sir_Goodwin_De_Moray|Sir Goodwin De Moray]] (at House De Moray, Waterdeep)

## History
[[Sir_Goodwin_De_Moray|Sir Goodwin]] gave this sending stone to his son [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] during their reunion at House De Moray in Waterdeep. Goodwin had undergone a dramatic personal transformation — clean-shaven, sober, and determined to reunite the De Moray family. The stone allows father and son to stay in contact as the party travels to Neverwinter.
