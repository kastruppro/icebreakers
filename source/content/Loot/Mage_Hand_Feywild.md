# Mage Hand (Feywild)

*Cantrip ability, conditional (Feywild only)*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Sir Modred De Moray]]
**Acquired:** Session 30
**Source:** Gift from [[Doggo]] at the Meadow Tree
**Status:** Active -- usable once per short rest while in the [[Feywild]]

## Description

A bespoke, Feywild-flavored version of the Mage Hand cantrip, gifted to [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] by the talking golden retriever the party named [[Doggo]]. The gift is a parting thank-you for [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] "lending a hand" to help retrieve a bone from a tree -- in a moment of Feywild literalism, [[Doggo]] had actually taken [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] entire hand off his wrist and spat it back on the ground, flower-crowned at the wound. After the bone was retrieved (by [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo's]] own Mage Hand), [[Doggo]] granted [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] this ability.

The somatic component is unique: **[[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] physically detaches his own hand from his wrist**. The detached hand floats and manipulates objects at will, functioning as a standard Mage Hand. When the cantrip ends, the hand returns and reattaches.

## Properties

### Casting
- **Once per short rest** while in the Feywild
- **Not usable outside the Feywild** -- the ability is tied to the plane

### Somatic Component
- [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] detaches his physical hand from his wrist
- The hand floats and manipulates objects as a standard Mage Hand
- No permanent damage to [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] arm -- the hand reattaches cleanly when the spell ends
- [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] immediately tested the ability at the Meadow Tree as a parlor trick

### Mechanical Effect
- Functions as standard **Mage Hand** cantrip (5th-level range, 10 lbs. object manipulation, cannot attack or deal damage, etc.)
- Duration: 1 minute (standard)
- No verbal component required (the detachment is the full somatic gesture)

## How It Was Acquired

In the sunlit meadow on the Feywild side of the oak portal, the party encountered a large tree with a high hollow. Below it, a talking golden retriever was barking at a rabbit who had stolen its bone. The dog asked [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] to *"lend me a hand, please."* [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] offered his hand. It came off at the wrist in the dog's mouth before he knew what had happened -- Feywild literalism in action. The dog spat it back out, covered in flowers at the wound. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] reattached it. No permanent damage; mild emotional damage.

[[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo]] ultimately solved the bone problem with his *own* Mage Hand -- the first time in 29 sessions of play he had ever cast it (the table cheered). The rabbit was spooked out of the tree, saying *"What the fuck is happening?"* in a deep voice, and vanished. The bone dropped.

[[Doggo]] -- grateful, and amused by the "lend me a hand" bit -- granted [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] the Mage Hand cantrip as a Feywild gift, themed around the detachment joke. [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] immediately tested it by detaching his hand again, this time on purpose.

## Notes

- The detachment is a *visible, mechanical* part of [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] hand now -- not a cosmetic effect
- The gift is explicitly tied to the Feywild -- when [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred]] returns to the Material Plane, the ability presumably goes dormant (not yet tested)
- Pairs well with [[Sir_Modred_De_Moray|Modred's]] blackened-fingers corruption storyline aesthetically (the hand already looks like it wants to come off)
- Not yet tested whether this counts against a spellcaster's cantrip list or is purely innate
- The rabbit's voice and vanishing act suggest standard Feywild shenanigans; no plot thread opened
