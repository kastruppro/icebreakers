# The Princes of Tharizdun

*Wondrous item (book), uncommon*

## Quick Reference
**Holder:** [[The_Icebreakers|The Icebreakers]] (party possession)
**Acquired:** Session 23
**Rarity:** Uncommon
**Type:** Lore Book / Plot Item

## Description
A black-covered book featuring four menacing humanoid figures on its cover. The book contains information about [[Tharizdun]], the Betrayer God, and his "Princes" - powerful servants or aspects of the dark deity.

## Contents
The book contains chapters detailing:
- Information about [[Tharizdun]] and his worship
- Details about Tharizdun's "Princes" - including one called **"Bull Hydra"**
- Other strange names and entities associated with the cult
- Confirms earlier mentions of **"four princes"** and **four hideouts**

## Location Found
Inside the mysterious shrine chest that [[Milo_Swiftclock|Milo Swiftclock]] recovered from House Castellers' hidden wall during Session 21. The chest was opened at the start of Session 23.

## Shrine Chest Contents
The chest contained:
- 4 Healing Potions (distributed one to each party member)
- [[Ring_of_Cold_Resistance|Ring of Cold Resistance]] (glowing blue ring)
- A vial of dark purple/red corruption liquid (released by Modred into the ritual pool)
- **This book** - The Princes of Tharizdun

## Significance
This book is a major plot item that provides crucial information about the enemy the party faces:
- Confirms the existence of four "Princes" serving Tharizdun
- Provides the name "Bull Hydra" as one of these princes
- Suggests there are four hideouts, one for each prince
- May contain additional lore useful for understanding and defeating the cult

## Notes
- Should be studied thoroughly for intelligence on the enemy
- The "four princes" have been mentioned before - this book may reveal their identities
- Victor Castellers had made a deal with "the four princes" - this book may explain what that entailed
- The party has already encountered cult hideouts - this book may help locate the remaining ones
